## Instructions

### Make the Cake

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 350°F. |
| 2 | Mix flour, sugar, cinnamon, cooking oil, finely grated carrots, baking soda, salt, and eggs until combined. |
| 3 | Divide batter into 3 layers. |
| 4 | Bake at 350°F for 30 minutes. |
| 5 | Cool cake layers completely before icing. |

### Make the Icing

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Combine cream cheese, powdered sugar, coconut, margarine, vanilla, and chopped pecans. |
| 2 | Mix until smooth. |
| 3 | Frost cooled carrot cake layers. |

## Notes

This handwritten recipe belongs to Sue Wallesch.

The handwritten card specifies baking the carrot cake in 3 layers at 350°F for 30 minutes.

The icing is preserved with the cake because it was written on the same card as Carrot Cake Icing.

Prep time, total time, servings, and cooling time are estimated. Box/can sizes for powdered sugar and coconut were not specified on the handwritten card.
