Timatim Salad

A fresh tomato-cucumber salad with lemon and onion that brings acidity, brightness, and cooling contrast to the Doro Wat platter.

Prep15m
Cook0m
Total15m + 10m rest
Serves2-4 side servings
TomatoCucumberFresh acid

Ingredient List

Salad Base

Tomato, cucumber, and onion.

Dressing

Lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and black pepper.

Optional Heat & Herbs

Jalapeno or serrano, parsley or cilantro.

Measured Ingredients

Timatim Salad

1TomatoDiced.
1/2CucumberDiced.
2-3 TbspOnionFinely sliced.
1 TbspLemon juice
1 TbspOlive oil
To tasteSalt and black pepper
OptionalJalapeno or serranoMinced.
OptionalParsley or cilantroChopped.

Instructions

  1. Dice tomato and cucumber. Finely slice onion.
  2. Toss tomato, cucumber, onion, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and black pepper together.
  3. Add optional minced chile and herbs if using.
  4. Let sit 10-15 minutes before serving so the flavors settle.

Chef's Notes

This salad is the freshness and acid balance for the platter. The Doro Wat-style chicken is rich, spicy, and onion-heavy, so the salad should stay bright and simple.
