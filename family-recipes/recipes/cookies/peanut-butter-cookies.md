# Peanut Butter Cookies

> A preserved family peanut butter cookie recipe made with creamy peanut butter, butter flavor Crisco, brown sugar, and the classic fork crisscross pattern.

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 375°F. |
| 2 | Combine peanut butter, Crisco, brown sugar, milk, and vanilla in a large bowl. |
| 3 | Beat at medium speed until well blended. |
| 4 | Add egg and beat just until blended. |
| 5 | Combine flour, salt, and baking soda. |
| 6 | Add dry ingredients to creamed mixture and mix just until all ingredients are blended well. |
| 7 | Drop by heaping teaspoonfuls, 2 inches apart, onto ungreased cookie sheets. |
| 8 | Flatten slightly in a crisscross pattern with the tines of a fork. |
| 9 | Bake for 7-8 minutes, or until set and just beginning to brown. |
| 10 | Cool 2 minutes on cookie sheet. |
| 11 | Remove to a sheet of aluminum foil to cool completely before storing in a cookie canister. |
