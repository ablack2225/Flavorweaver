# Lemon Ice Box Pie

> A preserved family lemon ice box pie made with softened cream cheese, sweetened condensed milk, lemon juice, vanilla, and a graham cracker crust, chilled until set and optionally topped with cherry pie filling.

## Measured Ingredients

### Lemon Ice Box Pie

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 | 8-inch graham cracker pie crust | Prepared |
| 1 can | Sweetened condensed milk |  |
| 1/3 cup | Lemon juice |  |
| 1 (8 oz) package | Cream cheese | Softened |
| 1 tsp | Vanilla |  |
| 1 can | Cherry pie filling | Optional; chill before topping |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Beat softened cream cheese until light and fluffy. |
| 2 | Beat in sweetened condensed milk. |
| 3 | Mix in lemon juice and vanilla. |
| 4 | Pour filling into the prepared graham cracker crust. |
| 5 | Cover and chill for 3-4 hours. |
| 6 | Before serving, top pie with chilled cherry pie filling, if desired. |

## Notes

This lemon ice box pie is remembered as another family favorite.

Jimmie and Sue were both known in the family for their pies. This lemon ice box pie is preserved as part of that family pie tradition.
