# Creamy Mashed Potato Bake

> A preserved family side dish made with hot mashed potatoes, sour cream, cheddar cheese, garlic powder, and French's French Fried Onions baked in layers until golden.

## Measured Ingredients

### Creamy Mashed Potato Bake

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 1/3 cups | French's French Fried Onions | Divided |
| 3 cups | Hot mashed potatoes | Instant mashed potatoes for 6 servings may be used |
| 1 cup | Sour cream |  |
| 1/4 cup | Milk |  |
| 1/4 tsp | Garlic powder |  |
| 1 cup | Cheddar cheese | Grated, divided |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 350°F. |
| 2 | In a large bowl, combine mashed potatoes, sour cream, milk, and garlic powder; mix well. |
| 3 | Spoon half of the potato mixture into a greased 2-quart baking dish. |
| 4 | Sprinkle with 2/3 cup French Fried Onions and 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese. |
| 5 | Top with remaining potato mixture. |
| 6 | Bake for 30 minutes. |
| 7 | Top with remaining onions and remaining cheese. |
| 8 | Bake 5 minutes longer, until onions are golden. |
