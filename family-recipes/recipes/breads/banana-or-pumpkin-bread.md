# Banana or Pumpkin Bread

> A preserved family quick bread recipe that can be made as banana bread or adapted into pumpkin bread, with optional nuts and dates.

## Measured Ingredients

### Banana Bread Base

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1/2 cup | Margarine | Softened |
| 1 cup | Sugar | May use part brown sugar |
| 2 | Eggs |  |
| 2 medium | Bananas | Mashed; about 1 cup |
| 1/4 cup | Milk |  |
| 1 tsp | Vanilla | Handwritten addition on original page |
| 2 cups | Flour |  |
| 1 tsp | Baking soda |  |
| 1/2 tsp | Salt |  |
| 1/2 cup | Chopped nuts |  |
| Optional | Chopped dates | See notes |

### Pumpkin Bread Variation

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| As needed | Canned pumpkin | Use in place of banana |
| 1 3/4 cups | Flour | Decrease flour from banana version |
| 1/2 tsp | Cinnamon | Add for pumpkin version |
| 1/2 tsp | Nutmeg | Add for pumpkin version |
| 1/4 tsp | Ginger | Add for pumpkin version |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 350°F. |
| 2 | Cream margarine and sugar together. |
| 3 | Beat in eggs. |
| 4 | Stir in banana, milk, and vanilla. |
| 5 | Mix the dry ingredients together, then stir them in by hand until well combined. |
| 6 | Pour batter into a large loaf pan greased only on the bottom, or divide into 2 small loaf pans. |
| 7 | Bake 50-60 minutes or until done. Smaller loaves will bake a little faster. |
| 8 | Remove from pan and cool completely before wrapping for storage. |
| 9 | For pumpkin bread, use canned pumpkin in place of banana, decrease flour to 1 3/4 cups, add cinnamon, nutmeg, and ginger, then proceed as directed. |

## Notes

Original family note: chopped dates are optional, but at our house they are an absolute must in this recipe. The chewy texture of the dates is loved, and they add moisture too.

The original page lists prep time as 1 hour 15 minutes, but the method shows 50-60 minutes of baking. This file separates the timing into estimated prep time and documented bake time so the total is easier to understand.
