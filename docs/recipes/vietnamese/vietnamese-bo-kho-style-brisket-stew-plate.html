Vietnamese Bò Kho-Style Brisket Stew Plate

A complete Vietnamese-inspired bread-dipping plate: aromatic Bò Kho-style brisket stew, fresh bánh mì không-style dipping rolls, sesame-free pickled garden vegetables finished with lime, cilantro, and optional serrano, plus lime, herbs, serrano, black pepper, and sriracha at the table.

Kitchen Execution Summary

Complete Plate

This card covers the full dish: stew, dipping rolls, pickled vegetables, and garnish/table finish.

Bò Kho Stew

Tomato-rich brisket with lemongrass, ginger, garlic, star anise, fish sauce, and carrots.

Bánh Mì Không Rolls

Fresh crackly rolls baked close to service for dipping into the broth.

Pickles + Finish

Asian Pickled Garden Vegetables in Vietnamese / Bò Kho mode: lime, cilantro, optional serrano, no sesame.

Before Cooking Day / Advance Prep

Marinate Brisket — 2 hours to overnight.

Make Pickle Base — 2 hours to overnight.

Confirm Bread Setup — day before or morning of.

Full Plate Ingredient Roll-Up

Stew Core

2 to 2 1/2 lb brisket; fish sauce; soy sauce; tomato paste; ginger; garlic; lemongrass; five-spice; paprika; onion; bay; star anise; beef broth; hoisin; carrots.

Bread Component

2 cups all-purpose flour; 1 cup bread flour; 2 tsp active dry yeast; 1 tsp sugar; 1 cup water at 105°F–108°F plus up to 1/4 cup more; 1 1/2 tsp fine salt; 1 Tbsp neutral oil; 1 tsp lime juice; 1 cup hot water for steam.

Pickle Component

1 zucchini; 1 yellow squash; 1 carrot; 2 green onions; 1/4 cup rice vinegar; 2 Tbsp sugar; 1 tsp kosher salt; 1 Tbsp water; optional red pepper flakes. Finish with lime, cilantro, optional serrano.

Fresh Service

Lime wedges, cilantro, sliced green onion, thin-sliced serrano, black pepper, and sriracha stay close to service.

Measured Ingredients by Component

These collapsible sections are reference only. The cooking flow is handled by the stage-based prep bowls and full timeline below.

Vietnamese Bò Kho-Style Brisket Stew Ingredients

2 to 2 1/2 lb brisket, 2 1/2 Tbsp fish sauce plus more, 1 Tbsp soy sauce, 2 Tbsp neutral oil divided, 1 Tbsp brown sugar plus optional 1–2 tsp, 3 Tbsp tomato paste divided, 2 Tbsp ginger divided, 5–6 cloves garlic divided, 2 Tbsp lemongrass paste divided, 1–1 1/2 tsp five-spice divided, 1–1 1/2 tsp paprika divided, 1 medium onion, 2 bay leaves, 1 star anise pod, 4 cups beef broth, 1–2 cups water, 1 Tbsp hoisin, optional sriracha, 3 large carrots.

Bánh Mì Không-Style Dipping Rolls Ingredients

2 cups all-purpose flour, 1 cup bread flour, 2 tsp active dry yeast, 1 tsp sugar, 1 cup water at 105°F–108°F, up to 1/4 cup extra warm water, 1 1/2 tsp fine salt, 1 Tbsp neutral oil, 1 tsp fresh lime juice, and 1 cup hot water for oven steam.

Asian Pickled Garden Vegetables — Bò Kho Mode Ingredients

1 zucchini, 1 yellow squash, 1 carrot, 2 green onions, 1/4 cup rice vinegar, 2 Tbsp sugar, 1 tsp kosher salt, 1 Tbsp water, optional 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes. Finish with lime juice, cilantro, and optional thin-sliced serrano. Do not use sesame oil or sesame seeds.

Cross-Component Prep Map

Garlic: mince 5–6 cloves total, then divide 3–4 cloves for marinade and 2 cloves for Stew Aromatic Bowl 2.

Ginger: measure 2 Tbsp total, then divide 1 Tbsp for marinade and 1 Tbsp for Stew Aromatic Bowl 2.

Lemongrass: measure 2 Tbsp total, then divide 1 Tbsp for marinade and 1 Tbsp for Stew Aromatic Bowl 2.

Lime, cilantro, green onion, serrano: keep fresh finish and garnish portions close to service.

Stage-Based Prep Bowls

Bread Bowls

Bowl 1 — Yeast Bloom

1 cup water at 105°F–108°F; 1 tsp sugar; 2 tsp active dry yeast.

Bowl 2 — Dry Ingredients

2 cups all-purpose flour; 1 cup bread flour; 1 1/2 tsp fine salt.

Bowl 3 — Dough Additions

1 Tbsp neutral oil; 1 tsp lime juice; up to 1/4 cup extra warm water as needed.

Stew Bowls

Bowl 1 — Marinated Brisket

2 to 2 1/2 lb marinated brisket chunks.

Bowl 2 — Onion

1 medium onion, sliced or diced. Cook alone 5–7 minutes.

Bowl 3 — Garlic, Ginger & Lemongrass

2 cloves garlic, minced; 1 Tbsp ginger; 1 Tbsp lemongrass paste.

Bowl 4 — Tomato Paste & Spices

2 Tbsp tomato paste; 1/2 tsp paprika; 1/2 tsp five-spice if using; 2 bay leaves; 1 star anise pod.

Bowl 5 — Braise Liquids

4 cups beef broth; reserved marinade; 1 Tbsp fish sauce; 1 Tbsp hoisin; optional sugar; optional sriracha; water as needed.

Bowl 6 — Carrots

3 large carrots, thick diagonal pieces.

Pickle & Garnish Bowls

Bowl 1 — Pickle Base

Zucchini, yellow squash, carrot, green onions, rice vinegar, sugar, salt, water, optional red pepper flakes.

Bowl 2 — Bò Kho Finish

Drained pickles, lime juice, cilantro, optional serrano. No sesame.

Bowl 3 — Table Finish

Lime wedges, cilantro, sliced green onion, thin-sliced serrano, black pepper, sriracha.

Full Cooking Timeline and Instructions

  1. Marinate the brisket 2 hours to overnight.
  2. Make the pickle base 2 hours to overnight ahead.
  3. Start stew about 4 hours before serving; sear brisket.
  4. Cook onion 5–7 minutes, then add garlic, ginger, and lemongrass for 30–60 seconds.
  5. Toast tomato paste and spices 1–2 minutes, then add braise liquids and simmer 2 1/2–3 1/2 hours.
  6. Start bread about 2 hours 45 minutes before serving; bloom yeast, mix dough, rise, shape, and bake close to service.
  7. Add carrots during final 35–45 minutes of stew cooking.
  8. Finish pickles 15 minutes before service with lime, cilantro, and optional serrano.
  9. Finish stew with fish sauce/salt, lime or rice vinegar, sugar, sriracha, and black pepper as needed. Rest 10 minutes.
  10. Serve hot stew with fresh rolls, pickles, lime, herbs, serrano, black pepper, and sriracha.

Chef's Notes

This is the bread-dipping Bò Kho plate, not a noodle bowl. The complete plate is stew + fresh rolls + pickled vegetables + fresh garnish/table finish.

For the pickled vegetables, skip sesame oil and sesame seeds for this plate.
