# Beef and Potatoes

> A preserved family foil-packet dinner made with sirloin steak strips, red potatoes, bell pepper, cream of mushroom soup, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, and black pepper.

## Measured Ingredients

### Beef and Potatoes

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 lb | Boneless sirloin steak | 1/2-inch thick |
| 1/2 tsp | Garlic powder |  |
| 1/2 tsp | Black pepper |  |
| 4 medium | Red potatoes | Cubed |
| 1 small | Bell pepper | Cut in strips |
| 1 can | Cream of mushroom soup | Undiluted |
| 1 1/2 Tbsp | Worcestershire sauce |  |
| 4 large sheets | Aluminum foil | For packets |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 450°F. |
| 2 | Cut steak into long, slender strips, about 1/8 inch each. |
| 3 | Sprinkle beef strips with garlic powder and black pepper. |
| 4 | Combine beef with potatoes, bell pepper, cream of mushroom soup, and Worcestershire sauce. |
| 5 | Stir to combine all ingredients well. |
| 6 | Take 4 large sheets of aluminum foil. Center 1/4 of the prepared meat mixture onto each piece of foil. |
| 7 | Bring up foil sides. Double fold the top and ends to seal each packet, leaving room for heat circulation inside. |
| 8 | Place foil packets on a cookie sheet. |
| 9 | Bake 25-30 minutes in the preheated 450°F oven. |
