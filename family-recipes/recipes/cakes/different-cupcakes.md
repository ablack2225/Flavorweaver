# Different Cupcakes

> A preserved family chocolate cupcake recipe with semi-sweet chocolate chips and chopped nuts, described as brownies in cupcake form.

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 350°F. |
| 2 | Melt chocolate chips and margarine together. Stir until smooth. |
| 3 | Add chopped nuts to the chocolate mixture and stir. |
| 4 | In a separate bowl, combine sugar, flour, eggs, vanilla, and butter flavoring. |
| 5 | Mix by hand just until blended. |
| 6 | Add the chocolate mixture and stir to blend. Do not beat. |
| 7 | Pour into cupcake forms. |
| 8 | Bake for 35 minutes. |
| 9 | Do not frost. Serve as they are. |
