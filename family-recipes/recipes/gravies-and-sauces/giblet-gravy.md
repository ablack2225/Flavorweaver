# Giblet Gravy

> A preserved family gravy made with chicken broth, chopped chicken, boiled eggs, margarine, flour, and black pepper, traditionally served over cornbread dressing.

## Measured Ingredients

### Giblet Gravy

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1/2 cup | Cooked chopped chicken |  |
| 3 Tbsp | Margarine |  |
| To taste | Black pepper |  |
| 2 cups | Chicken broth |  |
| 2 | Boiled eggs | Grated fine |
| 2 Tbsp | Flour |  |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | In a medium saucepan, melt margarine. |
| 2 | Gradually stir in flour. |
| 3 | Cook and stir for about 1 minute. |
| 4 | Gradually blend in chicken broth, stirring constantly so the mixture does not get lumpy. |
| 5 | Cook and stir until gravy begins to thicken. |
| 6 | Stir in black pepper, grated boiled eggs, and chopped chicken. |
| 7 | Serve over cornbread dressing. |
