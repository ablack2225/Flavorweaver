Asian Pickled Garden Vegetables

Quick refrigerator pickles made with thinly sliced garden vegetables, a simple rice vinegar brine, and optional sesame oil and sesame seeds for a restaurant-style finish.

Prep15 min
Cook0 min
Chill2 hr to overnight
YieldAbout 1 quart
quick picklesgarden vegetablesrice vinegarfreshcrunchy

Ingredients

  • 1 zucchini, thinly sliced
  • 1 yellow squash, thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, peeled into ribbons or julienned
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 2 Tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 Tbsp water
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes, optional

Method

  1. Place vegetables into a jar or container.
  2. Whisk together rice vinegar, sugar, salt, and water.
  3. Pour brine over vegetables and stir or shake well.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 2 to 4 hours, or overnight for best flavor.
  5. Drain lightly before serving and finish with sesame oil and sesame seeds if desired.
