# Chicken Spaghetti

> A preserved family pasta casserole made with cooked chicken, spaghetti, cream soups, tomato soup, onion, celery, bell pepper, pimiento, chicken broth, and Velveeta cheese.

## Measured Ingredients

### Chicken Spaghetti

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 can | Cream of mushroom soup |  |
| 1 can | Cream of chicken soup |  |
| 1 can | Tomato soup |  |
| About 2 cups | Cooked chicken | Chopped |
| 1 lb | Spaghetti | Cooked |
| 3/4 cup each | Onion, celery, and bell pepper | Chopped |
| 1 1/4 tsp | Black pepper |  |
| 1 1/4 tsp | Salt |  |
| 1 tsp | Garlic powder |  |
| 1 small jar | Diced pimiento |  |
| 1 1/2 cups | Chicken broth or bouillon |  |
| 1/2 lb | Velveeta cheese | Cubed |
| Small amount | Margarine | For sautéing vegetables |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 350°F. |
| 2 | Sauté onion, celery, and bell pepper in a small amount of margarine until tender. |
| 3 | Cook spaghetti according to package directions; drain. |
| 4 | In a large pot, combine all ingredients together. |
| 5 | Heat thoroughly over low heat until all the cheese is melted. |
| 6 | Pour mixture into a large, greased casserole dish. |
| 7 | Bake covered at 350°F for 30 minutes. |
| 8 | Uncover and bake an additional 15 minutes. |
