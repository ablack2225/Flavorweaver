Gochujang Crema

A creamy, tangy, lightly spicy gochujang finishing sauce developed for Bold Bulgogi Bowls and now kept as a reusable Flavorweaver house condiment.

Prep5 min
Cook0 min
Total5 min
YieldAbout 1/3 cup
gochujangcremasaucehouse stapledrizzle sauce

Kitchen Execution Summary

Recipe Role

Reusable Korean-inspired house condiment and finishing sauce for bowls, especially Bold Bulgogi Bowls.

Flavor Target

Creamy, lightly spicy, tangy, and just sweet enough to round out the gochujang.

Texture Target

Thin enough to drizzle but thick enough to cling to rice, meat, and vegetables.

Key Technique

Stir until smooth first, then thin gradually with water one teaspoon at a time.

Stage-Based Prep Bowl

One Bowl — Crema Base

1/4 cup sour cream, 2 tsp gochujang, 1 to 2 tsp fresh lime juice, and pinch sugar. Stir smooth first, then thin with 1 to 3 tsp water, added 1 tsp at a time.

Do Not Forget

This is a one-bowl sauce, not a multi-bowl prep recipe.

Mix the sour cream, gochujang, lime juice, and sugar completely smooth before thinning.

Add water slowly, one teaspoon at a time, so the crema does not become too loose.

For bowls, aim for a clean drizzle that still clings to rice, meat, and vegetables.

For dipping or spreading, leave the crema slightly thicker.

Taste before adding optional adjustments; the canon version should stay creamy, tangy, lightly spicy, and balanced.

Instructions

  1. Add 1/4 cup sour cream, 2 tsp gochujang, 1 to 2 tsp fresh lime juice, and a pinch of sugar to a small bowl.
  2. Stir until completely smooth and evenly colored.
  3. Add 1 to 3 tsp water, 1 tsp at a time, until the sauce reaches drizzle consistency.
  4. Taste and adjust only as needed with more lime juice, a tiny pinch more sugar, more sour cream, or extra gochujang.
  5. Use immediately, or refrigerate until serving. Stir before drizzling.

Texture Goal

For bowls, the crema should be thin enough to drizzle but thick enough to cling to rice, meat, and vegetables.

If using as a dip or spread, leave it slightly thicker.

Flavor Balance Guide

Too Thick

Add water 1 teaspoon at a time.

Too Spicy

Add more sour cream.

Too Flat

Add a little more lime juice or rice vinegar.

Too Sharp

Add a tiny pinch more sugar or a few drops of honey.

Too Mild

Add more gochujang in 1/2 teaspoon increments.

Final Taste Target

The finished crema should be creamy, lightly spicy, tangy, and gently sweet. It should cool and round out spicy-sweet bowls without overpowering the main dish.

Chef's Notes

This crema was developed as the finishing sauce for Bold Bulgogi Bowls.

It adds creamy heat, tang, and a little sweetness without overpowering the main dish.

Thin gradually so the crema drizzles cleanly but still clings to rice, meat, and vegetables.
