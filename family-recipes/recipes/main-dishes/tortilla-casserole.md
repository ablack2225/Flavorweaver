# Tortilla Casserole

> A preserved family main-dish casserole layered with tortilla chips, seasoned ground beef, beans, corn, salsa cream cheese, cilantro, and Mexican-style shredded cheese.

## Measured Ingredients

### Tortilla Casserole

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 lb | Ground beef |  |
| 1 medium | Onion | Diced |
| 1 (1.25 oz) package | Taco seasoning |  |
| 1 (10 oz) can | Original Rotel | Undrained |
| 1 (15.5 oz) can | Corn | Drained |
| 1 (15.5 oz) can | Pinto beans | Drained |
| 1 cup | Salsa |  |
| 1 (8 oz) package | Cream cheese | Softened |
| 5 cups | Tortilla chips | Roughly chopped |
| 1/4 cup | Cilantro | Roughly chopped; divided |
| 2 cups | Mexican-style shredded cheese | Divided |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 450°F. |
| 2 | Spray an 8-inch casserole dish with cooking spray. |
| 3 | Cook ground beef and diced onion in a large skillet over medium heat, breaking meat up with a spatula and stirring frequently until meat is done. |
| 4 | Stir in taco seasoning, Rotel, corn, and pinto beans. |
| 5 | In a separate bowl, combine salsa and softened cream cheese. |
| 6 | Place 1/3 of the tortilla chips in the bottom of the casserole dish. |
| 7 | Add 1/2 of the meat mixture. |
| 8 | Drop the cream cheese and salsa mixture by spoonfuls onto the meat and gently spread to create a layer. |
| 9 | Add another 1/3 of the tortilla chips. |
| 10 | Add the remaining meat mixture and 1/2 of the shredded cheese. |
| 11 | Top with 1/2 of the cilantro, remaining tortilla chips, and remaining cheese. |
| 12 | Bake 10 minutes, or until cheese is golden-brown and casserole is bubbling. |
| 13 | Sprinkle with remaining cilantro before serving, if desired. |
