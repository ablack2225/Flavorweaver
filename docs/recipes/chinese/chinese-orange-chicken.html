Chinese Orange Chicken

Crispy double-fried chicken thighs tossed in an extra-generous glossy orange sauce with orange juice, marmalade, garlic, ginger, honey, brown sugar, soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, and fresh orange zest.

Ingredient List

Chicken

3 boneless skinless chicken thighs.

Marinade

Soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, garlic, black pepper.

Velveting and Coating

Egg white, cornstarch, flour, baking powder, salt.

Extra Orange Sauce

Premium orange juice, orange marmalade, brown sugar, honey, soy sauce, rice vinegar, garlic, ginger, orange zest, sesame oil, cornstarch slurry.

Measured Ingredients

Chicken and Marinade

3 boneless skinless chicken thighs; 3 Tbsp soy sauce; 1 Tbsp sesame oil; 1 1/2 tsp grated ginger; 4 minced garlic cloves; 1/2 tsp black pepper.

Velveting and Coating

1 egg white; 3 Tbsp cornstarch; dry coating of 1/2 cup flour, 1/2 cup cornstarch, 1/2 tsp baking powder, and 1/2 tsp salt.

Extra Orange Sauce

3/4 cup orange juice; 3 Tbsp orange marmalade; 3 Tbsp brown sugar; 1 1/2 Tbsp honey; 3 Tbsp soy sauce; 1 1/2 Tbsp rice vinegar; 3 grated garlic cloves; 1 1/2 tsp grated ginger; generous orange zest; 1 1/2 tsp sesame oil. Slurry: 1 1/2 Tbsp cornstarch plus 3 Tbsp cold water.

Do Not Forget

Use 3 boneless skinless chicken thighs. Six thighs was too much.

Don wants extra orange sauce, so this version uses an increased sauce batch even with 3 thighs.

Marinate 6 to 24 hours. Double-fry at 350°F, rest, then 375°F.

Fried Rice and Crab Rangoon are separate recipes and belong under pairings.

No nutrition panel is needed.

Full Cooking Timeline and Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken. Cut 3 thighs into bite-size pieces and marinate with soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, garlic, and black pepper for 6 to 24 hours.
  2. Velvet. Add egg white and cornstarch to the marinated chicken.
  3. Coat. Dredge in flour, cornstarch, baking powder, and salt.
  4. First fry. Fry at 350°F for 3 to 4 minutes. Rest 5 minutes.
  5. Second fry. Fry at 375°F for 1 to 2 minutes until crisp.
  6. Make sauce. Simmer orange sauce ingredients, add slurry, and cook until glossy.
  7. Toss and serve. Toss hot crispy chicken with sauce and garnish with green onion, sesame seeds, and orange zest.

Final Taste Target

Crisp, glossy, citrus-forward, lightly sweet, savory, garlicky, gingery, bright, and saucy without becoming soggy.
