# Guacamole

> A preserved family guacamole recipe made with ripe avocados, chopped onion, lemon juice, picante sauce, garlic powder, salt, black pepper, and a dash of Tabasco sauce.

## Measured Ingredients

### Guacamole

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 3-4 large | Ripe avocados | Slightly soft to the touch when squeezed |
| 1/4 cup | Onion | Finely chopped |
| Small amount | Lemon juice | Sprinkle over mashed avocado |
| 1/4-1/2 cup | Picante sauce | Adjust to taste |
| To taste | Salt and black pepper |  |
| To taste | Garlic powder |  |
| Dash | Tabasco sauce |  |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Peel avocados and remove the seeds. |
| 2 | Mash avocados with a fork. |
| 3 | Place mashed avocado in a small bowl. |
| 4 | Sprinkle with a small amount of lemon juice. |
| 5 | Add chopped onion, picante sauce, garlic powder, salt, black pepper, and Tabasco sauce. |
| 6 | Stir until well blended. |
| 7 | Serve soon after mixing. |
