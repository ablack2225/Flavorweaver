# Chocolate Pecan Pie

> A preserved family chocolate pecan pie recipe made with dark corn syrup, semi-sweet chocolate chips, eggs, sugar, vanilla, margarine, and chopped pecans in a 9-inch pie crust.

## Measured Ingredients

### Chocolate Pecan Pie

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 | 9-inch pie crust | Unbaked |
| 3 | Eggs | Slightly beaten |
| 1 cup | Dark corn syrup |  |
| 1/2 cup | Sugar |  |
| 1/2 cup | Semi-sweet chocolate chips | Melted with margarine |
| 2 Tbsp | Margarine | Melted with chocolate chips |
| 1 tsp | Vanilla |  |
| 1 1/2 cups | Chopped pecans |  |
| As desired | Cool-Whip | Optional topping |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 350°F. |
| 2 | Melt margarine and chocolate chips together in the microwave for about 1-2 minutes. |
| 3 | Stir chocolate mixture well. |
| 4 | In a medium bowl, beat together eggs, corn syrup, sugar, vanilla, and the chocolate mixture. |
| 5 | Stir in chopped pecans. |
| 6 | Pour filling into the unbaked pie crust. |
| 7 | Bake at 350°F for 50-60 minutes, or until pie is set. |
| 8 | Test doneness by inserting a knife halfway between the center and the edge of the pie. If the knife comes out clean, the pie is done; if not, bake a little longer. |
| 9 | Serve with Cool-Whip on top, if desired. |

## Notes

Original family note: this is a wonderful variation from the traditional pecan pie and is great with Cool-Whip on top.

Jimmie and Sue were both known in the family for their pies. This chocolate pecan pie is preserved as part of that family pie tradition.
