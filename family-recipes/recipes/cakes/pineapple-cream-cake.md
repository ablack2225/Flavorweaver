## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Bake cake according to package directions in a 13x9x2-inch pan. |
| 2 | Cool cake completely in the pan. |
| 3 | Combine crushed pineapple and sugar in a saucepan. Bring to a boil and boil 5 minutes. |
| 4 | Pour pineapple mixture over the cooled cake. |
| 5 | Prepare vanilla pudding according to package directions. Spread over cake and pineapple. |
| 6 | Top with banana slices and whipped topping. |
| 7 | Sprinkle coconut and chopped nuts on top. |
| 8 | Store in the refrigerator. |
