# Waldorf Salad

> A preserved family Waldorf salad made with sweet apples, celery, pecans, raisins, lemon juice, brown sugar, and a small amount of Miracle Whip dressing.

## Measured Ingredients

### Waldorf Salad

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 3 large | Sweet apples | Cored and chopped |
| 2 stalks | Celery | Chopped |
| 1/4 cup | Pecans | Chopped |
| 1/4 cup | Raisins |  |
| Light sprinkle | Lemon juice | To coat apples and prevent browning |
| Light sprinkling | Brown sugar |  |
| About 1 heaping Tbsp | Miracle Whip |  |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Place chopped apples into a bowl. |
| 2 | Lightly sprinkle apples with lemon juice and mix to coat all pieces, helping prevent browning. |
| 3 | Mix in raisins, celery, pecans, and a light sprinkling of brown sugar. |
| 4 | Stir mixture until well blended. |
| 5 | Add Miracle Whip dressing and stir until blended. |
