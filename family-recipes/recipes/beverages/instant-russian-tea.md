# Instant Russian Tea

> A preserved family instant Russian tea mix made with Tang beverage mix, sugar, instant tea with lemon, cloves, and cinnamon.

## Recipe Dashboard

| Status | Total Time | Servings |
|---|---:|---:|
| Preserved | 5 min | About 60-70 cups |

| Prep Time | Cook Time | Measurement Status |
|---:|---:|---|

## Ingredient List

| Component | Ingredients |
|---|---|
| Drink Mix Base | Tang beverage mix, unsweetened instant tea with lemon |
| Sweetener | Sugar |
| Spices | Cloves, cinnamon |
| Serving | Boiling water |

## Measured Ingredients

### Instant Russian Tea Mix

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 2 cups | Tang beverage mix |  |
| 1 1/2 cups | Sugar | See note for variation |
| 3/4 cup | Unsweetened instant tea with lemon |  |
| 1/2 tsp | Cloves |  |
| 1 tsp | Cinnamon |  |

### To Serve

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| About 3 tsp | Russian tea mix | Per cup |
| 1 cup | Boiling water | Or enough to fill cup |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Combine all ingredients and blend together well. |
| 2 | Store in an airtight container. |
| 3 | To serve, put about 3 teaspoons of mix into a cup. |
| 4 | Fill the cup with boiling water and stir. |

## Notes

Original family note: the instant tea with lemon could not be found, so 1 package of lemonade mix was substituted and the sugar was increased to about 2 cups.

Servings are estimated from the total amount of dry mix and the instruction to use about 3 teaspoons per cup. The original recipe page listed prep time as 5 minutes and left servings blank.
