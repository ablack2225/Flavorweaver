## Measured Ingredients

### Easy Chicken Fajita Skillet

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 lb | Boneless skinless chicken breasts | Cut into bite-size pieces |
| 1 tsp | Chili powder |  |
| 1/4 tsp | Salt |  |
| 2 cups / 8 oz | Elbow macaroni | Uncooked |
| 2 cups | Mixed red and green peppers | Coarsely chopped |
| 1 medium | Onion | Finely chopped |
| 1/2 lb / 8 oz | Velveeta | Cut up |
| 2 1/4 cups | Water | Added before macaroni |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Cook chicken, chili powder, and salt in a large nonstick skillet on medium-high heat for 5-7 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through, stirring occasionally. |
| 2 | Stir in water and bring to a boil. |
| 3 | Stir in uncooked macaroni. |
| 4 | Cover skillet. |
| 5 | Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer 5 minutes. |
| 6 | Add peppers and onion. |
| 7 | Cover and simmer an additional 5 minutes. |
| 8 | Add cut-up Velveeta. |
| 9 | Cook until Velveeta is melted, stirring frequently. |
