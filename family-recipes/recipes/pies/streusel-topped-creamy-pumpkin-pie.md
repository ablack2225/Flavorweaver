# Streusel Topped Creamy Pumpkin Pie

> A preserved recipe clipping for a chilled creamy pumpkin pie made with graham cracker crust, cheesecake pudding, whipped topping, and walnut-brown sugar streusel.

## Measured Ingredients

### Creamy Pumpkin Filling

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 1/4 cups | Cold milk |  |
| 1 cup | Canned pumpkin |  |
| 2 packages, 4-serving size each | Cheesecake flavor instant pudding |  |
| 1 tsp | Pumpkin pie spice |  |
| 1 tub / 8 oz | Frozen non-dairy whipped topping | Thawed, divided |

### Streusel Topping

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1/2 cup | Chopped walnuts | Diamond chopped walnuts style, as listed on clipping |
| 2 Tbsp | Brown sugar |  |
| 1 Tbsp | Butter |  |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Stir milk and pumpkin in a large bowl until smooth. |
| 2 | Add pudding mixes and pumpkin pie spice. |
| 3 | Beat with a wire whisk until well blended. |
| 4 | Gently stir in half of the whipped topping. |
| 5 | Spoon filling into the graham cracker crust. |
| 6 | Refrigerate for 4 hours, or until set. |
| 7 | To prepare streusel topping, mix chopped walnuts, brown sugar, and butter in a small microwaveable bowl. |
| 8 | Microwave on high for 2 1/2 minutes, or until bubbly, stirring once. |
| 9 | Cool streusel topping. |
| 10 | Sprinkle streusel over refrigerated pie just before serving. |
