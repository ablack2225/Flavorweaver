# Cole Slaw

> A preserved family cole slaw recipe made with green cabbage, carrots, onion, Miracle Whip, vinegar, sugar, salt, and pepper.

## Measured Ingredients

### Cole Slaw

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 large head | Green cabbage | Washed, cored, and coarsely chopped |
| Small amount | Onion | Chopped |
| 2-3 | Carrots | Grated |
| 1/2 cup | Sugar |  |
| 3/4 cup | Miracle Whip dressing |  |
| 1/4 cup | Vinegar |  |
| To taste | Salt and pepper | Add after dressing is mixed in |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Wash and core the cabbage. |
| 2 | Chop cabbage coarsely. This can be done with a food processor, if available. |
| 3 | Mix in grated carrots and chopped onion. |
| 4 | In a separate bowl, blend sugar, Miracle Whip, and vinegar together until well mixed. |
| 5 | Pour dressing mixture over cabbage mixture. |
| 6 | Stir to combine. |
| 7 | Add salt and pepper to taste. |
| 8 | Chill before serving, if desired. |
