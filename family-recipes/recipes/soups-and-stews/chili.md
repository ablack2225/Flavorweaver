# Chili

> A preserved family chili recipe made with ground beef, onion, tomatoes, chili powder, garlic powder, and a flour-thickened simmered base.

## Measured Ingredients

### Chili

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 lb | Ground beef |  |
| 1 large | Onion | Chopped |
| 3 1/2 Tbsp | Chili powder |  |
| 1 tsp | Salt |  |
| 1 (16 oz) can | Tomatoes | Blended with seasonings and water |
| 2 Tbsp | Flour | Stirred into browned meat mixture |
| 1 cup | Water |  |
| 1 tsp | Garlic powder |  |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | In a large pot, brown ground beef with chopped onion. |
| 2 | Stir flour into the browned meat mixture. |
| 3 | In a blender, mix tomatoes, chili powder, salt, garlic powder, and water. |
| 4 | Pour blended tomato mixture into the meat mixture. |
| 5 | Bring to a boil. |
| 6 | Cover, reduce heat to a simmer, and cook for 1 hour. |
| 7 | Stir frequently to prevent sticking. |
