# Cabbage Rolls

> A preserved family cabbage roll recipe filled with ground beef, celery, onion, bread crumbs, egg, basil, and pepper, then simmered in a sweet-tangy beef bouillon, tomato, brown sugar, and lemon juice sauce.

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Wash and trim a large head of cabbage. |
| 2 | Place cabbage in a large stock pot with about 2 inches of water. |
| 3 | Cover and bring to a boil. |
| 4 | Turn down to a simmer and cook 10-12 minutes to tenderize the leaves so they can be easily removed. |
| 5 | Remove cabbage from pan and allow it to cool while preparing the meat mixture. |
| 6 | Combine ground beef, onion, celery, bread crumbs, egg, basil, black pepper, and 1/2 tsp of the salt. |
| 7 | Mix until well combined. |
| 8 | Gently remove 8 of the outer cabbage leaves. |
| 9 | Fill each cabbage leaf with the meat mixture. |
| 10 | Roll up and turn ends down to seal. |
| 11 | Place cabbage rolls into a skillet. |
| 12 | Mix beef bouillon, brown sugar, lemon juice, chopped tomato, and remaining salt together. |
| 13 | Pour sauce mixture over the cabbage rolls. |
| 14 | Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover, and simmer about 45 minutes. |
| 15 | Spoon juice over the rolls occasionally during cooking. |
