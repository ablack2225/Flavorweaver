## Instructions

### Sue's Microwave Method

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | In a large microwave-safe bowl, mix all dry ingredients together: cereals, Bugles, bagel chips, optional rye chips, and pretzel sticks. |
| 2 | Melt butter or margarine in the microwave. |
| 3 | Stir Worcestershire sauce, seasoned salt, garlic powder, optional Tony Chachere's, and optional hot sauce into the melted butter. |
| 4 | Pour seasoned melted butter over the cereal mixture. |
| 5 | Stir until well combined and evenly coated. |
| 6 | Microwave on high for 6 minutes, stirring every 2 minutes. |
| 7 | Spread out to cool, then store in an airtight container. |

### Oven Method

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 275°F. Prepare 1-2 large rimmed baking sheets. |
| 2 | Add Corn Chex, Rice Chex, Wheat Chex, Bugles, bagel chips, optional rye chips, and pretzels to a large mixing bowl. Toss to combine. |
| 3 | In a microwave-safe bowl, melt butter with Worcestershire sauce, seasoned salt, garlic powder, optional Tony Chachere's, and optional hot sauce, heating at 30-second intervals until smooth. |
| 4 | Drizzle seasoned butter over the cereal mixture and gently toss until evenly coated. |
| 5 | Spread mix on baking sheets in a layer no thicker than 1 inch. |
| 6 | Bake for 30 minutes. Stir or toss mixture with a spatula. |
| 7 | Return to oven and bake 15 minutes more. |
| 8 | Cool completely before serving or storing. |

## Notes

This recipe belongs to Sue Wallesch.

Sue had this written down without Tony Chachere's, so Tony Chachere's is preserved as optional only.

Rye chips are also listed as optional.

Sue's written notes included a microwave method: mix all dry ingredients, melt margarine in the microwave, pour the melted butter over the cereal mixture, stir until well combined, then microwave 6 minutes on high, stirring every 2 minutes.

Hot sauce is optional for extra heat.

Timing and servings come from the clarified recipe. Microwave method and optional-ingredient notes are preserved from Sue's family-practice version.
