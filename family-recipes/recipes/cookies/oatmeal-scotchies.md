# Oatmeal Scotchies

> A preserved family oatmeal cookie recipe made with quick oats, brown sugar, cinnamon, and butterscotch-flavored morsels.

## Measured Ingredients

### Oatmeal Scotchies

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 1/4 cups | Flour |  |
| 1 tsp | Baking soda | Original lists soda |
| 1/2 tsp | Salt |  |
| 1/2 tsp | Ground cinnamon |  |
| 1 cup | Margarine | Softened |
| 3/4 cup | Sugar |  |
| 3/4 cup | Brown sugar | Packed |
| 2 | Eggs |  |
| 1 tsp | Vanilla |  |
| 3 cups | Quick cooking oatmeal | Uncooked |
| 1 (12 oz) package | Butterscotch-flavored morsels |  |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 375°F. |
| 2 | In a small bowl, combine flour, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon. Set aside. |
| 3 | In a large mixing bowl, beat margarine, sugar, brown sugar, eggs, and vanilla until creamy. |
| 4 | Gradually blend in flour mixture. |
| 5 | Stir in oatmeal and butterscotch morsels. |
| 6 | Drop by rounded tablespoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets. |
| 7 | Bake 7-8 minutes for chewier cookies, or 9-10 minutes for crisper cookies. |

## Notes

The recipe makes about 4 dozen cookies.

The original recipe preserves two texture options: bake 7-8 minutes for chewier cookies, or 9-10 minutes for crisper cookies.
