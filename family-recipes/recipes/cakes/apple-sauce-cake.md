## Measured Ingredients

### Apple Sauce Cake

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 3/4 cups | Flour | Sifted |
| 1/4 tsp | Salt |  |
| 1/2 tsp | Cinnamon |  |
| 1/2 tsp | Cloves |  |
| 1/2 tsp | Soda | Dissolved in hot applesauce |
| 1/2 cup | Shortening |  |
| 1 cup | Sugar |  |
| 1 | Egg | Beaten |
| 1 tsp | Vanilla |  |
| 1 cup | Applesauce | Hot, unsweetened |
| 1/2 cup | Raisins | Toss with a little flour before adding |
| 1/2 cup | Nut meats |  |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Heat oven to a moderate temperature. |
| 2 | Dissolve soda in hot unsweetened applesauce. |
| 3 | Cream shortening. |
| 4 | Add sugar and blend well. |
| 5 | Add beaten egg and applesauce mixture. |
| 6 | Toss raisins with a little flour. |
| 7 | Add remaining ingredients and blend. |
| 8 | Bake about 1 hour in a moderate oven. |
