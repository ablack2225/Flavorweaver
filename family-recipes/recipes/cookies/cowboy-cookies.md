## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 350°F. |
| 2 | Cream butter and sugars until light and fluffy. |
| 3 | Add vanilla and eggs, mixing well. |
| 4 | In a separate bowl, sift flour, baking soda, and salt together. |
| 5 | Gradually add flour mixture to sugar mixture. |
| 6 | Stir in chocolate chips, oatmeal, coconut, and pecans. |
| 7 | Drop by teaspoonfuls onto lightly greased or sprayed cookie sheet, or line cookie sheet with parchment paper. |
| 8 | Bake for 10 minutes at 350°F. |
| 9 | Remove from oven and cool on wire racks. |
