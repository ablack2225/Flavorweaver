# Fresh Peach Pie

> A preserved family fresh peach pie recipe made with sliced fresh peaches, lemon juice, cinnamon, sugar, flour, margarine, and pastry for an 8-inch two-crust pie.

## Measured Ingredients

### Fresh Peach Pie

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 | Pastry for 8-inch two-crust pie |  |
| 4 cups | Fresh peaches | Sliced; about 7-8 medium peaches |
| 1 tsp | Lemon juice |  |
| 2/3 cup | Sugar |  |
| 3 Tbsp | Flour |  |
| 1/4 tsp | Cinnamon |  |
| 1 Tbsp | Margarine | For dotting filling before top crust |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 425°F. |
| 2 | Prepare pastry for an 8-inch two-crust pie. |
| 3 | Mix sliced peaches and lemon juice. |
| 4 | Stir together sugar, flour, and cinnamon. |
| 5 | Mix sugar mixture with the peaches. |
| 6 | Turn peach filling into a pastry-lined pie pan. |
| 7 | Dot filling with margarine. |
| 8 | Cover with top crust that has slits cut in it. |
| 9 | Seal and flute the edges. |
| 10 | Bake 35-40 minutes, or until crust is brown and juice begins to bubble through the slits in the crust. |

## Notes

Jimmie and Sue were both known in the family for their pies. This fresh peach pie is preserved as part of that family pie tradition.

The original recipe calls for pastry for an 8-inch two-crust pie but does not include the pastry method on this page.
