## Measured Ingredients

### Orange Pecan Sweet Potatoes

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| As needed | Cooking spray | For baking dish |
| 3 lb / 4 large | Sweet potatoes | Peeled, chunked, and cooked |
| 1/2 cup | Chopped pecans | Toasted |
| 1 cup | Brown sugar |  |
| 2 Tbsp | Corn starch |  |
| 1 cup | Orange juice |  |
| 2 Tbsp | Butter |  |
| 1 tsp | Vanilla extract |  |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 350°F. |
| 2 | Spray a 3-quart, 13x9-inch baking dish with cooking spray. |
| 3 | Add cooked sweet potatoes and toasted pecans to the prepared baking dish. |
| 4 | In a small saucepan, combine brown sugar and corn starch. |
| 5 | Gradually add orange juice, whisking to blend. |
| 6 | Cook over high heat, stirring constantly, until mixture boils. |
| 7 | Reduce heat and simmer for 2-3 minutes. |
| 8 | Remove sauce from heat. |
| 9 | Add butter and vanilla. |
| 10 | Pour sauce over sweet potatoes. |
| 11 | Bake for 45 minutes, until edges are browned and bubbly. |
