## Measured Ingredients

### Peanut Butter Blossoms

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1/2 cup | Butter flavor all-vegetable shortening | Crisco style, as listed on clipping |
| 1/2 cup | Creamy peanut butter | Jif style, as listed on clipping |
| 1/2 cup | Brown sugar | Firmly packed |
| 1/2 cup | Granulated sugar | For dough |
| 1 large | Egg |  |
| 2 Tbsp | Milk |  |
| 1 tsp | Vanilla extract |  |
| 1 3/4 cups | All-purpose flour | Pillsbury BEST style, as listed on clipping |
| 1 tsp | Baking soda |  |
| 1/2 tsp | Salt |  |
| As needed | Sugar | For rolling dough balls |
| 48 | Foil-wrapped milk chocolate pieces | Unwrapped |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Heat oven to 375°F. |
| 2 | Cream together shortening, peanut butter, brown sugar, and 1/2 cup granulated sugar. |
| 3 | Add egg, milk, and vanilla. Beat well. |
| 4 | Stir flour, baking soda, and salt together. |
| 5 | Add dry mixture to creamed mixture. |
| 6 | Beat on low speed until stiff dough forms. |
| 7 | Shape dough into 1-inch balls. |
| 8 | Roll dough balls in sugar. |
| 9 | Place 2 inches apart on an ungreased cookie sheet. |
| 10 | Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until golden brown. |
| 11 | Immediately top each cookie with an unwrapped chocolate piece, pressing down firmly so the cookie cracks around the edge. |
| 12 | Remove from cookie sheets to cool. |
