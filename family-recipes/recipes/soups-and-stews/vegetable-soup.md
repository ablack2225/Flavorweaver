# Vegetable Soup

> A preserved family vegetable soup made with ground beef, tomatoes, tomato sauce, potatoes, carrots, mixed vegetables, beef bouillon, and simple seasonings.

## Measured Ingredients

### Vegetable Soup

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 lb | Ground beef |  |
| 1 large | Onion | Chopped |
| 2 (16 oz) cans | Tomatoes | Chopped |
| 1 (8 oz) can | Tomato sauce |  |
| 3-4 | Potatoes | Peeled and cubed |
| 3-4 | Carrots | Peeled and sliced |
| 1 (16 oz) can | Mixed vegetables | Added near the end |
| 4 cups | Beef bouillon |  |
| To taste | Salt and black pepper |  |
| Light sprinkling | Garlic powder |  |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | In a large stock pot, brown ground beef with chopped onion. |
| 2 | Drain off excess fat. |
| 3 | Mix in tomatoes, tomato sauce, carrots, potatoes, beef bouillon, and seasonings. |
| 4 | Bring to a boil. |
| 5 | Cover and reduce to a simmer. |
| 6 | Cook for about 45 minutes. |
| 7 | Add mixed vegetables. |
| 8 | Cook an additional 10-15 minutes. |
