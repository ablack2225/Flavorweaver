# Scalloped Potatoes

> A preserved recipe clipping for scalloped potatoes made with thinly sliced potatoes, onion, evaporated milk sauce, and grated Parmesan cheese.

## Measured Ingredients

### Scalloped Potatoes

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 2 lb / about 6 medium | Potatoes | Peeled and thinly sliced |
| 3 Tbsp | Butter or margarine |  |
| 1/4 cup | Chopped onion |  |
| 3 Tbsp | All-purpose flour |  |
| 1 tsp | Salt |  |
| 1/4 tsp | Ground black pepper |  |
| 1 (12 fl oz) can | Evaporated milk | Carnation style, as listed on clipping |
| 1 cup | Water |  |
| 1/3 cup | Grated Parmesan cheese |  |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 350°F. |
| 2 | Grease an 11x7-inch baking dish. |
| 3 | Place potatoes in a large saucepan. Cover with water and bring to a boil. |
| 4 | Cook over medium-high heat for 3-4 minutes. |
| 5 | Drain potatoes and set aside. |
| 6 | Heat butter in the same saucepan over medium heat. |
| 7 | Add onion and cook, stirring occasionally, for 1-2 minutes, or until onion is tender. |
| 8 | Stir in flour, salt, and pepper. |
| 9 | Gradually stir in evaporated milk and water. |
| 10 | Cook, stirring constantly, until mixture comes to a boil. |
| 11 | Remove sauce from heat. |
| 12 | Arrange potatoes in the prepared baking dish. |
| 13 | Pour milk mixture over potatoes. |
| 14 | Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. |
| 15 | Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until potatoes are tender and cheese is light golden brown. |
