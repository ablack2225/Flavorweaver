# Vanilla-Cherry Cream Dessert

> A preserved family no-bake layered dessert made with whole graham crackers, vanilla instant pudding, cherry pie filling, and Cool Whip.

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Mix vanilla pudding according to package directions. |
| 2 | In a large rectangular dish, spread a small amount of pudding over the bottom. |
| 3 | Place whole graham crackers in the dish to form a crust. |
| 4 | Spread on a layer of pudding, then half of the cherry filling. |
| 5 | Repeat with another layer of graham crackers, pudding, and cherry filling. |
| 6 | Cover the top with Cool Whip. |
| 7 | Refrigerate before serving. |
