# Hobo Dinners

## Measured Ingredients

### Hobo Dinners

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1/2 lb | Lean ground beef | Form into patties |
| 1/2 | Onion | Sliced |
| 4-5 large | Carrots | Peeled and sliced |
| 4-5 | Potatoes | Peeled and cubed |
| To taste | Salt and pepper | For vegetables and beef |
| To taste | Garlic powder | For vegetables and beef |
| Generous amount | Worcestershire sauce | Douse over top of meat |
| 4 Tbsp | Margarine | Sliced and dotted over carrots and potatoes |
| 2 large sheets | Aluminum foil | For packets |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 350°F. |
| 2 | On 2 large sheets of aluminum foil, place carrots and potatoes evenly. |
| 3 | Sprinkle vegetables with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. |
| 4 | Slice margarine and dot it over the carrots and potatoes. |
| 5 | Mix ground beef with salt, pepper, and garlic powder to taste. |
| 6 | Form ground beef into patties. |
| 7 | Place patties on top of the carrots and potatoes. |
| 8 | Place onion slices on top of the meat. |
| 9 | Douse a generous amount of Worcestershire sauce over the top of the meat. |
| 10 | Fold up the foil packets and place them on a baking sheet. |
| 11 | Bake in a preheated 350°F oven for 1 hour and 15 minutes. |
