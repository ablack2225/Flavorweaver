# Pineapple Cake

> A preserved family pineapple sheet cake made with crushed pineapple and topped with cream cheese frosting and chopped nuts while the cake is still hot.

## Recipe Dashboard

| Status | Total Time | Servings |
|---|---:|---:|
| Preserved | 45-50 min | 12-16 servings |

| Prep Time | Cook Time | Measurement Status |
|---:|---:|---|
| 15-20 min | 30 min | Documented |

## Ingredient List

| Component | Ingredients |
|---|---|
| Cake Batter | Flour, sugar, soda, eggs, crushed pineapple with juice |
| Frosting | Cream cheese, margarine, confectioners' sugar, milk, chopped nuts |

## Measured Ingredients

### Cake

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 3/4 cups | Flour |  |
| 1 3/4 cups | Sugar |  |
| 1 tsp | Baking soda |  |
| 2 | Eggs | Slightly beaten |
| 1 (20 oz) can | Crushed pineapple | With juice |

### Frosting

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 (3 oz) package | Cream cheese | Softened |
| 4 Tbsp | Margarine | Softened |
| 1 1/2 cups | Confectioners' sugar |  |
| Small amount | Milk | Add until frosting reaches spreading consistency |
| 1 cup | Chopped nuts | Mix into frosting |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 350°F. |
| 2 | Combine all cake ingredients by hand. |
| 3 | Pour batter into a greased and lightly floured 13x9x2-inch baking pan. |
| 4 | Bake for about 30 minutes. |
| 5 | For the frosting, combine cream cheese, margarine, and confectioners' sugar. |
| 6 | Add a small amount of milk to make the frosting the right spreading consistency. |
| 7 | Mix in the chopped nuts. |
| 8 | Spread frosting on the hot cake. |

The frosting is spread on the cake while it is still hot.
