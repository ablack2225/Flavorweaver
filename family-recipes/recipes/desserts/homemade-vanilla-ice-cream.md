# Homemade Vanilla Ice Cream

> A preserved family homemade vanilla ice cream recipe made in a 4-quart ice cream freezer. In the family, this ice cream became known as Susie-Bell and later Goga-Bell.

## Measured Ingredients

### Homemade Vanilla Ice Cream

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 4 | Eggs | Beaten |
| 2 cups | Sugar |  |
| 5 cups | Cream | Cold |
| 3 cups | Milk | Cold |
| 3 Tbsp | Vanilla |  |
| 1/8 tsp | Salt |  |

### Strawberry Variation

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 2 cups | Frozen strawberries | Pureed; add to cream mixture |

### Chocolate Variation

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 2 1/4 cups | Sugar | Use instead of 2 cups sugar |
| 4 squares | Unsweetened chocolate | Melt and cool slightly; mix into sugar and egg mixture before adding cream and milk |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Beat the eggs. |
| 2 | Beat in sugar and salt. |
| 3 | Gradually blend in cold cream and cold milk. |
| 4 | Mix in vanilla. |
| 5 | Pour mixture into a 4-quart ice cream freezer. |
| 6 | Freeze according to ice cream freezer directions. |

## Strawberry Variation

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Add 2 cups frozen, pureed strawberries to the cream mixture before freezing. |

## Chocolate Variation

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Increase sugar to 2 1/4 cups. |
| 2 | Melt 4 squares unsweetened chocolate and cool slightly. |
| 3 | Mix melted chocolate into the sugar and egg mixture before adding the cream and milk. |
| 4 | Freeze according to ice cream freezer directions. |

## Notes

Original family note: Rudy called this ice cream Susie-Bell. It was as good as any store-bought ice cream, and even better because it had that special homemade ice cream taste that could not come from a carton.

When Sue became a grandmother, the recipe was referenced as Goga-Bell from that day forward.
