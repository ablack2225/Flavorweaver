## Amy Kitchen Changes

| Recipe Element | Flavorweaver Kitchen Version |
|---|---|
| Butter or ghee | Use butter only. We do not use ghee. |
| Tomato measurement | Use practical pound measurement: about 1.3 lb fresh tomatoes. Remove gram measurements. |
| Nut base | Use cashews. Do not substitute almonds unless Amy specifically tests that later. |
| Service | Serve over basmati rice. This was the tested version and was excellent. |

## Cooking Structure Summary

| Stage | Purpose |
|---|---|
| First marinade | Seasons chicken with chili, salt, and lemon before the yogurt marinade. |
| Second marinade | Adds yogurt, ginger-garlic, spices, kasuri methi, and oil for deeper flavor. |
| Tomato-cashew sauce | Builds a creamy tomato sauce with cashews, butter, spices, and cream. |
| Chicken cook | Chicken is cooked separately before finishing in the sauce. |
| Final finish | Cream and kasuri methi round the sauce at the end. |
| Service | Serve over hot basmati rice. |

## Flavorweaver Cook Notes

- Use butter only in Amy's kitchen version.
- Keep the tomatoes as about **1.3 lb fresh tomatoes** rather than gram-based measurement.
- Use cashews for the sauce base.
- Serve over basmati rice.
- This was excellent as-is, so do not over-modify before the next repeat test.

## Do Not Forget

- Do not use ghee.
- Do not swap cashews for almonds unless intentionally testing a new version.
- Do not remove the basmati rice service note; this is how Amy tested it.
