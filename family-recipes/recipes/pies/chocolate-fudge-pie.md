# Chocolate Fudge Pie

> A preserved recipe clipping for two rich chocolate fudge pies with a cream cheese drizzle topping.

## Measured Ingredients

### Pie Shells

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |

| 2 | Deep-dish piecrusts | 9-inch |

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 375°F. |
| 2 | Prick the bottom and sides of piecrusts with a fork in several places. |
| 3 | Bake piecrusts for 5 minutes. |
| 4 | Remove piecrusts from oven and reduce oven heat to 325°F. |
| 5 | To prepare filling, melt butter and chocolate in a double boiler over low heat. Mix well and remove from heat. |
| 6 | In a medium bowl, combine sugar and eggs. Beat with a mixer at medium-high speed until ribbons form, about 10 minutes. |
| 7 | Add vanilla to the egg mixture. |
| 8 | In a medium bowl, combine all-purpose flour and cocoa. Mix well. |
| 9 | Using a firm spatula, fold melted chocolate into egg mixture. |
| 10 | Add flour mixture and stir until just blended. |
| 11 | Pour filling into piecrusts. |
| 12 | Place pies on a baking sheet. |
| 13 | Bake for 50 minutes. The pies are done when puffed and cracked all over the top. |
| 14 | Remove from oven and cool on a wire rack for 30 minutes. |
| 15 | Put a 10-inch piece of parchment paper on top and place a large plate on its surface. |
| 16 | Refrigerate overnight. |
| 17 | To prepare topping, combine cream cheese, heavy cream, and orange liqueur or lemon juice in a medium bowl. Beat with a mixer at medium speed until blended. |
| 18 | Reduce speed to low and gradually add powdered sugar. Beat until smooth. |
| 19 | Transfer topping to a plastic zip-top bag. Cut off one corner of the bag and drizzle topping over each piece of pie before serving. |
