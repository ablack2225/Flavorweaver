| 2 tsp | Creole seasoning | Used for chicken and seasoned flour mixture |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 425°F. |
| 2 | Dry chicken with a clean paper towel. |
| 3 | Sprinkle chicken with garlic powder and Creole seasoning. |
| 4 | In a large zipper-top plastic bag, combine flour, black pepper, cayenne pepper, paprika, and Creole seasoning. |
| 5 | Close bag and mix well. |
| 6 | Coat chicken in the bag with flour and seasonings. |
| 7 | Line a 9x13 glass baking dish with foil. |
| 8 | Add olive oil or melted butter to the lined baking dish. |
| 9 | Place chicken in the dish, skin side down. |
| 10 | Bake for 30 minutes. |
| 11 | Turn chicken, reduce oven heat to 325°F, and bake 35-45 minutes longer, or until done. |
| 12 | Chicken will be golden brown and very tender when finished. |
