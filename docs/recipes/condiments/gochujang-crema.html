Gochujang Crema

A creamy, tangy, lightly spicy gochujang finishing sauce developed for Bold Bulgogi Bowls and now kept as a reusable Flavorweaver house condiment.

Prep5 min
Cook0 min
Total5 min
YieldAbout 1/3 cup
gochujangcremasaucehouse stapledrizzle sauce

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 2 tsp gochujang
  • 1 to 2 tsp fresh lime juice
  • Pinch of sugar
  • 1 to 3 tsp water, as needed to thin

Method

  1. Add sour cream, gochujang, lime juice, and sugar to a small bowl.
  2. Stir until completely smooth and evenly colored.
  3. Add water one teaspoon at a time until drizzle consistency is reached.
  4. Taste and adjust with more lime, sugar, sour cream, or gochujang.

Texture Goal

Thin enough to drizzle but thick enough to cling to bowls. Keep thicker when using as a dip or spread.

Pairs Well With

  • Bold Bulgogi BowlsAttached dish and original use for this crema.
