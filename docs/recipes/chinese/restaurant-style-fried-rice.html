Restaurant-Style Fried Rice

Simple restaurant-style fried rice with day-old rice, eggs, onion, peas and carrots, green onions, soy sauce, butter, neutral oil, and sesame oil.

StatusWorking Recipe
Total30 min
Serves4
DifficultyEasy

Measured Ingredients

4 to 5 cups day-old rice; 2 eggs; 1/2 diced onion; 1 cup peas and carrots; 3 green onions; 3 Tbsp soy sauce; 1 Tbsp butter; 1 Tbsp neutral oil; 1 tsp sesame oil.

Stage-Based Prep Bowls

Bowl 1 — Rice

4 to 5 cups day-old rice. Break up clumps before cooking.

Bowl 2 — Eggs

2 eggs, lightly beaten. Cook first and reserve.

Bowl 3 — Vegetables

1/2 diced onion and 1 cup peas and carrots.

Bowl 4 — Finish

Green onions, soy sauce, butter, sesame oil.

Full Cooking Timeline and Instructions

  1. Cook eggs first and reserve.
  2. Saute onion and vegetables in neutral oil.
  3. Add day-old rice and toast lightly.
  4. Season with soy sauce and butter.
  5. Return eggs and fold through.
  6. Finish with sesame oil and green onions.

Final Taste Target

Savory, lightly buttery, hot, separated rather than mushy, and lightly toasted with tender vegetables and soft egg throughout.
