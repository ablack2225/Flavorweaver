# Pie Crust

> A preserved family pie crust recipe from Jimmie Hatton, with instructions for an 8-inch or 9-inch crust, adjustments for a 10-inch crust, and an optional baked pie shell method.

## Measured Ingredients

### 8-Inch or 9-Inch Pie Crust

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 cup | Flour |  |
| 1/2 tsp | Salt |  |
| 1/3 cup plus 1 Tbsp | Crisco shortening |  |
| 2-3 Tbsp | Ice cold water | Add 1 Tbsp at a time |

### 10-Inch Pie Crust Adjustment

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 1/3 cups | Flour | Increased amount for 10-inch crust |
| 1/2 cup | Crisco shortening | Increased amount for 10-inch crust |
| 3-4 Tbsp | Ice cold water | Increased amount for 10-inch crust |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Measure flour and salt into a bowl. |
| 2 | Cut in shortening thoroughly. |
| 3 | Sprinkle in ice water, 1 Tbsp at a time, mixing until all the flour is moistened and the dough almost cleans the side of the bowl. |
| 4 | Gather dough into a ball. |
| 5 | Shape into a flattened round on a lightly floured surface. |
| 6 | With a floured rolling pin, roll dough 2 inches larger than an inverted pie plate. |
| 7 | Fold crust into quarters, ease into pie plate, and unfold. |
| 8 | Trim overhanging edge of crust 1 inch from the rim of the pie plate. |
| 9 | Fold and roll under, even with the pan, then flute as desired. |
| 10 | For a 10-inch pie crust, use the 10-inch adjustment amounts and proceed as directed. |
| 11 | For a baked crust, prick the bottom and sides thoroughly with a fork. |
| 12 | Bake in a preheated 475°F oven for 8-10 minutes, until lightly brown. |
| 13 | Cool and add your favorite filling. |

## Notes

This pie crust recipe is preserved as Jimmie Hatton's family recipe.

Jimmie and Sue were both known in the family for their pies. This crust belongs with that family pie tradition.

The recipe makes one 8-inch or 9-inch pie crust. The original recipe also includes adjustments for making a 10-inch crust and instructions for baking an empty pie shell.
