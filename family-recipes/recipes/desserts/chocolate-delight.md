# Chocolate Delight

> A preserved family layered dessert with a baked nut crust, cream cheese and Cool Whip layer, vanilla pudding, chocolate pudding, and a Cool Whip topping.

## Measured Ingredients

### Chocolate Delight

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 3/4 cup | Margarine | Melted |
| 3/4 cup | Finely chopped nuts |  |
| 1 1/2 cups | Flour |  |
| 1 cup | Confectioners' sugar |  |
| 8 oz | Cream cheese | Softened |
| 1 (8 oz) container | Cool Whip | Use 1/2 container in cream cheese layer; reserve rest for topping |
| 2 small boxes | Instant vanilla pudding |  |

| 2 small boxes | Instant chocolate pudding |  |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 350°F. |
| 2 | Mix melted margarine, chopped nuts, and flour together. |
| 3 | Press mixture into the bottom of a 13x9-inch baking pan. |
| 4 | Bake for 15 minutes. |
| 5 | Allow crust to cool completely before proceeding with recipe. |
| 6 | For the first layer, combine confectioners' sugar, softened cream cheese, and 1/2 container Cool Whip. Blend thoroughly. |
| 7 | Spread cream cheese mixture over the cooled crust. |
| 8 | Mix vanilla pudding and 3 cups milk according to package directions. |
| 9 | Pour vanilla pudding over the cream cheese mixture. |
| 10 | Mix chocolate pudding and 3 cups milk according to package directions. |
| 11 | Pour chocolate pudding over the vanilla pudding. |
| 12 | Frost the top with the remaining Cool Whip. |
| 13 | Refrigerate several hours to allow dessert to set up before serving. |

## Notes

The original recipe calls for refrigerating several hours to allow the dessert to set up before serving.

The Cool Whip is handled as one 8 oz container divided between the cream cheese layer and the topping.

Servings, prep time, cooling time, chill time, and total time are estimated from the 13x9 pan and layered dessert method. Bake time is from the recipe text.
