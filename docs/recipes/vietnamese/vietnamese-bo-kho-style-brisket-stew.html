Vietnamese Bò Kho-Style Brisket Stew

A complete Vietnamese-inspired bread-dipping plate: aromatic Bò Kho-style brisket stew, fresh bánh mì không-style dipping rolls, sesame-free pickled garden vegetables finished with lime, cilantro, and optional serrano, plus lime, herbs, serrano, black pepper, and sriracha at the table.

Full Plate Ingredient Roll-Up

Stew Core

2 to 2 1/2 lb brisket; 2 1/2 Tbsp fish sauce plus more; 1 Tbsp soy sauce; 3 Tbsp tomato paste; 2 Tbsp ginger; 5–6 garlic cloves; 2 Tbsp lemongrass; 1–1 1/2 tsp five-spice; 1–1 1/2 tsp paprika; onion; bay; star anise; 4 cups beef broth; hoisin; carrots.

Bread Component

2 cups all-purpose flour; 1 cup bread flour; 2 tsp active dry yeast; 1 tsp sugar; 1 cup water at 105°F–108°F plus up to 1/4 cup more; 1 1/2 tsp fine salt; 1 Tbsp neutral oil; 1 tsp lime juice; 1 cup hot water for steam.

Pickle Component

1 zucchini; 1 yellow squash; 1 carrot; 2 green onions; 1/4 cup rice vinegar; 2 Tbsp sugar; 1 tsp kosher salt; 1 Tbsp water; optional 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes. Finish with lime, cilantro, optional serrano.

Fresh Service

Lime wedges, cilantro, sliced green onion, thin-sliced serrano, black pepper, and sriracha stay close to service.

Measured Ingredients by Component

These collapsible sections are reference only. The cooking flow is handled by the stage-based prep bowls and full timeline below.

Bò Kho Brisket Stew Ingredients

Marinade: 2 to 2 1/2 lb brisket, 1 1/2 Tbsp fish sauce, 1 Tbsp soy sauce, 1 Tbsp neutral oil, 1 Tbsp brown sugar, 1 Tbsp tomato paste, 1 Tbsp ginger, 3–4 cloves garlic, 1 Tbsp lemongrass paste, 1 tsp five-spice, 1 tsp paprika, 1/2 tsp black pepper.

Stew base: 1 Tbsp neutral oil, 1 medium onion, 2 cloves garlic, 1 Tbsp ginger, 1 Tbsp lemongrass paste, 2 Tbsp tomato paste, optional 1/2 tsp paprika, optional 1/2 tsp five-spice, 2 bay leaves, 1 star anise pod, 4 cups beef broth, 1–2 cups water, 1 Tbsp fish sauce, 1 Tbsp hoisin, optional 1–2 tsp brown sugar, optional 1–2 tsp sriracha, 3 large carrots.

Bánh Mì Không-Style Dipping Rolls Ingredients

2 cups all-purpose flour, 1 cup bread flour, 2 tsp active dry yeast, 1 tsp sugar, 1 cup water at 105°F–108°F, up to 1/4 cup extra warm water, 1 1/2 tsp fine salt, 1 Tbsp neutral oil, 1 tsp fresh lime juice, and 1 cup hot water for oven steam.

Asian Pickled Garden Vegetables — Bò Kho Mode Ingredients

1 zucchini, 1 yellow squash, 1 carrot, 2 green onions, 1/4 cup rice vinegar, 2 Tbsp sugar, 1 tsp kosher salt, 1 Tbsp water, optional 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes.

Bò Kho finish: drain lightly and finish with lime juice, cilantro, and optional thin-sliced serrano. Do not use sesame oil or sesame seeds for this plate.

Fresh Garnish / Table Finish Ingredients

Lime wedges, cilantro, sliced green onion, thin-sliced serrano, black pepper, and sriracha. Cut fresh items close to service.

Cross-Component Prep Map

Garlic: mince 5–6 cloves total, then divide 3–4 cloves for marinade and 2 cloves for Stew Aromatic Bowl 2.

Ginger: measure 2 Tbsp total, then divide 1 Tbsp for marinade and 1 Tbsp for Stew Aromatic Bowl 2.

Lemongrass: measure 2 Tbsp total, then divide 1 Tbsp for marinade and 1 Tbsp for Stew Aromatic Bowl 2.

Lime: measure 1 tsp juice for bread dough; keep fresh lime for pickle finish, stew finish, and garnish close to service.

Green onion, cilantro, serrano: prep garnish portions close to service for freshness.

Before Cooking Day / Advance Prep

Marinate Brisket

2 hours to overnight. Do not rinse before cooking. Keep the marinade flavor with the beef; scrape only heavy excess if needed before searing.

Make Pickle Base

2 hours to overnight. Make the Asian Pickled Garden Vegetables base, but do not add lime, cilantro, serrano, sesame oil, or sesame seeds yet.

Confirm Bread Setup

Day before or morning of. Confirm active dry yeast, all-purpose flour, bread flour, and a thermometer for 105°F–108°F water.

Do Not Prep Ahead

Do not chop cilantro, slice serrano, cut lime wedges, or finish the pickles until close to service.

Stage-Based Prep Bowls

Bread Bowls

Bowl 1 — Yeast Bloom

1 cup water at 105°F–108°F; 1 tsp sugar; 2 tsp active dry yeast.

Bowl 2 — Dry Ingredients

2 cups all-purpose flour; 1 cup bread flour; 1 1/2 tsp fine salt.

Bowl 3 — Dough Additions

1 Tbsp neutral oil; 1 tsp lime juice; up to 1/4 cup extra warm water as needed.

Stew Protein Bowls

Bowl 1 — Marinated Brisket

2 to 2 1/2 lb marinated brisket chunks, lightly scraped if needed.

Stew Aromatic Bowls

Bowl 1 — Onion

1 medium onion, sliced or diced. Cook alone after beef sears, 5–7 minutes.

Bowl 2 — Garlic, Ginger & Lemongrass

2 cloves garlic, minced; 1 Tbsp ginger, grated or paste; 1 Tbsp lemongrass paste. Add after onion softens; cook 30–60 seconds.

Bowl 3 — Tomato Paste & Spices

2 Tbsp tomato paste; 1/2 tsp paprika; 1/2 tsp Chinese five-spice if using; 2 bay leaves; 1 star anise pod.

Stew Braise Bowls

Bowl 1 — Broth, Reserved Marinade & Seasoning Liquids

4 cups beef broth; reserved marinade; 1 Tbsp fish sauce; 1 Tbsp hoisin; 1–2 tsp brown sugar if needed; 1–2 tsp sriracha if using; 1–2 cups water as needed.

Vegetable, Pickle & Garnish Bowls

Vegetable Bowl 1 — Carrots

3 large carrots, cut into thick diagonal pieces. Add during final 35–45 minutes.

Pickle Bowl 1 — Pickle Base

1 thin-sliced zucchini; 1 thin-sliced yellow squash; 1 carrot ribbons/julienne; 2 sliced green onions; 1/4 cup rice vinegar; 2 Tbsp sugar; 1 tsp kosher salt; 1 Tbsp water; optional 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes.

Pickle Bowl 2 — Vietnamese / Bò Kho Finish

Drained pickled vegetables; fresh lime juice to taste; cilantro to taste; optional thin-sliced serrano. No sesame.

Garnish Bowl 1 — Table Finish

Lime wedges; cilantro; sliced green onion; thin-sliced serrano; black pepper; sriracha.

Full Cooking Timeline and Instructions

  1. Marinate the brisket. Combine 2 to 2 1/2 lb brisket chunks with 1 1/2 Tbsp fish sauce, 1 Tbsp soy sauce, 1 Tbsp neutral oil, 1 Tbsp brown sugar, 1 Tbsp tomato paste, 1 Tbsp ginger, 3–4 minced garlic cloves, 1 Tbsp lemongrass paste, 1 tsp five-spice, 1 tsp paprika, and 1/2 tsp black pepper. Marinate at least 2 hours or overnight.
  2. Make Pickle Bowl 1. Combine zucchini, yellow squash, carrot, and 2 green onions with 1/4 cup rice vinegar, 2 Tbsp sugar, 1 tsp kosher salt, 1 Tbsp water, and optional red pepper flakes. Chill 2–4 hours or overnight.
  3. Start the stew about 4 hours before serving. Do not rinse brisket. Scrape only heavy excess marinade if needed and reserve it.
  4. Sear Stew Protein Bowl 1. Heat 1 Tbsp neutral oil and sear brisket in batches until deeply browned.
  5. Cook Stew Aromatic Bowl 1. Add 1 medium onion and cook 5–7 minutes.
  6. Bloom Stew Aromatic Bowl 2. Add 2 minced garlic cloves, 1 Tbsp ginger, and 1 Tbsp lemongrass paste. Cook 30–60 seconds.
  7. Toast Stew Aromatic Bowl 3. Add 2 Tbsp tomato paste and cook 1–2 minutes. Stir in paprika, five-spice if using, bay leaves, and star anise.
  8. Add Stew Braise Bowl 1. Return beef to the pot. Add 4 cups broth, reserved marinade, 1 Tbsp fish sauce, 1 Tbsp hoisin, optional sugar, optional sriracha, and water as needed. Simmer low 2 1/2–3 1/2 hours.
  9. Start Bread Bowl 1 about 2 hours 45 minutes before serving. Bloom 2 tsp active dry yeast with 1 cup water at 105°F–108°F and 1 tsp sugar for 5–10 minutes.
  10. Mix Bread Bowls 2 and 3. Add flour blend, salt, 1 Tbsp oil, 1 tsp lime juice, and up to 1/4 cup extra warm water. Knead 5–7 minutes, then rise 60–90 minutes.
  11. Shape rolls about 1 hour 15 minutes before serving. Divide into 4 short baguette-style rolls and rise 35–45 minutes.
  12. Add Vegetable Bowl 1. Add 3 large carrots during final 35–45 minutes of stew cooking.
  13. Bake rolls close to service. Preheat to 450°F with steam pan, slash rolls, add 1 cup hot water to steam pan, and bake 18–22 minutes.
  14. Finish Pickle Bowl 2. Drain pickles lightly and finish with lime, cilantro, and optional serrano. Do not add sesame.
  15. Finish stew. Remove 2 bay leaves and 1 star anise pod. Adjust with fish sauce/salt, lime or rice vinegar, sugar if sharp, sriracha if desired, and black pepper. Rest 10 minutes.
  16. Serve with Garnish Bowl 1. Serve hot stew with fresh rolls, pickles beside the stew, lime wedges, cilantro, green onion, serrano, black pepper, and sriracha at the table.

Chef's Notes

This is the bread-dipping Bò Kho lane, not a noodle bowl. The complete plate is stew + fresh rolls + pickled vegetables + fresh garnish/table finish.

Thai basil is optional when fresh is available. If fresh Thai basil is not available, omit it rather than substituting dried basil.

For the pickled vegetables, skip sesame oil and sesame seeds for this plate.
