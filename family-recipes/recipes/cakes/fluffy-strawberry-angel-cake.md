## Measured Ingredients

### Fluffy Strawberry Angel Cake

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 | Angel food cake | Slice into 3 layers |
| 1 cup | Boiling water |  |
| 1 (3 oz) box | Strawberry gelatin |  |
| 1/2 cup | Cold water |  |
| 1 (8 oz) carton | Cool Whip | Use half in filling and half for frosting |
| 1 pint | Fresh strawberries | Sliced; reserve some for garnish if desired |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Mix the gelatin with the boiling water and stir until completely dissolved. |
| 2 | Add the cold water. |
| 3 | Refrigerate about 1 hour, or until slightly thickened. |
| 4 | Remove gelatin mixture from the refrigerator. |
| 5 | Fold in the sliced strawberries, reserving some for garnish if desired. |
| 6 | Fold in half of the Cool Whip. |
| 7 | Return mixture to the refrigerator for about 15 minutes. |
| 8 | Slice the angel food cake into 3 layers. |
| 9 | Fill each layer with the gelatin mixture. |
| 10 | Frost the top and sides of the cake with the remaining Cool Whip. |
| 11 | Garnish with reserved strawberries, if desired. |
| 12 | Refrigerate until ready to serve. |
