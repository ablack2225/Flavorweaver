# Tator Tot Casserole

> A preserved family casserole made with ground beef, onion, cream of mushroom soup, cubed Velveeta cheese, and frozen tator tots baked until hot and set.

## Measured Ingredients

### Tator Tot Casserole

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 lb | Ground beef | Crumbled raw into baking dish |
| 1 medium | Onion | Chopped |
| 1 can | Cream of mushroom soup |  |
| 1 package | Frozen tator tots |  |
| 1/4 lb | Velveeta cheese | Cubed |
| To taste | Salt and pepper | Sprinkle lightly over beef |
| To taste | Garlic powder | Sprinkle lightly over beef |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 350°F. |
| 2 | Grease a baking dish, approximately 8x8x2 inches. |
| 3 | Crumble ground beef in the dish and sprinkle lightly with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. |
| 4 | Add chopped onion over the beef. |
| 5 | Mix cream of mushroom soup and Velveeta cheese together and heat until all the cheese is melted. |
| 6 | Pour soup and cheese mixture over the meat mixture. |
| 7 | Top with frozen tator tots. |
| 8 | Bake in a preheated 350°F oven for about 1 hour. |
