# Vitals Log Template

> This is a blank tracking template for private wellness use. Do not record actual vitals, diagnosis details, medication details, or supplement dosing in the public Flavorweaver repository.

Use this template in a private notebook, private spreadsheet, private Notion page, local file, or printed binder.

This log is meant to help notice patterns over time. It is not a medical diagnosis tool, emergency guide, or replacement for clinician advice.

### Orthostatic Notes

Record simple cues:

- dizziness
- lightheadedness
- shakiness
- chest fluttering
- shortness of breath
- weakness
- sweating
- nausea
- headache
- vision changes
- needed to sit or lie down

Stop if symptoms feel unsafe.

## Food and GI Pattern Notes

Use this only for broad pattern tracking, not calorie tracking.

| Date | Nutrition Bridge | Real-Food Meal | Starch / Bread | Vegetables | GI Response | Appetite / Fullness |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| YYYY-MM-DD | shake / broth / other | brief meal name | rice / noodles / naan / none | cooked cabbage / carrots / etc. | nausea / bloating / pain / tolerated | low / okay / too full |

Helpful notes:

- Was the meal warm, soft, and cooked?
- Was there too much bread, dairy, raw salad, or legumes?
- Did symptoms appear immediately or hours later?
- Was the portion too dense?
- Did the meal feel nourishing, heavy, or irritating?

## Gentle Logging Rules

- Track patterns, not perfection.
- Do not turn this into a punishment chart.
- Record enough to help future decisions, then move on.
- Use short notes on hard days.
- If a symptom feels severe, unusual, or unsafe, seek appropriate medical help rather than relying on the log.
