## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 350°F. |
| 2 | Cream butter and both sugars together. |
| 3 | Add eggs and vanilla and combine well. |
| 4 | Add flour, baking soda, and cinnamon and combine into ingredients. |
| 5 | Add quick oats and chopped dates. |
| 6 | Mix together. |
| 7 | Drop dough by teaspoonfuls onto an ungreased cookie sheet. |
| 8 | Bake for 11-12 minutes, or until golden brown. |
