## Verification Notes

Natural and seasoned rice vinegar should not be treated as interchangeable in nutrition calculations.

If a recipe is built around controlled sugar or sodium, prefer calculating with Natural Rice Vinegar unless Amy specifies the seasoned bottle was used.

If a different rice vinegar brand, black vinegar, rice wine, mirin, or sushi seasoning is used, create a separate product reference or mark the calculation as estimated pending label.
