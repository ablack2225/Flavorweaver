# Crispy Chewy Cornflake Cookies

> A preserved family cookie recipe made with sugar-frosted flakes cereal, quick oatmeal, brown sugar, vanilla, and cooking oil.

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 375°F. |
| 2 | Beat together sugar, brown sugar, vanilla, eggs, and cooking oil. |
| 3 | Combine flour, baking soda, salt, and oatmeal. |
| 4 | Blend dry ingredients into the sugar mixture. |
| 5 | Stir in the sugar-frosted flakes. |
| 6 | Roll into balls and place on an ungreased cookie sheet. |
| 7 | Bake for 8-10 minutes. Shorter baking time yields chewy cookies; longer baking time yields crispier cookies. |
