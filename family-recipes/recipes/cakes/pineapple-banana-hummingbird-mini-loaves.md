# Pineapple Banana Hummingbird Mini Loaves

## Measured Ingredients

### Mini Loaves

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 package, 2-layer size | Yellow cake mix |  |
| 1 (3.4 oz) package | Vanilla flavor instant pudding and pie filling |  |
| 4 | Eggs |  |
| 1/4 cup | Vegetable oil |  |
| 1 tsp | Ground cinnamon |  |
| 1 (20 oz) can | Crushed pineapple | Juice drained and 1 cup reserved |
| 1 cup | Reserved pineapple juice | From drained crushed pineapple |
| 2 medium | Ripe bananas | Well mashed, about 1 cup |
| 1/2 cup | Chopped pecans |  |
| As desired | Additional chopped pecans | Optional garnish |

### Cream Cheese Glaze

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 2 oz | Cream cheese | Softened |
| 1 Tbsp | Milk |  |
| 1/2 cup | Powdered sugar |  |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 350°F. |
| 2 | Combine cake mix, dry instant pudding, reserved pineapple juice, eggs, and oil in a large bowl. |
| 3 | Beat with mixer for 2 minutes, or until blended. |
| 4 | Add crushed pineapple, mashed bananas, and chopped pecans. |
| 5 | Mix just until blended. |
| 6 | Spray 5 foil mini loaf pans with nonstick cooking spray. |
| 7 | Pour prepared batter into the mini loaf pans. |
| 8 | Bake for 40 minutes, or until loaves test done. |
| 9 | Cool completely. |
| 10 | Combine softened cream cheese, milk, and powdered sugar to make glaze. |
| 11 | Drizzle cream cheese glaze over cooled loaves. |
| 12 | Garnish with additional chopped pecans, if desired. |
