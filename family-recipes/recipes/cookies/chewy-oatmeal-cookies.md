# Chewy Oatmeal Cookies

> A preserved family oatmeal cookie recipe made with quick oats, brown sugar, cinnamon, chopped nuts, and either raisins or chopped dates.

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 375°F. |
| 2 | Combine Crisco, brown sugar, egg, milk, and vanilla in a large bowl. |
| 3 | Beat at medium speed until well blended. |
| 4 | Combine oatmeal, flour, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon. |
| 5 | Mix dry ingredients into the creamed mixture until well blended. |
| 6 | Stir in chopped nuts and raisins or dates, if preferred. |
| 7 | Drop rounded tablespoonfuls onto greased cookie sheets. |
| 8 | Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until lightly browned. |
| 9 | Cool 2 minutes on cookie sheet. |
| 10 | Remove to a sheet of aluminum foil to cool completely. |
| 11 | Store in a cookie canister. |
