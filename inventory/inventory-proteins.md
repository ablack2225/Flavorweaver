## Buying From Store / Incoming

- Pork half loin
  - Cost-effective pork purchase.
  - Slice into pork loin cutlets for schnitzel, pork cutlets, stir-fry strips, pork medallions, and freezer portions.
  - For schnitzel: slice cutlets about 5 to 6 oz each, then pound to about 1/4 inch thick.

## Usually Kept / House Protein Standards

### Beef

- Whole brisket — Usually kept  
  - Used for breaking down into multiple kitchen forms.
  - Can become fresh-ground brisket, stew meat, braises, chopped beef, freezer portions, or specialty projects.

- Fresh-ground brisket — House standard  
  - Default ground beef replacement.
  - Preferred over grocery-store ground beef or standard chuck.
  - Used for burgers, bowls, meat sauces, meatballs, hamburger steak, bulgogi bowls, and ground beef-based recipes.

- Brisket stew meat — Usually kept  
  - Best for braises, stews, slow-cooked sauces, and world cuisine projects.
  - Good candidates: bò kho, adobo, birria, goulash, beef curry, beef tagine, ropa vieja.

### Chicken

- Chicken thighs — Usually kept  
  - Preferred for marinades, rice bowls, stews, grilling, roasting, and saucy dishes.
  - Strong choice for bold cuisines because they stay juicy.

- Chicken breasts — Usually kept  
  - Useful for lighter meals, shredding, soups, casseroles, grilled chicken, and quick prep.
  - May need extra care to avoid drying out.

### Pork

- Pork chops — Usually kept  
  - Good for pan-seared dinners, smothered dishes, Southern-style meals, marinades, and quick weeknight proteins.

- Bacon — Usually kept  
  - Used as a flavor base, fat source, topping, breakfast protein, and smoky accent.

- Smoked sausage — Usually kept  
  - Useful for beans, soups, skillet meals, cabbage dishes, rice dishes, and quick comfort meals.

- Italian sausage — Usually kept  
  - Used for Italian sauces, lasagna, soups, pizzas, meatballs, and casseroles.

- Breakfast sausage — Usually kept  
  - Used for breakfast-for-dinner, egg skillets, gravy, casseroles, and freezer-friendly meals.

- Chorizo — Usually kept  
  - Used for Mexican-inspired dishes, egg dishes, rice bowls, beans, soups, potatoes, and bold breakfast-for-dinner meals.

### Seafood

- Shrimp — Usually kept  
  - Used for stir-fries, fried shrimp, curries, rice bowls, pasta, seafood soups, and quick meals.

- Catfish — Usually kept  
  - Used for Southern-style meals, blackened fish, fried fish, seafood plates, and Cajun-inspired dinners.

### Eggs

- Eggs — Usually kept  
  - Core protein extender.
  - Used for breakfast-for-dinner, fried rice-style dishes, bowls, doro wat-style meals, baking, breading, sauces, and quick meals.
