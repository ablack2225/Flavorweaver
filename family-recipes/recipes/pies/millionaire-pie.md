## Measured Ingredients

### Millionaire Pie

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 (14 oz) can | Sweetened condensed milk |  |
| 1 (8 oz) package | Cream cheese | Softened |
| 1 (12 oz) container | Frozen whipped topping | Thawed |
| 1 (20 oz) can | Crushed pineapple | Drained |
| 1 1/2 cups | Chopped pecans |  |
| 2 (9 inch) | Prepared graham cracker crusts |  |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | In a large bowl, blend sweetened condensed milk and softened cream cheese together until smooth. |
| 2 | Gently fold in thawed whipped topping. |
| 3 | Stir in drained crushed pineapple and chopped pecans. |
| 4 | Divide filling evenly between the prepared graham cracker crusts. |
| 5 | Chill pies until set, about 3-4 hours. |
| 6 | Serve chilled. |

## Notes

This recipe was preserved from Rudy Wallesch's handwritten recipe note and Amy's clarified family-practice version.

The handwritten note appeared to read Millionaire Pie and included condensed milk, pineapple, pecans, lemon juice, Cool Whip, and graham cracker crust. Amy provided the clarified active version for preserving the usable recipe.

Prep time and servings are estimated. Chill time and two-pie yield are from the clarified recipe.
