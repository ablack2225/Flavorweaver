# Gingerbread Cookies

> A preserved recipe clipping for rolled gingerbread cutout cookies made with molasses, brown sugar, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, cloves, and optional decorating icing.

## Measured Ingredients

### Gingerbread Cookies

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 3 cups | All-purpose flour |  |
| 1 tsp | Ground cinnamon |  |
| 1/2 tsp each | Ground ginger, ground nutmeg, and ground cloves |  |
| 1 tsp | Baking soda |  |
| 1 cup | Spread | Imperial style, as listed on clipping |
| 1/2 cup | Molasses |  |
| 3/4 cup | Light brown sugar | Firmly packed |
| 2 Tbsp | Water |  |
| As desired | Decorating icing | Optional |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Combine flour, spices, and baking soda in a medium bowl; set aside. |
| 2 | In a large bowl, beat spread, molasses, brown sugar, and water with an electric mixer until combined. |
| 3 | With mixer on low speed, gradually add flour mixture just until combined, scraping down sides of bowl occasionally. |
| 4 | Divide dough into quarters. |
| 5 | Wrap each portion in plastic wrap and flatten into a rectangle. |
| 6 | Refrigerate dough for 2 hours or overnight. |
| 7 | Preheat oven to 350°F. |
| 8 | Remove plastic wrap from dough. |
| 9 | Roll each dough quarter about 1/4-inch thick on a well-floured board with a well-floured rolling pin. |
| 10 | Cut dough into shapes with a 3 1/2-inch cookie cutter. |
| 11 | Arrange cookies on nonstick cookie sheets, 1 inch apart. |
| 12 | Bake for 9 minutes, or until set. |
| 13 | Remove cookies from sheets and cool completely. |
| 14 | Re-roll dough trimmings to cut additional cookies. |
| 15 | Decorate with decorating icing, if desired. |
