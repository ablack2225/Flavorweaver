Misir Wat

Ethiopian-style spiced red lentils simmered with onion, garlic, ginger, tomato paste, and House Berbere-Style Spice Blend until thick, soft, spoonable, and deeply seasoned.

Prep10m
Cook25m
Total35m
Serves2 to 4 side servings
Red lentilsBerbereEthiopian side

Ingredient List

Red Lentil Stew

Red lentils, onion, butter or oil, garlic, ginger, House Berbere-Style Spice Blend, tomato paste, water or broth, salt, and optional lemon.

Measured Ingredients

Misir Wat

1 cupRed lentilsRinsed well.
1 smallOnionFinely diced.
1 TbspUnsalted butter or oilUse oil for dairy-free version.
2 clovesGarlicMinced.
1 tspFresh grated ginger or ginger paste
1 to 1 1/2 TbspHouse Berbere-Style Spice BlendAdjust to heat and spice preference.
1 TbspTomato pasteAdds body and depth.
2 1/2 to 3 cupsWater or brothStart with 2 1/2 cups and add more as needed.
To tasteSaltAdd near the end.
Small squeezeLemonOptional, if the lentils taste heavy.

Instructions

  1. Rinse the red lentils well and set aside.
  2. Cook the onion in butter or oil over medium heat until soft and reduced.
  3. Add garlic, ginger, House Berbere-Style Spice Blend, and tomato paste. Cook 1-2 minutes, stirring often, until fragrant and deepened. Do not let the spices burn.
  4. Add rinsed red lentils and 2 1/2 cups water or broth. Stir well, scraping the bottom of the pot.
  5. Simmer uncovered for 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the lentils soften, break down, and become thick.
  6. Add more liquid as needed if the lentils thicken before they are fully soft.
  7. Season with salt to taste.
  8. Finish with a small squeeze of lemon if the lentils taste heavy.

Chef's Notes

Misir Wat is the more traditional red-lentil direction for the Ethiopian platter. The working platter may use Split Pea Alicha when red lentils are unavailable, but this is the component to use when building the more traditional-style version.

The final texture should be thick, soft, and spoonable. Add liquid gradually as needed and stir more often near the end so the lentils do not scorch.
