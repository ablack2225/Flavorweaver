# Apricot Nectar Cake

> A preserved family tube cake made with lemon cake mix, apricot nectar, eggs, sugar, vegetable oil, and a simple powdered sugar glaze.

## Ingredient List

| Component | Ingredients |
|---|---|

## Measured Ingredients

### Cake

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 box | Lemon flavored cake mix |  |
| 1 cup | Apricot nectar |  |
| 4 | Eggs |  |
| 1/2 cup | Sugar |  |
| 3/4 cup | Vegetable oil |  |

### Glaze

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 cup | Powdered sugar |  |
| 1 Tbsp | Milk |  |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 350°F. |
| 2 | Beat together all ingredients except the powdered sugar and milk. |
| 3 | Beat about 2 minutes, until thoroughly mixed. |
| 4 | Pour into a greased and floured tube pan. |
| 5 | Bake for about 1 hour, or until the cake tests done. |
| 6 | Immediately turn cake out onto a serving platter and allow to cool completely. |
| 7 | Make a glaze with the powdered sugar and milk. |
| 8 | Drizzle glaze over the cake. |
