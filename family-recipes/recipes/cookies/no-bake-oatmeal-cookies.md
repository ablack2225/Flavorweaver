# No-Bake Oatmeal Cookies

> A preserved family no-bake oatmeal cookie recipe made with sugar, cocoa, milk, margarine, quick oats, and optional peanut butter.

## Measured Ingredients

### No-Bake Oatmeal Cookies

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 2 cups | Sugar |  |
| 3 Tbsp | Cocoa |  |
| 1/2 cup | Milk |  |
| 1/2 cup | Margarine | 1 stick |
| 1/4 cup | Peanut butter | Optional |
| 2 1/2 cups | Quick oatmeal | Uncooked |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Mix sugar, cocoa, milk, and margarine together in a saucepan. |
| 2 | Place on stove and bring to a boil. |
| 3 | Boil and stir for 1 minute. |
| 4 | Remove from heat. |
| 5 | Add peanut butter, if desired, and oatmeal. Stir until combined. |
| 6 | Either spread mixture onto a greased cookie sheet or drop by spoonfuls onto waxed paper. |
| 7 | Allow to cool. |
