## Notes

This non-edible household recipe was written in Sue Wallesch's handwriting on a Friday January 1981 calendar notepad kept beside Pete and Jimmie Hatton's phone.

Amy noted that anyone who went to Pete and Jimmie's house would have seen that calendar notepad sitting on the bar, making this note a uniquely recognizable piece of family history.

The original note says this recipe is not for human consumption and should be placed only where children and pets cannot reach it.

Amy also noted that this household recipe was worth its weight in gold when dealing with pesky roaches.

Prep time and drying time are estimated. Ingredient amounts are preserved from Amy's typed transcription.
