## Flavorweaver Calculation Notes

Use this reference when a recipe uses Great Value Green Split Peas from the 1 lb bag with the same dry 1/4 cup / 35g nutrition profile.

Quick conversion:

```text
1/4 cup dry = 130 calories, 22g carbs, 8g fiber, 14g net carbs, 8g protein
1/2 cup dry = 260 calories, 44g carbs, 16g fiber, 28g net carbs, 16g protein
1 cup dry = 520 calories, 88g carbs, 32g fiber, 56g net carbs, 32g protein
```

Net carbs are calculated as total carbohydrates minus dietary fiber.

## Verification Notes

Use dry measurements for recipe calculations unless the recipe gives cooked weight or cooked cup measurements.

Cooked volume changes with soaking, simmering, and water absorption, so calculate the recipe from the dry amount whenever possible.

If a different split pea brand, canned peas, cooked-only measurement, or yellow split peas are used, create a separate product reference or mark the calculation as estimated pending label.
