# Potato Patties

> A preserved recipe clipping for simple skillet potato patties made with mashed potatoes, onion, egg, and flour.

## Measured Ingredients

### Potato Patties

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 2 Tbsp | Onion | Finely chopped |
| About 2 cups | Mashed potatoes |  |
| 1 | Egg | Slightly beaten |
| About 1/4 cup | Flour | For dipping patties |
| As needed | Butter | For skillet; amount not specified on clipping |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Combine finely chopped onion, mashed potatoes, and slightly beaten egg. |
| 2 | Shape mixture into patties. |
| 3 | Dip patties in flour. |
| 4 | Melt butter in a skillet. |
| 5 | Add patties to skillet. |
| 6 | Cook about 5 minutes on each side, or until browned. |
