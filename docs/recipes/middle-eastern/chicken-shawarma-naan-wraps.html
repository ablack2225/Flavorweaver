Chicken Shawarma Naan Wraps with Garlic Sauce and Lemon Tahini

Spiced chicken thighs, warm naan, garlic sauce, lemon tahini, crispy potatoes, pickles, tomato, cucumber, onion, and herbs.

Kitchen Execution Summary

Shawarma Chicken

Chicken thighs marinated in warm spices, garlic, lemon, and oil, then cooked, rested, sliced thin, and re-crisped.

Warm Naan

Homemade naan is the house wrap base. Warm before assembly so it stays flexible.

Sauce Bar

Garlic sauce is the main sauce; lemon tahini adds a thin nutty stripe.

Pickles + Potatoes

Pickled acid and crispy potatoes make the wrap feel complete.

Before Cooking Day / Advance Prep

For Sunday service: marinate Saturday evening. A 12 to 18 hour marinade is ideal.

Friday prep: mix the dry spice blend and make sauces. Do not put the lemon-garlic marinade on the chicken until Saturday.

Naan: make dough Saturday and refrigerate after the first rise if using the house naan recipe.

Service Countdown

Saturday evening: marinate chicken.

Sunday, 2 to 3 hours before serving: bring naan dough out if refrigerated and prep potatoes.

Sunday, 60 minutes before serving: cook potatoes and adjust sauces.

Sunday, 35 minutes before serving: cook chicken, rest, slice, and re-crisp.

At service: warm naan and build wraps.

Full Wrap Ingredient Roll-Up

Chicken

1 lb chicken thighs, oil, lemon, garlic, cumin, coriander, smoked paprika, turmeric, allspice, cardamom, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, black pepper, cayenne, salt, honey.

Wrap Base

3 to 4 naan plus crispy potatoes or fries.

Sauces

Garlic sauce with mayo, yogurt or sour cream, garlic, lemon, oil, salt. Lemon tahini with tahini, lemon, garlic, salt, cumin, ice water.

Fresh Finish

Tomato, cucumber, onion, herbs, pickles or pickled onions, lemon wedges.

Measured Ingredients by Component

Shawarma Chicken Marinade

1 lb chicken thighs; 2 Tbsp oil; 1 Tbsp lemon juice; 1 tsp lemon zest; 4 grated garlic cloves; 1 tsp kosher salt; 2 tsp cumin; 1 tsp coriander; 1 tsp smoked paprika; 1/2 tsp turmeric; 1/2 tsp allspice; 1/4 tsp cardamom; 1/4 tsp cinnamon; 1/4 tsp ginger; tiny pinch nutmeg; 1/2 tsp black pepper; 1/8 to 1/4 tsp cayenne; 1 tsp honey or brown sugar; optional 1 Tbsp Greek yogurt.

Garlic Sauce

1/4 cup mayonnaise; 2 Tbsp Greek yogurt or sour cream; 1 to 2 grated garlic cloves; 1 to 2 tsp lemon juice; 1 tsp olive oil; pinch kosher salt; tiny pinch sugar or honey; cold water only if needed.

Lemon Tahini

1/4 cup tahini; 1 1/2 Tbsp lemon juice; 1 small grated garlic clove; 1/4 tsp kosher salt; 1/8 tsp cumin; 3 to 5 Tbsp ice-cold water; tiny drizzle honey only if bitter.

Wrap Fill

3 to 4 warm naan; 2 medium potatoes or frozen fries; 1 tomato; 1/2 cucumber; 1/4 to 1/2 small onion; 1/2 cup pickles or pickled onions; herbs; lemon wedges.

Stage-Based Prep Bowls

Bowl 1 — Chicken

1 lb chicken thighs.

Bowl 2 — Wet Base

Oil, lemon, garlic, honey, optional yogurt.

Bowl 3 — Spice Blend

Salt, cumin, coriander, smoked paprika, turmeric, allspice, cardamom, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, black pepper, cayenne.

Bowl 4 — Garlic Sauce

Mayo, yogurt or sour cream, garlic, lemon, oil, salt.

Bowl 5 — Lemon Tahini

Tahini, lemon, garlic, salt, cumin, ice water.

Bowl 6 — Wrap Build

Warm naan, crispy potatoes, fresh vegetables, pickles, herbs, sauces.

Do Not Forget

Marinate Saturday evening for Sunday. Keep lemon juice to 1 Tbsp. Cook thighs whole, rest, slice thin, then re-crisp. Garlic sauce is primary; tahini is a thin stripe. Drain pickles and wet toppings. Warm naan before filling.

Full Cooking Timeline and Instructions

  1. Saturday evening: combine marinade ingredients, coat 1 lb chicken thighs, cover, and refrigerate 12 to 18 hours.
  2. Make garlic sauce: stir sauce ingredients smooth and chill.
  3. Make lemon tahini: whisk tahini, lemon, garlic, salt, and cumin; add ice water 1 Tbsp at a time until pourable.
  4. Sunday: cook potatoes or fries until crisp and hold warm.
  5. Prep fill: slice tomato, cucumber, onion, herbs, and drain pickles.
  6. Cook chicken: cook whole marinated thighs until browned and cooked through.
  7. Rest and slice: rest 5 to 10 minutes, then slice thin across the grain.
  8. Re-crisp: return sliced chicken and juices to a hot pan for 1 to 2 minutes.
  9. Build wraps: warm naan, spread garlic sauce, add chicken, potatoes, pickles, fresh vegetables, herbs, more garlic sauce, and a thin stripe of lemon tahini.
  10. Set and serve: fold or roll; griddle seam-side down if desired. Serve immediately.

Building the Final Wrap

Warm naan, garlic sauce, hot sliced chicken, crispy potatoes, drained pickles or pickled onions, tomato, cucumber, onion, herbs, a second small spoon of garlic sauce, and a thin lemon tahini stripe.

Final Taste Target

Juicy, deeply spiced, garlicky, lemon-bright, tangy from pickles, creamy from sauce, nutty from a thin tahini stripe, and fresh from vegetables and herbs. Not soggy, tahini-heavy, or yogurt-dominant.

Chef's Notes

Tahini is useful here, but it should not replace the garlic sauce. If the chicken was fully thawed earlier than Friday, cook it Saturday, chill it, and re-crisp the sliced meat Sunday before assembly.
