## Measured Ingredients

### Fudge Brownies

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 2/3 cup | Margarine |  |
| 1 1/2 cups | Sugar |  |
| 2/3 cup | Water |  |
| 1 (12 oz) package | Semi-sweet chocolate chips |  |
| 2 tsp | Vanilla |  |
| 4 | Eggs | Add one at a time |
| 1 1/2 cups | Flour |  |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 325°F. |
| 2 | Combine margarine, sugar, and water in a medium saucepan. |
| 3 | Heat until mixture begins to boil. |
| 4 | Remove from heat and add chocolate chips and vanilla. |
| 5 | Blend until all chocolate is melted. |
| 6 | Beat in eggs, one at a time. |
| 7 | Combine all dry ingredients and gradually mix into the chocolate mixture until well blended. |
| 8 | Mix in chopped nuts. |
| 9 | Spread batter into a greased 13x9x2-inch baking pan. |
| 10 | Bake for 50 minutes. |
| 11 | Allow to cool. |
| 12 | Cut into squares. |
