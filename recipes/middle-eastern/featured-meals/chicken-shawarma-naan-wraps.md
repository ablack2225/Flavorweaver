# Chicken Shawarma Naan Wraps with Garlic Sauce and Lemon Tahini

> Deeply spiced chicken thighs cooked hot, sliced thin, and re-crisped into shawarma-style edges, wrapped in warm homemade naan with toum-style garlic sauce, lemon tahini, crisp potatoes, pickles, tomato, cucumber, onion, and herbs.

## Before Cooking / Advance Prep

- **Marinade timing** — Marinate the chicken **12 to 18 hours before cooking**. Do not extend the lemon-garlic marinade beyond 24 hours.
- **Dry spice blend** — The shawarma spice blend can be mixed 1 to 2 days ahead and kept covered.
- **Sauces** — Garlic sauce and lemon tahini can be made up to 1 day ahead and refrigerated.
- **Required naan component** — If making [Fluffy Skillet Naan](../../indian/breads/fluffy-skillet-naan.md), the dough can be refrigerated after the first rise, then brought to room temperature before shaping and cooking.
- **Potato prep** — Potatoes can be cut and held in cold water the day of cooking, then dried very well before cooking.

## Do Not Forget

- Marinate the chicken **12 to 18 hours before cooking**.
- Keep the lemon juice controlled at **1 Tbsp** for the overnight chicken marinade.
- [Fluffy Skillet Naan](../../indian/breads/fluffy-skillet-naan.md) is the required house bread component unless using prepared naan.
- Cook chicken thighs whole, rest them, slice thin, then re-crisp for shawarma-style edges.
- Do not drown the wrap in tahini. Use lemon tahini as a thin stripe.
- Garlic sauce is the main chicken-shawarma sauce.
- Pickles or pickled onions are essential, not decoration.
- Warm naan before filling so it bends and wraps cleanly.
- Drain wet toppings before assembly.

## Full Cooking Timeline and Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | **Mix the dry shawarma spice blend.** In a small bowl, combine **1 tsp kosher salt**, **2 tsp ground cumin**, **1 tsp ground coriander**, **1 tsp smoked paprika**, **1/2 tsp turmeric**, **1/2 tsp allspice**, **1/4 tsp ground cardamom**, **1/4 tsp cinnamon**, **1/4 tsp ground ginger**, a **tiny pinch nutmeg**, **1/2 tsp black pepper**, and **1/8 to 1/4 tsp cayenne**. Cover and hold until ready to marinate. |
| 2 | **Make the chicken marinade.** In a medium bowl, whisk **2 Tbsp olive oil or neutral oil**, **1 Tbsp lemon juice**, **1 tsp lemon zest**, **4 grated garlic cloves**, **1 tsp honey or brown sugar**, and optional **1 Tbsp Greek yogurt**. Add the full dry shawarma spice blend and whisk until the marinade is thick, fragrant, and evenly colored. |
| 3 | **Marinate the chicken thighs.** Add **1 lb boneless skinless chicken thighs** to the marinade and turn until every piece is coated. Keep the thighs whole for cooking. Cover and refrigerate **12 to 18 hours**. |
| 4 | **Make the toum-style garlic sauce.** In a small bowl, stir together **1/4 cup mayonnaise**, **2 Tbsp Greek yogurt or sour cream**, **1 to 2 grated garlic cloves**, **1 to 2 tsp lemon juice**, **1 tsp olive oil**, a **pinch of kosher salt**, and a **tiny pinch of sugar or honey**. If the sauce is too thick to spread, loosen with **1 to 2 tsp cold water**, adding only **1 tsp at a time**. Chill until service. |
| 5 | **Make the lemon tahini sauce.** In a separate small bowl, whisk **1/4 cup tahini**, **1 1/2 Tbsp lemon juice**, **1 small grated garlic clove**, **1/4 tsp kosher salt**, and **1/8 tsp ground cumin**. The tahini will thicken first. Whisk in **3 to 5 Tbsp ice-cold water**, adding **1 Tbsp at a time**, until pale, creamy, and pourable. Add a tiny drizzle of honey only if the tahini tastes bitter. |
| 6 | **Prepare the required naan component.** Make or warm **3 to 4 naan**. For the house version, use [Fluffy Skillet Naan](../../indian/breads/fluffy-skillet-naan.md). If using refrigerated naan dough, bring it to room temperature before shaping, proofing, rolling, and cooking according to the naan recipe. Keep cooked naan covered and warm so it stays flexible. |
| 7 | **Prepare the crispy potato tuck-in.** Cut **2 medium potatoes** into **1/4-inch fries or small wedges**, or use frozen fries. If using fresh potatoes, rinse or soak briefly, dry very well, then toss with **1 to 2 Tbsp neutral oil**, **1/2 tsp kosher salt**, optional **1/2 tsp paprika**, and optional **1/4 tsp garlic powder**. Roast, air-fry, or fry until crisp. Hold warm. |
| 8 | **Prep the fresh and pickled wrap fill.** Slice **1 medium tomato**, **1/2 cucumber**, and **1/4 to 1/2 small onion**. If the onion is sharp, soak it in cold water for **10 minutes**, then drain. Chop a **small handful of parsley or cilantro** if using. Drain **1/2 cup pickles or pickled onions** very well so the naan does not get soggy. |
| 9 | **Cook the chicken thighs hot.** Remove the marinated chicken from the refrigerator **20 to 30 minutes** before cooking. Heat a cast iron skillet, griddle, broiler, grill pan, or electric skillet until hot. Cook the whole marinated thighs until deeply browned at the edges and cooked through, about **4 to 6 minutes per side** depending on thickness. |
| 10 | **Rest and slice the chicken.** Rest the cooked chicken **5 to 10 minutes** so the juices settle. Slice very thin across the grain into shawarma-style strips. Save the resting juices. |
| 11 | **Re-crisp the sliced chicken.** Return the thin-sliced chicken and saved juices to a hot skillet for **1 to 2 minutes**, tossing just until the edges pick up color and the spices smell toasted again. Do not dry it out. |
| 12 | **Warm the naan for assembly.** Warm **3 to 4 naan** until soft and flexible. If desired, lightly griddle each naan before filling. Do not fill cold naan. |
| 13 | **Build each wrap with measured components.** For each warm naan, spread about **1 Tbsp toum-style garlic sauce**, add **1/3 to 1/4 of the hot sliced chicken**, about **1/4 cup crispy potatoes**, **1 to 2 Tbsp drained pickles or pickled onions**, a controlled layer of tomato, cucumber, onion, and herbs, another **1 to 2 tsp garlic sauce**, and a thin **1 to 2 tsp stripe of lemon tahini**. |
| 14 | **Set the wrap and serve.** Fold or roll the naan. Optional: griddle seam-side down for **30 to 60 seconds** so the wrap holds together. Serve immediately with extra garlic sauce, lemon tahini, pickles, and lemon at the table. |

## Building the Final Wrap

| Layer | Build Detail |
|---|---|
| 1. Required Warm Naan | Use **1 warm naan per wrap**, preferably [Fluffy Skillet Naan](../../indian/breads/fluffy-skillet-naan.md). Warm until flexible; do not fill cold naan. |
| 2. Garlic Sauce Foundation | Spread about **1 Tbsp toum-style garlic sauce** so every bite has garlic sauce without making the naan wet. |
| 3. Hot Shawarma Chicken | Add **1/3 to 1/4 of the thin-sliced re-crisped chicken**, depending on whether making 3 or 4 wraps. |
| 4. Crispy Potato Tuck-In | Add a small line of crispy potatoes, about **1/4 cup per wrap** if using. |
| 5. Pickled Acid | Add **1 to 2 Tbsp drained pickles or pickled onions**. This is essential contrast. |
| 6. Fresh Crunch | Add tomato, cucumber, onion, and herbs in controlled amounts so the naan does not get soggy. |
| 7. Sauce Finish | Add **1 to 2 tsp garlic sauce** and a thin **1 to 2 tsp lemon tahini stripe**. |
| 8. Wrap and Griddle | Fold tightly and griddle seam-side down if desired. Serve immediately. |

## Final Taste Target

The finished wrap should taste juicy, deeply spiced, garlicky, lemon-bright, lightly smoky, tangy from pickles, creamy from sauce, nutty from a thin tahini stripe, and fresh from vegetables and herbs.

The chicken should have browned edges and a sliced shawarma texture, not large bland chunks. The naan should stay warm and flexible. The wrap should not be soggy, tahini-heavy, or yogurt-dominant.

## Chef's Notes

Tahini is useful here, but it should not replace the garlic sauce. For chicken shawarma, the garlic sauce is the main sauce; lemon tahini is a nutty finishing stripe.

For best results, marinate the chicken 12 to 18 hours before cooking. The dry spice blend and sauces can be made earlier, but the lemon-garlic chicken marinade should not sit on the chicken for more than 24 hours.
