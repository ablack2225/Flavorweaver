# Ultimate Chocolate Chip Cookies

> A preserved family chocolate chip cookie recipe made with butter flavor Crisco, brown sugar, semi-sweet chocolate chips, and chopped pecans.

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 375°F. |
| 2 | Combine Crisco, brown sugar, milk, and vanilla in a large mixing bowl. |
| 3 | Beat at medium speed until well blended. |
| 4 | Beat in egg thoroughly. |
| 5 | Combine flour, salt, and baking soda. |
| 6 | Mix dry ingredients into creamed mixture just until blended. |
| 7 | Stir in chocolate chips and chopped pecans. |
| 8 | Drop by rounded tablespoonfuls, 3 inches apart, onto ungreased cookie sheets. |
| 9 | Bake 8-10 minutes for chewy cookies. Cookies will appear moist; do not overbake. |
| 10 | Bake 11-13 minutes for crisp cookies. |
| 11 | Cool on cookie sheet for 2 minutes. |
| 12 | Remove to a sheet of aluminum foil to cool completely. |

## Notes

The recipe makes about 3 dozen cookies.

The original recipe preserves two texture options: bake 8-10 minutes for chewy cookies, or 11-13 minutes for crisp cookies. For chewy cookies, the cookies will appear moist; do not overbake.
