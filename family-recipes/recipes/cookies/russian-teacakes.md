# Russian Teacakes

> A preserved family holiday cookie recipe made with softened margarine, confectioners' sugar, vanilla, flour, salt, and finely chopped nuts, then rolled in confectioners' sugar while warm and again after cooling.

## Measured Ingredients

### Russian Teacakes

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 cup | Margarine | Softened |
| 1/2 cup | Confectioners' sugar | Plus more for rolling cookies |
| 1 tsp | Vanilla |  |
| 2 1/4 cups | Flour |  |
| 1/4 tsp | Salt |  |
| 3/4 cup | Finely chopped nuts |  |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 400°F. |
| 2 | Mix margarine, confectioners' sugar, and vanilla thoroughly. |
| 3 | Work in flour, salt, and nuts until dough holds together. |
| 4 | Shape dough into 1-inch balls. |
| 5 | Place on an ungreased cookie sheet. |
| 6 | Bake 10-12 minutes, or until set but not brown. |
| 7 | While still warm, roll cookies in confectioners' sugar. |
| 8 | Cool, then roll in confectioners' sugar again. |
