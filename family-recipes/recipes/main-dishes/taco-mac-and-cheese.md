# Taco Mac & Cheese

> A preserved recipe clipping for a one-skillet taco macaroni and cheese made with ground beef, salsa, chili powder, elbow macaroni, and Velveeta.

## Measured Ingredients

### Taco Mac & Cheese

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 lb | Ground beef |  |
| 1/3 cup | Thick and chunky salsa | Taco Bell Home Originals style, as listed on clipping |
| 1 1/2 tsp | Chili powder |  |
| 2 cups / 8 oz | Elbow macaroni | Uncooked |
| 1/2 lb / 8 oz | Velveeta | Cut up |
| 2 3/4 cups | Water | Added with salsa and chili powder |
| 1/2 cup | Crushed tortilla chips | Optional garnish |
| 1/4 cup | Chopped tomato | Optional garnish |
| 2 Tbsp | Chopped green onion | Optional garnish |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Brown ground beef in a large skillet over medium-high heat. |
| 2 | Drain beef. |
| 3 | Stir in water, salsa, and chili powder. |
| 4 | Bring mixture to a boil. |
| 5 | Stir in uncooked macaroni. |
| 6 | Cover skillet. |
| 7 | Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer 8-10 minutes, or until water is almost absorbed. |
| 8 | Add cut-up Velveeta. |
| 9 | Cook until Velveeta is melted, stirring frequently. |
| 10 | Garnish with crushed tortilla chips, chopped tomato, and chopped green onion, if desired. |
