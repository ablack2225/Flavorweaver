# Souper Meat Loaf

> A preserved family meat loaf recipe made with lean ground beef, bread crumbs, egg, cream of mushroom soup, dried onion soup mix, and a simple mushroom soup gravy made from reserved drippings.

## Measured Ingredients

### Souper Meat Loaf

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 2 lb | Lean ground beef |  |
| 1/2 cup | Bread crumbs |  |
| 1 | Egg | Beaten |
| 1 can | Cream of mushroom soup | Divided |
| 1 package | Dried onion soup mix |  |
| 1/4 cup | Water | For gravy |
| 2 Tbsp | Reserved drippings | Reserved after baking |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 350°F. |
| 2 | Mix 1/2 cup cream of mushroom soup, dried onion soup mix, ground beef, bread crumbs, and beaten egg thoroughly. |
| 3 | Place mixture in a 2-quart oblong baking dish and firmly shape into an 8x4-inch loaf. |
| 4 | Bake in a preheated 350°F oven for 1 hour and 15 minutes, or until done. |
| 5 | Spoon off fat and reserve 2 Tbsp drippings. |
| 6 | In a small saucepan, combine remaining cream of mushroom soup, water, and reserved drippings. |
| 7 | Heat thoroughly over low heat, stirring occasionally. |
| 8 | Serve the gravy over the top of the meat loaf slices. |
