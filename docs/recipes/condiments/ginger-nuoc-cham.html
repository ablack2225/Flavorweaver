Restaurant-Style Ginger Nuoc Cham

A bright, sweet-salty ginger fish sauce with lime, garlic, serrano, and optional lemongrass.

Prep10 min
Cook10 min
Cool30 min
YieldAbout 1 1/2 cups
vietnamese-inspireddipping saucefish saucegingerlimebrightsweet-salty

Ingredient List

Syrup Base

Water, sugar, ginger paste, garlic, optional lemongrass paste.

Finish

Fish sauce, lime juice, serrano.

Measured Ingredients

Syrup Base

1 cupWater
1/2 cupSugar
1 1/2-2 TbspGinger pasteAdjust to taste.
2 clovesGarlicSmashed.
1 TbspLemongrass pasteOptional.

Finish

1/2 cupRed Boat fish sauce
2LimesJuiced.
1SerranoFinely sliced.

Instructions

  1. Combine water, sugar, ginger paste, smashed garlic, and optional lemongrass paste in a small saucepan.
  2. Bring to a gentle simmer and cook for 5-10 minutes.
  3. Remove from heat and cool completely.
  4. Once cooled, whisk in fish sauce, lime juice, and sliced serrano.
  5. Taste and adjust ginger, lime, or heat as needed.

Chef's Notes

Cooling the syrup before adding fish sauce and lime keeps the finished sauce brighter and fresher.

Use as a dipping sauce, drizzle sauce, marinade component, or bowl finisher.
