Smoky Guajillo-Harissa Chicken over Golden Couscous

Smoky house guajillo-harissa chicken thighs over buttery golden lemon-herb couscous with garlic yogurt-tahini sauce, cucumber-mint salad, Thai chili harissa hot honey, and crispy spiced chickpeas.

Meal TypeComplete Plate
Total2 hr 45 min
Serves2 generous plates

Kitchen Execution Summary

Chicken

Chicken thighs marinated in a smoky guajillo-harissa style paste with garlic, lemon, cumin, coriander, caraway, smoked paprika, and red pepper flakes.

Couscous

Golden lemon-herb couscous made with broth, butter, olive oil, cumin, turmeric or paprika, lemon zest, parsley, and optional mint.

Cooling Sauce

Garlic yogurt-tahini sauce that keeps the heat balanced without dulling the chile flavor.

Fresh + Crunch

Cucumber-mint salad and crispy spiced chickpeas provide freshness, crunch, and 9.5+ plate contrast.

Heat Finish

Thai chili harissa hot honey adds gloss, sweetness, and a controlled final heat spike.

Measured Ingredients by Component

Smoky Guajillo-Harissa Paste

3 dried guajillo chiles, stemmed and seeded; 2 to 3 garlic cloves; 2 Tbsp olive oil; 1 Tbsp lemon juice; 1 tsp lemon zest; 1 tsp cumin; 1 tsp coriander; optional 1/4 tsp caraway; 1/2 tsp smoked paprika; 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes; 1/2 tsp kosher salt; 1 to 3 Tbsp chile soaking water only as needed.

Chicken Marinade

4 chicken thighs; all but 1 Tbsp smoky guajillo-harissa paste; 1 Tbsp olive oil; 1 tsp honey; 1/2 tsp kosher salt; 1/4 tsp black pepper. Reserve 1 Tbsp paste before coating the chicken.

Golden Lemon-Herb Couscous

1 cup couscous; 1 cup chicken broth or water plus bouillon; 1 Tbsp butter; 1 tsp olive oil; 1/4 tsp turmeric or paprika; 1/4 tsp cumin; 1/4 tsp kosher salt; 1/2 tsp lemon zest; 1 to 2 tsp lemon juice; 2 Tbsp parsley; optional 1 Tbsp mint.

Garlic Yogurt-Tahini Sauce

1/2 cup Greek yogurt or sour cream; 1 Tbsp tahini; 1 Tbsp lemon juice; 1/2 tsp lemon zest; 1 small grated garlic clove; 1 tsp olive oil; 1/4 tsp kosher salt; pinch black pepper; 1 to 2 tsp water only if needed; tiny drizzle honey only if too sharp.

Cucumber-Mint Salad

1 1/2 cups diced cucumber; 2 Tbsp parsley; 1 Tbsp mint; optional 1 Tbsp finely diced red onion; 1 tsp lemon juice; 1 tsp olive oil; 1/8 tsp kosher salt; pinch black pepper.

Thai Chili Hot Honey

1 Tbsp reserved guajillo-harissa paste; 1 Tbsp honey; 1 tsp lemon juice; 1/2 tsp olive oil or butter; 1/2 to 1 finely minced red Thai chili; tiny pinch kosher salt.

Crispy Spiced Chickpeas

1 drained 15-oz can chickpeas; 1 Tbsp olive oil; 1/2 tsp cumin; 1/2 tsp smoked paprika; 1/4 tsp coriander; 1/4 tsp garlic powder; 1/4 tsp kosher salt; pinch black pepper; optional pinch red pepper flakes or cayenne; tiny pinch lemon zest after crisping.

Stage-Based Prep Bowls

Paste Bowl

3 soaked guajillos, 2 to 3 garlic cloves, 2 Tbsp olive oil, 1 Tbsp lemon juice, 1 tsp lemon zest, 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp coriander, optional 1/4 tsp caraway, 1/2 tsp smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes, 1/2 tsp salt.

Chicken Bowl

4 chicken thighs, remaining paste after reserving 1 Tbsp, 1 Tbsp olive oil, 1 tsp honey, 1/2 tsp salt, and 1/4 tsp pepper.

Couscous Bowl

1 cup couscous; liquid bowl with 1 cup broth, 1 Tbsp butter, 1 tsp olive oil, 1/4 tsp turmeric or paprika, 1/4 tsp cumin, and 1/4 tsp salt; finish with lemon and herbs.

Cooling Sauce Bowl

1/2 cup yogurt or sour cream, 1 Tbsp tahini, 1 Tbsp lemon juice, 1/2 tsp lemon zest, 1 grated garlic clove, 1 tsp olive oil, 1/4 tsp salt, and pepper.

Fresh Bowl

1 1/2 cups cucumber, 1/8 tsp salt, then finish with 2 Tbsp parsley, 1 Tbsp mint, 1 tsp lemon juice, and 1 tsp olive oil.

Crunch Bowl

1 can chickpeas, 1 Tbsp olive oil, 1/2 tsp cumin, 1/2 tsp smoked paprika, 1/4 tsp coriander, 1/4 tsp garlic powder, 1/4 tsp salt, pepper, and optional chile pinch.

Do Not Forget

Reserve 1 Tbsp guajillo-harissa paste before coating the chicken.

Dry chickpeas thoroughly or they will steam instead of crisp.

Use 1 cup liquid to 1 cup couscous; do not over-wet the couscous.

Keep Thai chili in the hot honey so the plate has controlled heat and balanced contrast.

For hot-running electric skillets, start chicken around 300°F and raise only if browning is too slow.

Full Cooking Timeline and Instructions

  1. Toast and soak guajillos. Stem and seed 3 dried guajillo chiles. Toast 10 to 20 seconds per side, then soak in hot water 10 to 15 minutes.
  2. Blend paste. Blend softened chiles with 2 to 3 garlic cloves, 2 Tbsp olive oil, 1 Tbsp lemon juice, 1 tsp lemon zest, 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp coriander, optional 1/4 tsp caraway, 1/2 tsp smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes, 1/2 tsp salt, and just enough soaking water to make a thick paste.
  3. Marinate chicken. Reserve 1 Tbsp paste. Coat 4 chicken thighs with remaining paste, 1 Tbsp olive oil, 1 tsp honey, 1/2 tsp salt, and 1/4 tsp pepper. Marinate at least 1 hour.
  4. Crisp chickpeas. Heat oven or air fryer to 400°F. Pat 1 can chickpeas very dry. Toss with 1 Tbsp olive oil, 1/2 tsp cumin, 1/2 tsp smoked paprika, 1/4 tsp coriander, 1/4 tsp garlic powder, 1/4 tsp salt, pepper, and optional chile pinch. Cook 20 to 30 minutes until crisp. Finish with lemon zest.
  5. Make sauce. Whisk 1/2 cup yogurt or sour cream, 1 Tbsp tahini, 1 Tbsp lemon juice, 1/2 tsp lemon zest, 1 grated garlic clove, 1 tsp olive oil, 1/4 tsp salt, and pepper. Thin only if needed.
  6. Make salad. Salt 1 1/2 cups cucumber with 1/8 tsp salt, rest 5 to 10 minutes, then drain. Finish with 2 Tbsp parsley, 1 Tbsp mint, optional onion, 1 tsp lemon juice, 1 tsp olive oil, and pepper.
  7. Make hot honey. Stir 1 Tbsp reserved paste, 1 Tbsp honey, 1 tsp lemon juice, 1/2 tsp olive oil or butter, 1/2 to 1 minced Thai chili, and a tiny pinch salt.
  8. Cook chicken. Heat skillet to about 300°F. Cook chicken until deeply browned and cooked through, about 5 to 7 minutes per side for boneless thighs, longer for bone-in. Raise toward 325°F only if browning is slow. Rest 5 minutes.
  9. Cook couscous. Bring 1 cup broth, 1 Tbsp butter, 1 tsp olive oil, 1/4 tsp turmeric or paprika, 1/4 tsp cumin, and 1/4 tsp salt to a boil. Stir in 1 cup couscous, cover, remove from heat, and rest 5 minutes. Fluff with 1/2 tsp lemon zest, 1 to 2 tsp lemon juice, 2 Tbsp parsley, and optional 1 Tbsp mint.
  10. Build plates. Divide couscous between 2 plates. Add chicken, sauce, cucumber salad, crispy chickpeas, herbs, lemon wedge, and a controlled drizzle of Thai chili hot honey.

Final Taste Target

The finished plate should taste smoky, spicy, juicy, lemon-bright, buttery, creamy, herbaceous, and crisp. The chicken carries deep guajillo body and harissa-style spice; the Thai chili hot honey hits as a glossy final heat spike; the couscous stays fluffy; the sauce cools; the salad refreshes; and the chickpeas add crunch.
