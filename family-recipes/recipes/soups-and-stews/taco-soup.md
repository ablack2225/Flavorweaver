# Taco Soup

> A preserved family taco soup made with ground beef, ranch dressing mix, taco seasoning, beans, corn, tomatoes, green chilies, and beef bouillon.

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Brown ground beef with chopped onion. |
| 2 | Drain off excess fat. |
| 3 | Add all remaining ingredients. |
| 4 | Mix well. |
| 5 | Simmer, covered, for about 45 minutes. |

## Notes

Original family note: spicy and so delicious on a cold winter's day.

Sue noted that regular ranch-style beans may be used if desired, but the family liked the jalapeños in theirs.

The printed recipe lists 6-8 servings and 1 hour prep time. Since the soup simmers for about 45 minutes, the printed prep time appears to function as total time. Active prep time is estimated. Can sizes are not specified in the original recipe.
