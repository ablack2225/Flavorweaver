# Pecan Pie

> A preserved family pecan pie recipe made with dark Karo syrup, sugar, flour, eggs, evaporated milk, vanilla, and pecans in an 8-inch unbaked pie crust.

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 375°F. |
| 2 | Combine sugar, salt, and flour. |
| 3 | Beat in corn syrup, eggs, evaporated milk, and vanilla. |
| 4 | Blend in pecans. |
| 5 | Pour filling into an 8-inch unbaked pie crust. |
| 6 | Bake in a preheated 375°F oven for about 50 minutes, or until pie is set. |
| 7 | Cool before cutting. |

## Notes

Jimmie and Sue were both known in the family for their pies. This pecan pie is preserved as part of that family pie tradition.

This is the traditional pecan pie version preserved alongside the Chocolate Pecan Pie variation.

The printed recipe lists 1 hour 15 minutes prep time. Since the bake time is about 50 minutes, the printed prep time appears to function more like total time. Servings and active prep time are estimated.
