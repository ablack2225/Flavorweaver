# Coconut-Pecan Frosting

> A preserved handwritten frosting recipe from Sue Wallesch, made with evaporated milk, egg yolks, sugar, margarine, flaked coconut, and chopped pecans.

## Measured Ingredients

### Coconut-Pecan Frosting

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 cup | Evaporated milk |  |
| 1 cup | Sugar |  |
| 3 | Egg yolks | Beaten |
| 1/2 cup | Margarine |  |
| 1 tsp | Vanilla |  |
| 1 1/3 cups | Flaked coconut |  |
| 1 cup | Chopped pecans |  |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Combine evaporated milk, beaten egg yolks, sugar, and margarine. |
| 2 | Cook over medium heat until thickened, about 12 minutes. |
| 3 | Add vanilla. |
| 4 | Add flaked coconut and chopped pecans. |
| 5 | Cool until thick enough to spread. |
