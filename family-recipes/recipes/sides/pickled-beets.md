# Pickled Beets

> A preserved family side dish made with whole or sliced beets warmed in a sweet vinegar spice mixture and marinated for several days before serving.

## Ingredient List

| Component | Ingredients |
|---|---|
| Pickling Mixture | Sugar, water, vinegar, ground cloves, ground allspice, ground cinnamon |
| Beets | Several cans of whole or sliced beets |

## Measured Ingredients

### Pickled Beets

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 2 cups | Sugar |  |
| 2 cups | Water |  |
| 2 cups | Vinegar |  |
| 1 tsp | Ground cloves |  |
| 1 tsp | Ground allspice |  |
| 1 Tbsp | Ground cinnamon |  |
| Several cans | Whole or sliced beets | Open and drain off liquid before adding to pickling mixture |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | In a large saucepan, combine all ingredients except the beets. |
| 2 | Bring sugar-vinegar mixture to a boil. |
| 3 | Open beets and drain off the liquid. |
| 4 | Add beets to the boiling mixture. |
| 5 | Heat thoroughly. |
| 6 | Store beets in a jar or bowl in the refrigerator. |
| 7 | Marinate several days before serving. |

<!-- recipe-card:end -->

## Notes

Original longer-storage option: the heated beets may be placed into clean, hot jars and sealed for longer storage.

Original refrigerator option: the beets may be stored in a jar or bowl in the refrigerator and should marinate several days before serving.

Original reuse note: the sugar-vinegar mixture may be reused by adding more beets to it and storing it in the refrigerator. The beets keep for a long time when stored in the refrigerator.
