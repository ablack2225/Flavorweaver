# Turtle Cake

> A preserved family turtle cake made with German chocolate cake mix, caramel, sweetened condensed milk, and chopped pecans layered through the center.

## Measured Ingredients

### Turtle Cake

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 box | German chocolate cake mix |  |
| 1 cup | Water |  |
| 1/2 cup | Cooking oil |  |
| 1 stick | Margarine |  |
| 3 | Eggs |  |
| 1 can | Sweetened condensed milk | Use half in batter and half in caramel center |
| 1 cup | Chopped pecans |  |
| 1 lb | Caramels | Unwrapped and melted with remaining condensed milk |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 325°F. |
| 2 | Beat together cake mix, water, oil, eggs, margarine, and half of the sweetened condensed milk. |
| 3 | Reserve the other half of the sweetened condensed milk for the center mixture. |
| 4 | Pour half of the batter into a greased and floured 13x9x2-inch baking pan. |
| 5 | Bake for about 20 minutes. |
| 6 | While the first layer is baking, unwrap the caramels and mix them with the remaining sweetened condensed milk in a microwave-safe bowl. |
| 7 | Microwave on high until all caramels are melted. |
| 8 | Stir in the chopped pecans. |
| 9 | Pour the caramel-pecan mixture over the baked first layer. |
| 10 | Pour remaining cake batter over the caramel layer. |
| 11 | Bake for another 25-30 minutes, or until done in the middle. |
| 12 | Frost if desired, or serve as is. |
