## Measured Ingredients

### Banana Date Bars

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 3 medium | Ripe bananas | Peeled; purée 1 banana and dice remaining 2 bananas |
| 1/2 cup | Butter |  |
| 1 cup | Brown sugar | Packed |
| 2 | Eggs |  |
| 2 cups | Flour |  |
| 1 tsp | Baking soda |  |
| 1 tsp | Ground cinnamon |  |
| 1/2 tsp | Baking powder |  |
| 1/2 tsp | Ground nutmeg |  |
| 1/4 tsp | Salt |  |
| 1 cup | Chopped dates |  |
| 1 cup | Chopped walnuts |  |
| As desired | Powdered sugar | For sprinkling after cooling |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 350°F. |
| 2 | Purée 1 banana in a blender to make about 1/2 cup. |
| 3 | Dice the remaining 2 bananas. |
| 4 | Cream butter with brown sugar until light and fluffy. |
| 5 | Beat in eggs. |
| 6 | Beat in puréed banana. |
| 7 | Combine dry ingredients. |
| 8 | Beat dry ingredients into creamed mixture until well blended. |
| 9 | Fold in chopped dates, chopped walnuts, and diced bananas. |
| 10 | Turn batter into a greased 13x9-inch baking pan. |
| 11 | Bake at 350°F for 25 minutes. |
| 12 | Cool in pan. |
| 13 | Sprinkle with powdered sugar. |
| 14 | Cut into bars. |
