## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 350°F. |
| 2 | Mix margarine and cocoa together and warm in microwave. |
| 3 | Mix sugar and flour with canned milk and vanilla. |
| 4 | Add eggs and beat well. |
| 5 | Blend in cocoa mixture. |
| 6 | Pour into unbaked pie crust. |
| 7 | Bake for 1 hour at 350°F. |
