Premium Crab Rangoon

Crispy fried wontons filled with cream cheese, crab, green onion, savory seasonings, and a small touch of powdered sugar, served with homemade sweet and sour sauce.

StatusWorking Recipe
Total45 min, plus chilling
YieldAbout 24
DifficultyModerate

Measured Ingredients

Crab Rangoon Filling

8 oz softened cream cheese; 4 to 6 oz drained crab meat; 2 minced green onions; 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce; 1/2 tsp soy sauce; 1/2 tsp garlic powder; 1/4 tsp onion powder; pinch black pepper; 2 tsp powdered sugar.

Wrappers and Frying

About 24 wonton wrappers, water for sealing, and neutral oil for frying at 350°F.

Homemade Sweet & Sour Sauce

1/4 cup tomato paste; 1/3 cup pineapple juice; 2 Tbsp rice vinegar; 2 Tbsp brown sugar; 1 Tbsp honey; 1 Tbsp orange juice; 1 tsp soy sauce; pinch ginger. Slurry: 1 tsp cornstarch plus 1 Tbsp cold water.

Do Not Forget

Drain crab well. Chill the filling. Do not overfill wontons. Seal edges firmly with water. Fry at 350°F until golden.

The sweet and sour sauce belongs with this recipe card.

No nutrition panel is needed.

Full Cooking Timeline and Instructions

  1. Mix filling ingredients until combined, then chill.
  2. Simmer sweet and sour sauce ingredients until smooth.
  3. Add cornstarch slurry and cook until glossy.
  4. Fill wonton wrappers with a small spoonful of filling and seal firmly.
  5. Fry at 350°F until golden and crisp.
  6. Serve hot with homemade sweet and sour sauce.

Final Taste Target

Crisp, creamy, lightly sweet, savory, rich, and balanced by glossy tangy sweet and sour sauce.
