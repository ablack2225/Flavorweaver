Family Cookbook · Preservation · Memory

For Sue, and for the recipes that found their way home

This cookbook began in 2000 as a birthday gift for Sue.

In 2026, this archive was expanded and preserved in honor of Sue’s memory after discovering a treasure trove of recipes left behind after Mom/Goga’s passing. Some were handwritten on sticky notes, some clipped from boxes or magazines, some shared by church friends, and some still carried the marks of years spent in a well-loved kitchen.

Together, they tell the story of family, friendship, faith, food, and the hands that kept everyone fed.
