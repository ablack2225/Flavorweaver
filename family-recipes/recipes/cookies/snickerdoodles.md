# Snickerdoodles

> A preserved family snickerdoodle recipe made with margarine, shortening, cream of tartar, and a cinnamon-sugar coating.

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 400°F. |
| 2 | Thoroughly mix margarine, shortening, sugar, and eggs. |
| 3 | Blend in flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt. Mix well until all dry ingredients are moistened. |
| 4 | Shape dough by rounded teaspoonfuls into balls. |
| 5 | Mix 2 Tbsp sugar and 2 tsp cinnamon in a small bowl. |
| 6 | Roll the balls in the cinnamon-sugar mixture. |
| 7 | Place cookies 2 inches apart on an ungreased cookie sheet. |
| 8 | Bake 8-10 minutes, or until set. |
| 9 | Immediately remove from cookie sheet to a sheet of foil to cool. |
