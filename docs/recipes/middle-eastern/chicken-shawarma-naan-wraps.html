Chicken Shawarma Naan Wraps with Garlic Sauce and Lemon Tahini

Deeply spiced chicken thighs cooked hot, sliced thin, and re-crisped into shawarma-style edges, wrapped in warm homemade naan with garlic sauce, lemon tahini, crisp potatoes, pickles, tomato, cucumber, onion, and herbs.

Before Cooking / Advance Prep

Marinade timing: marinate the chicken 12 to 18 hours before cooking. Do not extend the lemon-garlic marinade beyond 24 hours.

Dry spice blend: the shawarma spice blend can be mixed 1 to 2 days ahead and kept covered.

Sauces: garlic sauce and lemon tahini can be made up to 1 day ahead and refrigerated.

Naan: if making house naan, the dough can be refrigerated after the first rise, then brought to room temperature before shaping and cooking.

Measured Ingredients

Shawarma Chicken Marinade

1 lb chicken thighs; 2 Tbsp oil; 1 Tbsp lemon juice; 1 tsp lemon zest; 4 grated garlic cloves; 1 tsp kosher salt; 2 tsp cumin; 1 tsp coriander; 1 tsp smoked paprika; 1/2 tsp turmeric; 1/2 tsp allspice; 1/4 tsp cardamom; 1/4 tsp cinnamon; 1/4 tsp ginger; tiny pinch nutmeg; 1/2 tsp black pepper; 1/8 to 1/4 tsp cayenne; 1 tsp honey or brown sugar; optional 1 Tbsp Greek yogurt.

Toum-Style Garlic Sauce

1/4 cup mayonnaise; 2 Tbsp Greek yogurt or sour cream; 1 to 2 grated garlic cloves; 1 to 2 tsp lemon juice; 1 tsp olive oil; pinch kosher salt; tiny pinch sweetener; 1 to 2 tsp cold water only if needed.

Lemon Tahini

1/4 cup tahini; 1 1/2 Tbsp lemon juice; 1 small grated garlic clove; 1/4 tsp kosher salt; 1/8 tsp cumin; 3 to 5 Tbsp ice-cold water; tiny drizzle honey only if bitter.

Wrap Fill

3 to 4 warm naan; 2 medium potatoes or frozen fries; 1 tomato; 1/2 cucumber; 1/4 to 1/2 small onion; 1/2 cup pickles or pickled onions; small handful herbs; optional lemon wedges.

Stage-Based Prep Bowls

Bowl 1 — Chicken

1 lb chicken thighs, left whole.

Bowl 2 — Wet Base

2 Tbsp oil; 1 Tbsp lemon juice; 1 tsp lemon zest; 4 grated garlic cloves; 1 tsp honey or brown sugar; optional 1 Tbsp Greek yogurt.

Bowl 3 — Spice Blend

1 tsp salt; 2 tsp cumin; 1 tsp coriander; 1 tsp smoked paprika; 1/2 tsp turmeric; 1/2 tsp allspice; 1/4 tsp cardamom; 1/4 tsp cinnamon; 1/4 tsp ginger; tiny pinch nutmeg; 1/2 tsp black pepper; 1/8 to 1/4 tsp cayenne.

Bowl 4 — Re-Crisp Chicken

Cooked rested chicken, sliced thin, plus saved pan juices.

Bowl 5 — Garlic Sauce

1/4 cup mayo; 2 Tbsp yogurt or sour cream; 1 to 2 garlic cloves; 1 to 2 tsp lemon juice; 1 tsp olive oil; salt; tiny sweetener.

Bowl 6 — Lemon Tahini

1/4 cup tahini; 1 1/2 Tbsp lemon juice; 1 small garlic clove; 1/4 tsp salt; 1/8 tsp cumin; 3 to 5 Tbsp ice water.

Bowl 7 — Required Naan

3 to 4 warm naan, preferably Fluffy Skillet Naan.

Bowl 8 — Crispy Potatoes

2 medium potatoes; 1 to 2 Tbsp oil; 1/2 tsp salt; optional 1/2 tsp paprika; optional 1/4 tsp garlic powder.

Bowl 9 — Fresh Fill

1 tomato; 1/2 cucumber; 1/4 to 1/2 onion; herbs.

Bowl 10 — Pickled Finish

1/2 cup pickles or pickled onions, drained well.

Do Not Forget

Marinate 12 to 18 hours before cooking. Keep lemon juice to 1 Tbsp. Fluffy Skillet Naan is the required house bread component unless using prepared naan. Cook thighs whole, rest, slice thin, then re-crisp. Garlic sauce is primary; tahini is a thin stripe. Drain pickles and wet toppings.

Full Cooking Timeline and Instructions

  1. Mix the dry shawarma spice blend. In a small bowl, combine 1 tsp kosher salt, 2 tsp ground cumin, 1 tsp ground coriander, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1/2 tsp allspice, 1/4 tsp ground cardamom, 1/4 tsp cinnamon, 1/4 tsp ground ginger, a tiny pinch nutmeg, 1/2 tsp black pepper, and 1/8 to 1/4 tsp cayenne. Cover and hold until ready to marinate.
  2. Make the chicken marinade. In a medium bowl, whisk 2 Tbsp olive oil or neutral oil, 1 Tbsp lemon juice, 1 tsp lemon zest, 4 grated garlic cloves, 1 tsp honey or brown sugar, and optional 1 Tbsp Greek yogurt. Add the full dry shawarma spice blend and whisk until thick and fragrant.
  3. Marinate the chicken thighs. Add 1 lb boneless skinless chicken thighs to the marinade and turn until every piece is coated. Keep the thighs whole for cooking. Cover and refrigerate 12 to 18 hours.
  4. Make the toum-style garlic sauce. Stir together 1/4 cup mayonnaise, 2 Tbsp Greek yogurt or sour cream, 1 to 2 grated garlic cloves, 1 to 2 tsp lemon juice, 1 tsp olive oil, a pinch kosher salt, and a tiny pinch sugar or honey. Thin with 1 to 2 tsp cold water, adding only 1 tsp at a time if needed. Chill until service.
  5. Make the lemon tahini sauce. Whisk 1/4 cup tahini, 1 1/2 Tbsp lemon juice, 1 small grated garlic clove, 1/4 tsp kosher salt, and 1/8 tsp ground cumin. Whisk in 3 to 5 Tbsp ice-cold water, adding 1 Tbsp at a time, until pale and pourable.
  6. Prepare the required naan component. Make or warm 3 to 4 naan. For the house version, use Fluffy Skillet Naan. Keep cooked naan covered and warm so it stays flexible.
  7. Prepare the crispy potato tuck-in. Cut 2 medium potatoes into 1/4-inch fries or small wedges, or use frozen fries. Toss fresh potatoes with 1 to 2 Tbsp neutral oil, 1/2 tsp kosher salt, optional 1/2 tsp paprika, and optional 1/4 tsp garlic powder. Roast, air-fry, or fry until crisp. Hold warm.
  8. Prep the fresh and pickled wrap fill. Slice 1 medium tomato, 1/2 cucumber, and 1/4 to 1/2 small onion. Chop a small handful of parsley or cilantro if using. Drain 1/2 cup pickles or pickled onions very well.
  9. Cook the chicken thighs hot. Remove marinated chicken from the refrigerator 20 to 30 minutes before cooking. Cook whole thighs in a hot skillet, griddle, broiler, grill pan, or electric skillet until browned at the edges and cooked through, about 4 to 6 minutes per side.
  10. Rest and slice the chicken. Rest cooked chicken 5 to 10 minutes. Slice very thin across the grain into shawarma-style strips. Save the resting juices.
  11. Re-crisp the sliced chicken. Return sliced chicken and saved juices to a hot skillet for 1 to 2 minutes, tossing just until the edges pick up color and the spices smell toasted again.
  12. Warm the naan for assembly. Warm 3 to 4 naan until soft and flexible. Do not fill cold naan.
  13. Build each wrap with measured components. For each warm naan, spread about 1 Tbsp garlic sauce, add 1/3 to 1/4 of the hot sliced chicken, about 1/4 cup crispy potatoes, 1 to 2 Tbsp drained pickles or pickled onions, tomato, cucumber, onion, herbs, another 1 to 2 tsp garlic sauce, and a thin 1 to 2 tsp stripe lemon tahini.
  14. Set the wrap and serve. Fold or roll the naan. Optional: griddle seam-side down for 30 to 60 seconds so the wrap holds together. Serve immediately.

Building the Final Wrap

Use 1 warm naan per wrap. Layer 1 Tbsp garlic sauce, 1/3 to 1/4 of the hot sliced chicken, about 1/4 cup crispy potatoes, 1 to 2 Tbsp drained pickles or pickled onions, tomato, cucumber, onion, herbs, 1 to 2 tsp garlic sauce, and a thin 1 to 2 tsp lemon tahini stripe.

Final Taste Target

Juicy, deeply spiced, garlicky, lemon-bright, tangy from pickles, creamy from sauce, nutty from a thin tahini stripe, and fresh from vegetables and herbs. Not soggy, tahini-heavy, or yogurt-dominant.

Chef's Notes

Tahini is useful here, but it should not replace the garlic sauce. For chicken shawarma, the garlic sauce is the main sauce; lemon tahini is a nutty finishing stripe.

For best results, marinate the chicken 12 to 18 hours before cooking. The dry spice blend and sauces can be made earlier, but the lemon-garlic chicken marinade should not sit on the chicken for more than 24 hours.
