# Green Bean Casserole

> A preserved family side dish made with cut green beans, cream of mushroom soup, milk, black pepper, French fried onions, and an optional touch of soy sauce.

## Measured Ingredients

### Green Bean Casserole

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 1/3 cups | French fried onions | Divided |
| 1 can | Cream of mushroom soup |  |
| 2 (14.5 oz) cans | Cut green beans |  |
| 3/4 cup | Milk |  |
| 1/8 tsp | Black pepper |  |
| 1 tsp | Soy sauce | Optional family-practice addition |
| As desired | Grated cheddar cheese | Optional; add with final onion topping |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 350°F. |
| 2 | Combine cream of mushroom soup, milk, black pepper, and optional soy sauce; mix well. |
| 3 | Add green beans and 2/3 cup French fried onions. |
| 4 | Pour mixture into a lightly greased 1 1/2-quart casserole dish. |
| 5 | Bake in a preheated 350°F oven for 30 minutes. |
| 6 | Remove from oven and stir. |
| 7 | Sprinkle with remaining 2/3 cup French fried onions. |
| 8 | Add grated cheddar cheese on top with the onions, if desired. |
| 9 | Bake 5 minutes longer, until onions are golden. |
