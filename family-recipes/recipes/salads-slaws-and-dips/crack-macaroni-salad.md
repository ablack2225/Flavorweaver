# Crack Macaroni Salad

> A preserved family macaroni salad made with ranch-style dressing mix, purple onion, black olives, and enough mayonnaise to make a creamy chilled salad.

## Measured Ingredients

### Crack Macaroni Salad

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 10-12 oz bag | Macaroni | Cook according to package directions |
| 1 package | Ranch-style dressing mix |  |
| 1/2 cup | Purple onion | Chopped |
| 1/2 cup | Black olives | Sliced |
| As needed | Mayonnaise | Enough to make a good creamy mixture |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Cook macaroni according to package directions. |
| 2 | Drain macaroni and rinse with cold water. |
| 3 | Place macaroni in a large mixing bowl. |
| 4 | Add ranch dressing mix and stir until macaroni is coated with the mixture. |
| 5 | Stir in chopped purple onion and sliced black olives. |
| 6 | Blend in enough mayonnaise to make a good creamy mixture. |
| 7 | Cover and refrigerate for several hours before serving. |
