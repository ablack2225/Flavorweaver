## Measured Ingredients

### Hamburger Stroganoff

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 lb | Ground beef |  |
| 1 medium | Onion | Chopped |
| 1/4 cup | Margarine |  |
| 2 Tbsp | Flour |  |
| 1 tsp | Salt |  |
| 1 tsp | Garlic powder |  |
| 1/4 tsp | Black pepper |  |
| 1 can | Cream of mushroom soup |  |
| 1 cup | Sour cream | Stir in at the end and heat through |
| 2 cups | Hot cooked noodles | Cook according to package directions |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | In a large skillet, cook and stir ground beef and onion until meat is lightly browned and onion is tender. |
| 2 | Drain off excess fat. |
| 3 | Add margarine and melt into the meat mixture. |
| 4 | Stir in flour, salt, and garlic powder. |
| 5 | Cook about 5 minutes, stirring often. |
| 6 | Stir in cream of mushroom soup. |
| 7 | Simmer uncovered about 10 minutes. |
| 8 | Stir in sour cream and heat through. |
| 9 | Add noodles that have been cooked according to package directions. |
| 10 | Serve hot. |
