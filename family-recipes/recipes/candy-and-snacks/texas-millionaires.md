# Texas Millionaires

## Measured Ingredients

### Texas Millionaires

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 large bag | Caramels | About 14 oz; unwrapped |
| 1 1/2 Tbsp | Milk |  |
| 2 cups | Pecans | Coarsely chopped |
| 12 oz | Chocolate-flavored candy coating | Melt for dipping |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Unwrap caramels. |
| 2 | Combine caramels and milk in a saucepan. |
| 3 | Cook over low heat until caramels are melted, stirring frequently. |
| 4 | Stir in chopped pecans. |
| 5 | Drop by rounded teaspoonfuls onto a sheet of buttered wax paper. |
| 6 | Cool, then cover and chill until caramel balls are thoroughly chilled. |
| 7 | Place candy coating in the top of a double boiler and bring water to a boil. |
| 8 | Reduce heat to low and cook until coating melts. |
| 9 | Dip the caramel balls into the coating. |
| 10 | Place on a sheet of wax paper to cool. |
