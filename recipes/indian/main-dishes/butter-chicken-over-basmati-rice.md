# Butter Chicken over Basmati Rice

> Butter chicken served over basmati rice with a creamy tomato-cashew sauce, marinated chicken, and a butter-based finish.

## Cooking Structure Summary

| Stage | Purpose |
|---|---|
| First marinade | Seasons chicken with chili, salt, and lemon before the yogurt marinade. |
| Second marinade | Adds yogurt, ginger-garlic, spices, kasuri methi, and oil for deeper flavor. |
| Tomato-cashew sauce | Builds a creamy tomato sauce with cashews, butter, spices, and cream. |
| Chicken cook | Chicken is cooked separately before finishing in the sauce. |
| Final finish | Cream and kasuri methi round the sauce at the end. |
| Service | Serve over hot basmati rice. |

## Flavorweaver Cook Notes

- Use butter only; do not use ghee.
- Use about **1.3 lb fresh tomatoes** rather than gram-based measurement.
- Use cashews for the sauce base.
- Serve over basmati rice.

## Do Not Forget

- Do not use ghee.
- Do not swap cashews for almonds unless intentionally testing a new version.
- Keep the basmati rice service note.
