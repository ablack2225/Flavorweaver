# Burrito Casserole

> A preserved family main-dish casserole layered with seasoned ground beef, Ranch Style Beans, enchilada sauce, tomatoes and green chilies, chicken and rice soup, torn flour tortillas, sour cream, and cheddar cheese.

## Measured Ingredients

### Burrito Casserole

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 lb | Ground beef |  |
| 1 large | Onion | Chopped |
| 1 Tbsp | Garlic powder |  |
| 1 can | Ranch Style Beans |  |
| 1 (10 oz) can | Enchilada sauce |  |
| 1 can | Tomatoes and green chilies |  |
| 1 can | Chicken and rice soup |  |
| 6 | Regular-size flour tortillas | Torn into small pieces for layering |
| 2 cups | Cheddar cheese | Grated; divided |
| 1 cup | Sour cream | Divided |
| 2 | Beef bouillon cubes |  |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 350°F. |
| 2 | Brown ground beef and onion. |
| 3 | Drain excess fat. |
| 4 | Add all ingredients except tortillas, cheese, and sour cream. |
| 5 | Let mixture simmer about 10 minutes. |
| 6 | Place half of the meat mixture in a large casserole dish. |
| 7 | Top with 3 flour tortillas torn into small pieces. |
| 8 | Add half of the sour cream and half of the grated cheese. |
| 9 | Top with remaining meat mixture. |
| 10 | Cover with remaining flour tortillas and sour cream. |
| 11 | Top with the rest of the cheese. |
| 12 | Bake at 350°F for about 30 minutes. |
| 13 | Serve hot. |
