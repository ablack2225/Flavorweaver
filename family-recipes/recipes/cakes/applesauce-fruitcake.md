## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 325°F. |
| 2 | Cream butter and sugar together. |
| 3 | Add eggs and vanilla and beat well. |
| 4 | Mix a small amount of flour with the fruit to help keep it from clumping or sinking. |
| 5 | Add flour and mix well. |
| 6 | Stir in dates, raisins, nuts, applesauce, cinnamon, cloves, and soda. |
| 7 | Add candied fruit, if desired. |
| 8 | Bake at 325°F until done. |

The handwritten card does not specify pan size, exact bake time, box sizes for dates or raisins, or serving yield. Those details are intentionally left flexible or estimated rather than invented.
