# Earthquake Cake

> A preserved family cake made with German chocolate cake mix, coconut, pecans, and cream cheese filling dropped in dollops over the batter before baking.

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 350°F. |
| 2 | Spread coconut in the bottom of a well-greased 13x9x2-inch baking pan. |
| 3 | Sprinkle pecans over the coconut. |
| 4 | Prepare cake mix as directed on the box. |
| 5 | Pour cake batter over the coconut and pecans. |
| 6 | Mix softened margarine, softened cream cheese, confectioners' sugar, and vanilla together. Beat until smooth. |
| 7 | Drop the cream cheese mixture by spoonfuls in dollops onto the top of the cake batter. Do not stir. |
| 8 | Bake for 45-50 minutes. |

## Notes

The cream cheese mixture is dropped onto the cake batter in dollops and not stirred in. This creates the earthquake-style texture as the cake bakes.

Servings, prep time, cooling time, and total time are estimated from the pan size and method. The original recipe page listed prep time as 1 hour and left servings blank.
