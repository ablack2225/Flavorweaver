Amy Kitchen Changes

Butter Only

Use butter only. We do not use ghee.

Tomatoes

Use practical pound measurement: about 1.3 lb fresh tomatoes. Remove gram measurements.

Cashew Base

Use cashews. Do not substitute almonds unless Amy specifically tests that later.

Basmati Service

Serve over basmati rice. This was the tested version and was excellent.

Cooking Structure Summary

This dish uses the source recipe's general butter chicken structure without reproducing the full published recipe.

First Marinade

Seasons chicken with chili, salt, and lemon before the yogurt marinade.

Second Marinade

Adds yogurt, ginger-garlic, spices, kasuri methi, and oil for deeper flavor.

Tomato-Cashew Sauce

Builds a creamy tomato sauce with cashews, butter, spices, and cream.

Finish & Serve

Chicken finishes in the sauce, cream and kasuri methi round it out, and the tested service is hot basmati rice.
