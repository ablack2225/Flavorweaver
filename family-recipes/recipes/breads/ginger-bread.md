# Ginger Bread

> A preserved family gingerbread-style sweet bread made with molasses, hot water, cloves, ginger, cinnamon, and baking powder.

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 350°F. |
| 2 | Cream margarine and sugar together. |
| 3 | Add egg and molasses; mix well. |
| 4 | Add dry ingredients, alternating with the hot water, until all is added. |
| 5 | Mix until smooth. |
| 6 | Pour into a greased 8x8x2-inch square pan. |
| 7 | Bake for 35-40 minutes, or until done. |
