## Cabbage Planning Note

Green cabbage is preferred for stews, cabbage sautés, Atakilt Wat-style vegetables, Southern-style sides, and sturdy cooked dishes.

Napa cabbage is better for Asian bowls, stir-fries, spring rolls, and lighter cooked or raw applications.

Red cabbage is useful for slaws, pickles, and color, but should not be used as a direct substitute in dishes where color bleeding would be a problem.

## Cucumber Note

Mini cucumbers are excellent for salads because they have tender skin, good crunch, and less watery texture.

For cucumber salads, use:

- 2 to 3 mini cucumbers in place of 1/2 large cucumber
- No peeling needed unless the skin is tough or bitter
