# Cabbage Wedges with Cheese

> A preserved family side dish made with tender cabbage wedges, a bacon-dripping cheddar cheese sauce, and crumbled bacon on top.

## Measured Ingredients

### Cabbage Wedges with Cheese

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 medium head | Green cabbage | Cut into 6 wedges |
| 6 slices | Bacon | Fried and crumbled |
| 2 Tbsp | Bacon drippings | Reserved for sauce |
| 1 Tbsp | Flour |  |
| 1/2 tsp | Salt | Plus salted water for cooking cabbage |
| 1/8 tsp | Pepper |  |
| Few grains | Cayenne pepper |  |
| 3/4 cup | Evaporated milk |  |
| 1/4 cup | Water |  |
| 3/4 cup | Cheddar cheese | Grated |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Remove wilted outside leaves from cabbage. |
| 2 | Cut cabbage into 6 wedges. |
| 3 | Place cabbage wedges in a large pan with 1 inch salted water. |
| 4 | Cover and heat to boiling. |
| 5 | Reduce heat and simmer 12-15 minutes, until cabbage is tender. |
| 6 | Fry bacon, drain fat, and reserve 2 Tbsp bacon drippings for the sauce. Crumble bacon and set aside. |
| 7 | In a small saucepan, mix bacon drippings with flour, salt, pepper, and cayenne until well blended. |
| 8 | Add evaporated milk and water. |
| 9 | Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until mixture begins to thicken. |
| 10 | Add grated cheddar cheese and stir until melted. |
| 11 | Drain cabbage and place wedges on a platter. |
| 12 | Pour cheese sauce over cabbage. |
| 13 | Top with crumbled bacon pieces. |
