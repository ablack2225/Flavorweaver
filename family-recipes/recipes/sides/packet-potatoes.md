# Packet Potatoes

> A preserved family side dish made with red potatoes and onions baked in a sealed foil packet with olive oil, seasoned salt, and black pepper.

## Measured Ingredients

### Packet Potatoes

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 | Onion | Thinly sliced |
| 4 medium | Red potatoes | Washed and cut into small cubes |
| 2 Tbsp | Olive oil | Drizzled over potatoes |
| 1 tsp | Seasoned salt |  |
| 1/4 tsp | Black pepper |  |
| As needed | Non-stick cooking spray | For foil |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 450°F. |
| 2 | Spray a large piece of foil with non-stick cooking spray. |
| 3 | Place sliced onions in the center of the foil. |
| 4 | Top onions with washed, cubed red potatoes. |
| 5 | Drizzle with olive oil. |
| 6 | Sprinkle with seasoned salt and black pepper. |
| 7 | Bring up foil sides. |
| 8 | Double fold the top and ends to seal the packet, leaving room for heat circulation inside. |
| 9 | Place packet on a cookie sheet. |
| 10 | Bake for 30-35 minutes. |
| 11 | Carefully open foil and serve. |
