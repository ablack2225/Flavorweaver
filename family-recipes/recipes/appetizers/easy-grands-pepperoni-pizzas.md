> A preserved recipe clipping for quick mini pepperoni pizzas made with refrigerated biscuits, pizza sauce, cheese, and pepperoni.

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Heat oven to 375°F. |
| 2 | Press each biscuit into a 6-inch round. |
| 3 | Place biscuit rounds on greased cookie sheets. |
| 4 | Top each round with pizza sauce, cheese, and pepperoni. |
| 5 | Bake for 10-15 minutes. |
