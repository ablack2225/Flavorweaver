# Southwestern Chicken Skillet

## Measured Ingredients

### Southwestern Chicken Skillet

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 lb | Boneless skinless chicken breasts | Cut into bite-sized pieces |
| 2 cups / 8 oz | Elbow macaroni | Uncooked |
| 1/2 lb / 8 oz | Velveeta | Cut up |
| 1 (10 oz) can | RO*TEL diced tomatoes and green chilies | Undrained |
| 1/2 cup | Sour cream | Breakstone's or Knudsen style, as listed on clipping |
| 2 Tbsp | Fresh parsley | Chopped; optional |
| 2 cups | Water | Added before macaroni |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Cook chicken in a large nonstick skillet on medium-high heat for 5-7 minutes, or until cooked through, stirring occasionally. |
| 2 | Stir in water and bring to a boil. |
| 3 | Stir in uncooked macaroni. |
| 4 | Cover skillet. |
| 5 | Reduce heat to medium. |
| 6 | Simmer for 15 minutes, or until water is almost absorbed. |
| 7 | Add Velveeta and undrained tomatoes. |
| 8 | Cook until Velveeta is melted, stirring frequently. |
| 9 | Remove from heat. |
| 10 | Stir in sour cream. |
| 11 | Sprinkle with parsley, if desired. |
