## Flavor / Ingredient Notes

Kecap manis is a thick Indonesian-style sweet soy sauce. It relies on sugar, commonly palm sugar, for its signature syrupy texture and deep sweetness.

Compared with thin soy sauce, sweet soy sauce is much higher in sugar and carbohydrates.
