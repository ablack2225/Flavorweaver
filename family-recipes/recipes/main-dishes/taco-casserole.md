## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | In a skillet over medium heat, sauté ground beef, onion, green bell pepper, and garlic. |
| 2 | Add tomato sauce, tomatoes, and spices. |
| 3 | Simmer the beef mixture. |
| 4 | Spray a baking pan with cooking spray. |
| 5 | Place a layer of tortillas in the bottom of the baking pan. |
| 6 | Add a layer of beef mixture over the tortillas. |
| 7 | Continue layering tortillas and beef mixture. |
| 8 | Sprinkle shredded Jack and cheddar cheese over the top layer. |
| 9 | Bake at 350°F for 30 minutes. |
