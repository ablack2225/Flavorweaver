Peanut Dipping Sauce

A creamy peanut dipping sauce built with ginger nuoc cham, hoisin, soy sauce, garlic, ginger, and lime.

Prep10 min
Cook0 min
Total10 min
YieldAbout 1 cup
dipping saucepeanut saucevietnamese-inspiredhoisingingergarliccreamy

Ingredient List

Sauce Base

Peanut butter, ginger nuoc cham, hoisin, soy sauce, garlic, ginger, lime, warm water.

Optional Additions

Sesame oil or fresh chile.

Measured Ingredients

Sauce

1/2 cupCreamy peanut butter
2 TbspRestaurant-Style Ginger Nuoc ChamRequired sauce component.
1 TbspHoisin sauce
1 TbspSoy sauce
1 cloveGarlicMinced.
1 tspGrated ginger or ginger paste
1/2LimeJuiced.
2-4 TbspWarm waterUse to thin.
Optional 1/2 tspSesame oilFor deeper richness.
OptionalFresh chileMinced, for heat.

Instructions

  1. Whisk peanut butter, ginger nuoc cham, hoisin, soy sauce, garlic, ginger, and lime juice until combined.
  2. Add warm water 1 tablespoon at a time until smooth and dippable.
  3. Add optional sesame oil or fresh chile if desired.
  4. Taste and adjust lime, water, heat, or saltiness as needed.

Chef's Notes

This sauce is meant to be flexible. Use less water for a thicker dipping sauce or more water for a drizzleable bowl sauce.

Pairs Well With

  • Fresh Vegetable Spring RollsPrimary dipping sauce.
  • Lemongrass Chicken BowlsOptional drizzle.
