# Banana Split Cake

> A preserved family no-bake banana split cake with a graham cracker crust, fluffy margarine-sugar filling, bananas, crushed pineapple, Cool Whip, and nuts.

## Measured Ingredients

### Filling and Topping

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 2 | Eggs | See raw egg note |
| 2 cups | Confectioners' sugar |  |
| 2 sticks | Margarine | Softened |
| 6 | Bananas | Sliced thin |
| 1 large can | Crushed pineapple | Drained |
| 1 (8 oz) container | Cool Whip |  |
| 1 cup | Chopped nuts |  |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Melt 1 stick of margarine and combine with graham cracker crumbs. |
| 2 | Press crumb mixture into the bottom of a large casserole dish to form a crust. |
| 3 | Whip the eggs, confectioners' sugar, and remaining 2 softened sticks of margarine for 10 minutes, until light and fluffy. |
| 4 | Pour filling over the graham cracker crust. |
| 5 | Layer sliced bananas over the filling. |
| 6 | Add drained crushed pineapple over the bananas. |
| 7 | Cover the top with Cool Whip. |
| 8 | Sprinkle chopped nuts on top. |
| 9 | Refrigerate for several hours before slicing and serving. |

## Notes

Original family note: this cake was the oldest daughter's favorite dessert for years. She absolutely loved it, and it was a hit anywhere it was taken.

This preserved recipe contains raw eggs in the no-bake filling. Use pasteurized eggs if preferred.
