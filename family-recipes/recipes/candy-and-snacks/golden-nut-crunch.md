# Golden Nut Crunch

> A preserved family snack mix made with mixed nuts, Golden Grahams cereal, Cheerios, pretzels, Parmesan cheese, and savory seasonings.

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 300°F. |
| 2 | Mix nuts and melted margarine in a large bowl until the nuts are well coated. |
| 3 | Add Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, oregano, and celery salt. Toss until the nuts are well coated. |
| 4 | Add cereal and pretzels. Stir until mixture is completely blended and all pieces are coated with seasoning. |
| 5 | Spread mixture out in an ungreased baking pan. |
| 6 | Bake for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally. |
| 7 | Pour out onto waxed paper to cool. |
| 8 | Cool completely before storing in an airtight container. |

## Notes

Original family note: neighbor Paula Turner gave this recipe in December 1982.

Original family note: the Golden Grahams cereal gives it a little sweet taste. It is similar to Texas Trash with a sweet twist.

The instructions mention garlic powder, but the original ingredient list does not provide an amount. It is preserved as an amount-not-listed ingredient.
