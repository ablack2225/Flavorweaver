# Jimmie's Caramel Popcorn

## Measured Ingredients

### Jimmie's Caramel Popcorn

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 6 quarts | Popped corn |  |
| 2-3 cups | Peanuts or pecans |  |
| 1 cup | Margarine | 2 sticks |
| 2 cups | Brown sugar | Packed |
| 1/2 cup | Dark Karo syrup | Dark corn syrup |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 250°F. |
| 2 | In a saucepan, mix margarine, brown sugar, and syrup. |
| 3 | Bring to a boil and boil for 5 minutes, stirring frequently. |
| 4 | Pour caramel mixture over the popcorn and nuts. |
| 5 | Mix until all popcorn and nuts are coated. |
| 6 | Pour into a baking pan. |
| 7 | Bake for 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes. |
| 8 | Pour out onto waxed paper. |
| 9 | Separate and allow to cool completely. |
| 10 | Store in an airtight container. |
