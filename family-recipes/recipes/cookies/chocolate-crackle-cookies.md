# Chocolate Crackle Cookies

> A preserved handwritten cookie recipe from Sue Wallesch, made with bittersweet chocolate, Dutch cocoa powder, brown sugar, and powdered sugar coating.

## Measured Ingredients

### Chocolate Crackle Cookies

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 8 oz | Bittersweet chocolate | Melted and cooled |
| 1 1/4 cups | Flour |  |
| 1/2 cup | Dutch cocoa powder |  |
| 2 tsp | Baking powder |  |
| 1/4 tsp | Salt |  |
| 1/2 cup | Unsalted butter |  |
| 1 1/3 cups | Brown sugar |  |
| 2 large | Eggs |  |
| 1 tsp | Vanilla |  |
| 1/3 cup | Milk |  |
| 1 cup | Powdered sugar | For rolling dough and preventing sticking |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Heat oven to 350°F. |
| 2 | Break up chocolate and melt in a double boiler. Allow to cool. |
| 3 | Sift flour, baking powder, cocoa, and salt together. |
| 4 | In a large bowl, beat butter until light and fluffy with the brown sugar. |
| 5 | Add eggs and vanilla and beat until combined. |
| 6 | Add cooled melted chocolate. |
| 7 | Beat on low, adding the dry ingredients alternately with the milk, until combined. |
| 8 | Divide dough into quarters, wrap in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for 2 hours. |
| 9 | Roll each portion of dough into a log about 16 inches long by 1 inch in diameter, using powdered sugar to prevent sticking. |
| 10 | Wrap logs in plastic and chill for 30 minutes. |
| 11 | Cut each log into 1-inch pieces and toss in powdered sugar. |
| 12 | Roll each piece into a ball, then roll in powdered sugar again to coat completely. |
| 13 | Place cookies 2 inches apart on cookie sheet. |
| 14 | Bake about 12-15 minutes, until cookies flatten and sugar splits. |
