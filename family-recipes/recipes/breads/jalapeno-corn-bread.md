# Jalapeño Corn Bread

> A preserved recipe clipping for jalapeño corn bread made with corn muffin mix, separated eggs, buttermilk, sour cream, diced peppers, and butter pieces baked in a preheated cast iron skillet.

## Measured Ingredients

### Jalapeño Corn Bread

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 package | Corn muffin mix | Jiffy style, as listed on clipping |
| 3 | Eggs | Separated |
| 3/4 cup | Buttermilk |  |
| 1/2 cup | Sour cream | Dairy Pride style, as listed on clipping |
| 4 | Jalapeño peppers | Cored, seeded, and diced |
| 1 small | Red pepper | Diced |
| 4 Tbsp | Butter | Diced |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 400°F. |
| 2 | Grease a 10-inch cast iron skillet and preheat it in the oven. |
| 3 | In a large bowl, mix egg yolks, buttermilk, and sour cream well. |
| 4 | Add corn muffin mix and diced peppers. |
| 5 | Mix until moist. |
| 6 | Whip egg whites to stiff peaks. |
| 7 | Fold whipped egg whites and diced butter pieces into batter. |
| 8 | Pour mixture into the heated skillet. |
| 9 | Bake for 25-30 minutes. |
