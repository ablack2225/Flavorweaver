# Coffee Cake

> A preserved family coffee cake made with canned biscuits, brown sugar, margarine, and chopped pecans, baked in a tube or Bundt pan.

## Measured Ingredients

### Coffee Cake

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 (10 count) can | Biscuits | Large variety |
| 1 cup | Brown sugar | Packed |
| 1/2 cup | Margarine | Melted |
| 1/2 cup | Chopped pecans |  |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 350°F. |
| 2 | Melt the margarine. |
| 3 | Cut the biscuits into fourths. |
| 4 | In a bowl, combine biscuits, brown sugar, margarine, and pecans. |
| 5 | Stir to blend all ingredients well. |
| 6 | Pour mixture into a greased tube or Bundt pan. |
| 7 | Bake for 25-30 minutes. |
| 8 | Remove from oven and allow cake to cool in the pan about 10 minutes. |
| 9 | Turn out onto a serving platter. |
| 10 | Cut into serving-size pieces and serve. |
