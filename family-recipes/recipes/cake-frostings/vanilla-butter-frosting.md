# Vanilla Butter Frosting

> A preserved family vanilla butter frosting made with softened margarine, confectioners' sugar, vanilla, and milk.

## Measured Ingredients

### Standard Batch

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|

### For a 3-Layer Cake

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Blend margarine and confectioners' sugar. |
| 2 | Stir in vanilla and milk. |
| 3 | Beat until frosting is smooth and of spreading consistency. |
| 4 | Use to fill and frost cake. |

## Notes

Original family note: the standard batch fills and frosts two 8- or 9-inch layers or a 13x9-inch cake.

Original family note for a 3-layer cake: use 1/4 cup softened margarine, 4 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar, 2 teaspoons vanilla, and about 3 tablespoons milk.

Prep and total time are estimated from the simple blend-and-beat method. The original recipe page left servings and prep time blank.
