Thai-Inspired Bang Bang Shrimp Bowls

Crispy shrimp with creamy-sweet chile sauce, coconut-lime rice, garlic-oyster broccoli, toasted coconut, crispy fried onions, green onion, mint, and lime.

Make First / Can Hold / Make Last

Make first: chile-garlic paste, sweet chile sauce, toasted coconut. Can hold: rice warm, broccoli uncovered, sauce chilled. Make last: shrimp and assembly.

Measured Ingredients

Chile-garlic paste: 2 to 4 guajillo chiles, 4 garlic cloves, 2 Tbsp vinegar, 1 Tbsp water, 1 tsp sugar, 1/2 tsp salt, 1 to 2 tsp crushed red pepper or 1/4 tsp cayenne.

Sweet chile sauce: 1/2 cup water, 1/4 cup vinegar, 1/3 cup sugar, 1 to 2 Tbsp paste, 1 garlic clove, 1/2 tsp ginger, 1/4 tsp salt, 1 tsp cornstarch plus 1 Tbsp water.

Creamy sauce: 1/2 cup mayonnaise, 1/4 cup cooled sweet chile sauce, 1 to 2 tsp chile-garlic paste, 1/2 tsp lime juice, pinch salt.

Shrimp: 1 lb shrimp, 1 cup dairy soak, 1 cup cornstarch, 1/2 tsp salt, 1/4 tsp pepper, oil. Dairy soak: 1/2 cup evaporated milk, 1/2 cup water, 1 Tbsp vinegar.

Rice: 1 cup basmati rice, 3/4 cup coconut milk, 3/4 cup water, 1/2 tsp salt, 1 tsp sugar, zest of 1 lime, 1 to 2 tsp lime juice, 1/4 to 1/3 cup toasted coconut, 1 green onion.

Finish: 1 batch Garlic-Oyster Broccoli, 4 to 6 Tbsp crispy fried onions, 1 to 2 green onions, 1 to 2 Tbsp mint, lime wedges.

Stage-Based Prep Bowls

Sauce 1: 2 to 4 guajillos; 4 garlic; 2 Tbsp vinegar; 1 Tbsp water; 1 tsp sugar; 1/2 tsp salt; heat.

Sauce 2: 1/2 cup water; 1/4 cup vinegar; 1/3 cup sugar; 1 to 2 Tbsp paste; 1 garlic; 1/2 tsp ginger; 1/4 tsp salt.

Sauce 3: 1 tsp cornstarch; 1 Tbsp water.

Sauce 4: 1/2 cup mayo; 1/4 cup cooled sweet chile sauce; 1 to 2 tsp paste; 1/2 tsp lime.

Rice: 1 cup rice; 3/4 cup coconut milk; 3/4 cup water; 1/2 tsp salt; 1 tsp sugar. Finish with lime, coconut, green onion.

Shrimp: 1 lb shrimp in 1 cup dairy soak. Coating: 1 cup cornstarch, 1/2 tsp salt, 1/4 tsp pepper.

Assembly: rice, 1 batch broccoli, shrimp, crispy onions, coconut, green onion, mint, lime.

Instructions

  1. Make the chile-garlic paste with the measured Sauce 1 ingredients.
  2. Simmer Sauce 2 ingredients 3 to 5 minutes; add Sauce 3 slurry and cool completely.
  3. Cook the measured rice ingredients. Toast coconut separately at about 300°F for 2 to 5 minutes. Fold lime, coconut, and green onion into the cooked rice.
  4. Whisk Sauce 4 ingredients.
  5. Prepare one batch of Garlic-Oyster Broccoli.
  6. Rest the dairy soak 5 to 10 minutes. Soak shrimp exactly 10 minutes.
  7. Coat shrimp and fry at 350°F to 375°F for 1 to 2 minutes in small batches. Drain on a rack.
  8. Toss shrimp with enough sauce to lightly coat.
  9. Build bowls with rice, broccoli, shrimp, coconut, 2 to 3 Tbsp crispy fried onions per generous bowl, green onion, mint, and lime.
