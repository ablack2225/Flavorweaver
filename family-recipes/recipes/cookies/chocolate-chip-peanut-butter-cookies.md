# Chocolate Chip Peanut Butter Cookies

> A preserved family cookie recipe credited to Sue Wallesch, who made these often. This recipe includes both drop-cookie instructions and a pan-cookie bar variation.

## Measured Ingredients

### Chocolate Chip Peanut Butter Cookies

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 cup / 2 sticks | Butter or margarine | Softened |
| 1 cup | Creamy peanut butter | Reese's style, as listed on clipping |
| 1 cup | Granulated sugar |  |
| 1 cup | Packed light brown sugar |  |
| 2 | Eggs |  |
| 2 1/2 cups | All-purpose flour |  |
| 1 1/2 tsp | Baking soda |  |
| 1 tsp | Baking powder |  |
| 1/2 tsp | Salt |  |
| 2 cups | Semi-sweet chocolate chips or milk chocolate chips | 12-ounce semi-sweet package or 11.5-ounce milk chocolate package |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Heat oven to 375°F. |
| 2 | In a large bowl, beat butter, peanut butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and eggs on medium speed of an electric mixer until well blended. |
| 3 | Stir flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt together. |
| 4 | Gradually add dry mixture to the butter mixture, beating until well blended. |
| 5 | Stir in chocolate chips. |
| 6 | Drop by rounded teaspoons onto an ungreased cookie sheet. |
| 7 | Bake for 8-10 minutes, or until set. |
| 8 | Cool slightly, then remove from cookie sheet to a wire rack. |

## Pan Recipe Variation

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Spread batter into a greased 15 1/2 x 10 1/2 x 1-inch jelly-roll pan. |
| 2 | Bake at 375°F for 20 minutes, or until golden brown. |
| 3 | Cool completely in pan on wire rack. |
| 4 | Cut into bars. Makes about 48 bars. |
