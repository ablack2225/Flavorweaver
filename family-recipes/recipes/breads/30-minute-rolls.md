### 30 Minute Rolls

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Mix yeast, warm water, and sugar together. Let stand 10 minutes. |
| 2 | In a separate bowl, mix flour, baking powder, salt, and dry milk. |
| 3 | Add oil to the yeast mixture. |
| 4 | Add yeast mixture to the dry mixture. |
| 5 | Knead until smooth. |
| 6 | Shape dough into rolls. |
| 7 | Let rolls rise 30 minutes. |
| 8 | Bake at 350°F until done. |
