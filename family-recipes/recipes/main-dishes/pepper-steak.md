# Pepper Steak

> A preserved family pepper steak recipe made with tenderized round steak, green peppers, tomatoes, onion, soy sauce, beef bouillon, garlic, ginger, and a cornstarch-water thickener.

### Pepper Steak

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Trim steak and pound to 1/4-inch thickness using a meat mallet. |
| 2 | Cut steak into serving-size pieces. |
| 3 | Brown steak in hot oil in a large skillet, then drain. |
| 4 | Return steak to skillet. |
| 5 | Add tomatoes, green peppers, onion, water, soy sauce, pepper, bouillon granules, garlic powder, and ginger. |
| 6 | Stir until well combined. |
| 7 | Cover, reduce heat, and simmer about 55 minutes, stirring occasionally. |
| 8 | Optional thickening step from the ingredient list: combine cornstarch and water, then stir into the sauce near the end of cooking if thickening is desired. |
| 9 | Serve over hot rice. |
