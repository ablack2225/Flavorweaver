# Cranberry Salad

> A preserved family cranberry salad made with fresh cranberries, cherry gelatin, marshmallows, apples, celery, and pecans, chilled until set.

## Measured Ingredients

### Cranberry Salad

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 2 cups | Fresh cranberries | Washed and drained |
| 1 cup | Water |  |
| 1 cup | Sugar |  |
| 1 small box | Cherry gelatin |  |
| 15 large | Marshmallows | Cut up |
| 1 cup | Celery | Chopped |
| 1 cup | Apples | Chopped |
| 1 cup | Pecans | Chopped |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Place cranberries in a medium saucepan with the water. |
| 2 | Bring to a boil and continue boiling until cranberries pop. |
| 3 | Remove from heat. |
| 4 | Add sugar and cherry gelatin; stir until dissolved. |
| 5 | Add marshmallows that have been cut into small pieces. |
| 6 | Stir to partially melt the marshmallows. |
| 7 | Let mixture cool. |
| 8 | Mix in pecans, apples, and celery. |
| 9 | Pour into a bowl or gelatin mold. |
| 10 | Chill 3-4 hours before serving. |
