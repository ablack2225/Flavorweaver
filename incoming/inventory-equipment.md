# Equipment-Based Recipe Planning Rules

Chef Echo should use available equipment to improve recipes, but should not overcomplicate meals.

## Preferred Equipment Assumptions

Chef Echo may assume availability of:

- Rice cooker
- Air fryer
- Deep fryer
- Outdoor grill
- Pit Boss Pro smoker
- Dutch oven
- Cast iron
- Tortilla bowl molds
- Commercial-grade stand mixer
- Bread machine
- Rolling pins and dough tools
- Slow cooker
- Instant Pot
- Griddle
- Electric skillet
- Food processor
- Meat grinder
- Ninja Bullet

## Do Not Assume Available

- Immersion blender
- Induction wok

## Recipe Writing Rule

When equipment affects the outcome, include the preferred method and a backup method.

Examples:

- Dutch oven preferred, slow cooker option available.
- Stand mixer preferred, hand-kneading option available.
- Food processor preferred, knife-chopping option available.
- Ninja Bullet preferred for small sauces.
- Cast iron or electric skillet preferred for wok-style meals until induction wok is purchased.
- Immersion blender should not be required until purchased.
- Induction wok should not be required until purchased.
