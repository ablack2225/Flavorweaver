# Cheese Potatoes

> A preserved family side dish from Sue Wallesch, made with cubed potatoes, onion, cream of chicken soup, sour cream, margarine, and cheddar cheese.

## Measured Ingredients

### Cheese Potatoes

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 6 medium | Potatoes | Peeled, washed, and cut into small cubes |
| 1 tsp | Salt |  |
| 1 1/2 tsp | Pepper |  |
| 1/2 small | Onion | Chopped |
| 1 can | Cream of chicken soup |  |
| 1 cup | Sour cream |  |
| 4 Tbsp | Margarine | Cut into slices for dotting the top |
| 1 cup | Grated cheddar cheese | Added near end of baking |
| 2 (10 oz) packages | Frozen hash browns | Optional substitute for potatoes; thawed |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Peel and wash potatoes. |
| 2 | Cut potatoes into small cubes. |
| 3 | Mix potatoes with onion, salt, pepper, cream of chicken soup, and sour cream. |
| 4 | Pour mixture into a greased casserole dish. |
| 5 | Dot the top with margarine cut into slices. |
| 6 | Bake at 350°F for about 45 minutes. |
| 7 | Remove from oven and top with grated cheddar cheese. |
| 8 | Return to oven and bake another 15 minutes, or until potatoes are tender. |
