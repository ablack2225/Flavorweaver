# Cheese Rolls

> A preserved family appetizer made with cream cheese, sharp cheddar, garlic, red pepper, and pecans, shaped into rolls and served with crackers.

## Measured Ingredients

### Cheese Rolls

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 3 (8 oz) packages | Cream cheese | Allow to soften |
| 1 lb | Sharp cheddar cheese | Grated |
| Dash | Salt |  |
| 1 tsp | Ground red pepper |  |
| 4 cloves | Garlic | Minced |
| 1/2 cup | Pecans | Chopped |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Put the cream cheese and cheddar into a large bowl. Cover with plastic wrap and allow to soften. |
| 2 | Add the salt, ground red pepper, minced garlic, and chopped pecans. Mix thoroughly. An electric mixer may be used. |
| 3 | Shape the mixture into rolls and wrap. |
| 4 | Store in the refrigerator until ready to serve. |
| 5 | Serve with crackers. |
