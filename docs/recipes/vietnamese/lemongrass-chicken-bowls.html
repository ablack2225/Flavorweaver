Lemongrass Chicken Bowls

Fresh Vietnamese-inspired chicken bowls with lemongrass-marinated chicken, angel hair pasta, crisp vegetables, pickled vegetables, crushed cashews, and generous ginger nuoc cham.

Prep35 min
Cook20 min
Marinate2 to 4 hr
Serves2 to 3
chicken bowllemongrass chickenginger nuoc chamnapa cabbagesauce-driven

Formula Components

  • Lemongrass chicken with chicken thighs, ginger nuoc cham, lemongrass paste, garlic, and neutral oil.
  • Cooked angel hair pasta as the bowl base.
  • Fresh vegetables such as napa cabbage, cucumber, and zucchini ribbons or matchsticks.
  • Asian pickled garden vegetables for brightness.
  • Crushed cashews and extra ginger nuoc cham to finish.

Method

  1. Marinate chicken with ginger nuoc cham, lemongrass paste, garlic, and oil.
  2. Cook chicken until browned, cooked through, and aromatic.
  3. Prepare pasta and fresh vegetables.
  4. Build bowls with pasta, vegetables, chicken, pickled vegetables, cashews, and sauce.

Pairs Well With
