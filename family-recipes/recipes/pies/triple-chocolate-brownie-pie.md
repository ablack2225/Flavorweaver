# Triple Chocolate Brownie Pie

> A preserved family brownie pie made with cocoa, semi-sweet chocolate chips, chopped nuts, and vanilla, served with vanilla ice cream and Hershey's chocolate topping.

| 1/4 tsp | Salt |  |
| 1/2 cup | Semi-sweet chocolate chips |  |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 350°F. |
| 2 | Lightly grease an 8-inch pie plate. |
| 3 | In a small mixing bowl, beat eggs. |
| 4 | Blend sugar and melted margarine into the beaten eggs. |
| 5 | In a separate bowl, stir together flour, cocoa, and salt. |
| 6 | Add dry ingredients to the egg mixture, beating until well blended. |
| 7 | Stir in chocolate chips, chopped nuts, and vanilla. |
| 8 | Spread batter into the prepared pie plate. |
| 9 | Bake for 35 minutes, or until set. The pie will not test done in the center. |
| 10 | Cool completely. |
| 11 | Cut into wedges. |
| 12 | Serve with vanilla ice cream and top with Hershey's chocolate topping. |
