# Stuffed Green Peppers

> A preserved family stuffed pepper recipe made with green bell peppers filled with ground beef, onion, cooked rice, tomato sauce, garlic powder, and optional American cheese slices.

## Measured Ingredients

### Stuffed Green Peppers

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 4 large | Green bell peppers | Seeds and membranes removed |
| 5 cups | Boiling salted water | For softening peppers |
| 1 lb | Ground beef |  |
| 1/2 medium | Onion | Chopped |
| 1 tsp | Salt |  |
| 1/2 tsp | Garlic powder |  |
| 1 cup | Cooked rice |  |
| 1 (15 oz) can | Tomato sauce | 1 cup in filling; remainder over peppers |
| As desired | American cheese slices | Optional |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 350°F. |
| 2 | Cut a thin slice from the stem end of each green pepper. |
| 3 | Remove all seeds and membranes, then wash peppers thoroughly. |
| 4 | Cook peppers in boiling salted water for 5 minutes; drain. |
| 5 | Cook ground beef and onion until meat is lightly browned and onion is tender. |
| 6 | Drain excess fat. |
| 7 | Stir in salt, garlic powder, cooked rice, and 1 cup tomato sauce. |
| 8 | Reserve remaining tomato sauce to pour over the peppers. |
| 9 | Stuff each pepper with the meat mixture. |
| 10 | Stand peppers upright in an ungreased baking dish. |
| 11 | Pour remaining tomato sauce over the peppers. |
| 12 | Cover and bake for 45 minutes. |
| 13 | Uncover and bake 15 minutes more. |
| 14 | Add American cheese slices at the end, if desired. |

## Notes

Original family hint: Sue usually cut each pepper in half and put the meat mixture in each half, then covered with sauce and baked as directed.

The printed recipe lists 4 servings and 1 hour 15 minutes prep time. Since the bake time is 1 hour, the printed prep time appears to function more like total time. Active prep time is estimated.
