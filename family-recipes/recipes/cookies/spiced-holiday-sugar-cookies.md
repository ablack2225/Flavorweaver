# Spiced Holiday Sugar Cookies

> A preserved recipe clipping for nutmeg-spiced holiday sugar cookies rolled, cut into shapes, baked, glazed, and decorated.

## Measured Ingredients

### Cookies

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 cup | Butter | Softened |
| 1 1/4 cups | Sugar |  |
| 1 | Egg |  |
| 2 tsp | Vanilla extract |  |
| 2 1/3 cups | Flour |  |
| 1 tsp | Baking soda |  |
| 1/4 tsp | Ground nutmeg |  |
| 1/8 tsp | Salt |  |
| As desired | Colored sugars or jimmies | Optional decoration |

### Glaze

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 3-4 tsp | Milk | Additional may be added to thin glaze |
| 1/2 tsp | Vanilla extract |  |
| 3-4 drops | Food color | Optional; additional colors may be made in separate bowls |
| 1 cup | Confectioners' sugar | Additional may be added to stiffen glaze |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Beat softened butter in a large bowl with an electric mixer on medium-high speed for 30 seconds. |
| 2 | Add sugar, egg, and vanilla. |
| 3 | Mix flour, baking soda, nutmeg, and salt together. |
| 4 | Gradually stir dry ingredients into the butter mixture. |
| 5 | Chill dough until firm, about 1 hour. |
| 6 | Preheat oven to 375°F. |
| 7 | Roll dough to 1/8-inch thickness on a lightly floured surface. |
| 8 | Cut into shapes with favorite cookie cutters. |
| 9 | Sprinkle with colored sugars, if desired. |
| 10 | Bake on a greased baking sheet for 8-10 minutes, or until lightly browned. |
| 11 | Cool cookies 1 minute on cookie sheet. |
| 12 | Remove cookies to a wire rack and cool completely. |
| 13 | Mix glaze ingredients together. Add additional water or milk as needed to thin, or more powdered sugar to stiffen. |
| 14 | Blend in 3-4 drops food color, if desired. To make multiple colors, divide icing among small bowls and tint each with 1-2 drops food color. |
| 15 | Glaze and decorate cooled cookies. |
