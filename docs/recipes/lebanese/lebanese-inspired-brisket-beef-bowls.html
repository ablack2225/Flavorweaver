Lebanese-Inspired Brisket Beef Bowls

Buttery toasted vermicelli lemon-herb basmati rice with juicy Lebanese-spiced ground brisket patties, garlic-lemon tahini yogurt sauce, cucumber-tomato parsley salad, pickle-brine onion and cucumber, and scratch lemon-garlic pita chips.

Kitchen Execution Summary

Rice

Toasted vermicelli basmati rice finished after cooking with lemon zest, lemon juice, parsley, and optional mint.

Beef

75/25 ground brisket shaped into mini patties, chilled, then cooked at 300°F in Amy's hot electric skillet.

Sauce and Freshness

Garlic-lemon tahini yogurt sauce, cucumber-tomato parsley salad, and pickle-brine onion-cucumber topping.

Crunch

Scratch pita cooked as flatbread, cut into chips, seasoned, and baked crisp.

Measured Ingredients by Component

Scratch Pita / Pita Chips

Dough: 1 cup AP flour; 1/2 tsp instant yeast; 1/2 tsp sugar; 1/2 tsp kosher salt; 1 Tbsp olive oil; 1/3 cup warm water, plus 1 to 2 Tbsp more only if needed.

Chip finish: 1 Tbsp olive oil; 1/4 tsp garlic powder; 1/4 tsp paprika; tiny pinch cumin; pinch kosher salt; tiny pinch lemon zest after baking.

Toasted Vermicelli Lemon-Herb Basmati Rice

3/4 cup basmati rice; 1/4 cup broken vermicelli, angel hair, or thin spaghetti; 1 Tbsp butter; 1 tsp olive oil; 1 1/2 cups chicken broth or water plus bouillon; 1/4 tsp kosher salt; 1/2 tsp lemon zest; 1 to 2 tsp fresh lemon juice; 2 Tbsp chopped parsley; optional 1 Tbsp chopped mint.

Lebanese-Spiced Brisket Patties

1 lb 75/25 ground brisket; 1/4 cup grated onion, squeezed dry; 1 1/2 tsp minced or grated garlic; 1/3 cup finely chopped parsley; 1 1/4 tsp kosher salt; 1/2 tsp black pepper; 1 tsp allspice; 3/4 tsp cumin; 1/2 tsp coriander; 1/2 tsp paprika; 1/8 tsp cinnamon; optional pinch cayenne; optional 1 Tbsp fine breadcrumbs or crushed pita only if loose.

Garlic-Lemon Tahini Yogurt Sauce

1/2 cup Greek yogurt or sour cream; 2 Tbsp tahini; 1 Tbsp fresh lemon juice; 1/2 tsp lemon zest; 1 small grated garlic clove; 1 tsp olive oil; 1/4 tsp kosher salt; pinch black pepper; 1/4 to 1 tsp water only if needed; tiny drizzle honey only if too sharp.

Salad and Pickled Topping

Salad: 1 cup diced cucumber; 1 cup diced tomato; 2 Tbsp chopped parsley; optional 1 Tbsp finely diced onion; 1 tsp lemon juice; 1 tsp olive oil; 1/8 tsp kosher salt; pinch black pepper; optional 1 Tbsp mint.

Pickled topping: 1/2 cup pickle-brine red onion and cucumber, drained very well; 1 tsp lemon juice; 1/4 tsp lemon zest; 1 Tbsp parsley; a few drops olive oil; small pinch sumac preferred, or 1/16 tsp Tajin only if sumac is unavailable.

Stage-Based Prep Bowls

Dry Pita Mix

1 cup AP flour; 1/2 tsp instant yeast; 1/2 tsp sugar; 1/2 tsp kosher salt.

Pita Liquid

1 Tbsp olive oil; 1/3 cup warm water, plus 1 to 2 Tbsp extra water only if needed.

Rice Bowls

3/4 cup basmati rice; 1/4 cup broken vermicelli; 1 Tbsp butter; 1 tsp olive oil; 1 1/2 cups broth; 1/4 tsp salt; finish with 1/2 tsp lemon zest, 1 to 2 tsp lemon juice, and 2 Tbsp parsley.

Beef Bowls

1 lb brisket; 1/4 cup squeezed grated onion; 1 1/2 tsp garlic; 1/3 cup parsley; 1 1/4 tsp salt; 1/2 tsp pepper; 1 tsp allspice; 3/4 tsp cumin; 1/2 tsp coriander; 1/2 tsp paprika; 1/8 tsp cinnamon.

Sauce Bowl

1/2 cup yogurt or sour cream; 2 Tbsp tahini; 1 Tbsp lemon juice; 1/2 tsp lemon zest; 1 grated garlic clove; 1 tsp olive oil; 1/4 tsp salt; pinch pepper.

Fresh Bowls

Salad: 1 cup cucumber, 1 cup tomato, 1/8 tsp salt, then finish with 2 Tbsp parsley, 1 tsp lemon juice, and 1 tsp olive oil. Pickles: 1/2 cup drained pickle-brine onion/cucumber, 1 tsp lemon juice, 1/4 tsp lemon zest, 1 Tbsp parsley, and sumac or 1/16 tsp Tajin.

Do Not Forget

Prep bowls include measurements, and the instructions repeat measurements at the moment of use.

Do not add lemon juice to the rice before cooking. Add 1 to 2 tsp lemon juice and 1/2 tsp lemon zest after cooking.

Squeeze the grated onion very dry before adding 1/4 cup squeezed grated onion to the beef.

Cook patties at 300°F in Amy's electric skillet. Use 325°F only if browning is too slow.

Use sumac if available. If not, use only 1/16 tsp Tajin on the pickled onion-cucumber topping, not in the beef, rice, sauce, or salad.

Full Cooking Timeline and Instructions

  1. Make the pita dough. Mix 1 cup AP flour, 1/2 tsp instant yeast, 1/2 tsp sugar, and 1/2 tsp kosher salt. Add 1 Tbsp olive oil and 1/3 cup warm water. Knead 4 to 6 minutes, adding 1 to 2 Tbsp extra water only if needed. Rest 45 to 60 minutes.
  2. Shape the beef. Mix 1 lb brisket, 1/4 cup squeezed grated onion, 1 1/2 tsp garlic, 1/3 cup parsley, 1 1/4 tsp salt, 1/2 tsp pepper, 1 tsp allspice, 3/4 tsp cumin, 1/2 tsp coriander, 1/2 tsp paprika, and 1/8 tsp cinnamon. Shape into about 8 patties, roughly 2 oz each, 3 inches long, and 1/2 inch thick. Chill 20 to 30 minutes.
  3. Make sauce. Whisk 1/2 cup yogurt or sour cream, 2 Tbsp tahini, 1 Tbsp lemon juice, 1/2 tsp lemon zest, 1 grated garlic clove, 1 tsp olive oil, 1/4 tsp salt, and pinch pepper. Thin with 1/4 to 1 tsp water only if needed.
  4. Make salad and pickles. Salt 1 cup cucumber and 1 cup tomato with 1/8 tsp salt; rest 5 to 10 minutes and drain. Finish with 2 Tbsp parsley, 1 tsp lemon juice, and 1 tsp olive oil. Toss 1/2 cup drained pickle-brine onion/cucumber with 1 tsp lemon juice, 1/4 tsp lemon zest, 1 Tbsp parsley, and sumac or 1/16 tsp Tajin.
  5. Cook rice. Toast 1/4 cup broken vermicelli in 1 Tbsp butter and 1 tsp olive oil. Add rinsed 3/4 cup basmati rice, then 1 1/2 cups broth and 1/4 tsp salt. Cook until tender. After cooking, fold in 1/2 tsp lemon zest, 1 to 2 tsp lemon juice, and 2 Tbsp parsley.
  6. Cook pita and chips. Roll dough into 3 to 4 flatbreads about 1/8 inch thick. Cook at 275°F, increasing to 300°F only if needed, about 1 to 2 minutes per side. Cut into chips, toss with 1 Tbsp olive oil, 1/4 tsp garlic powder, 1/4 tsp paprika, optional cumin, and salt. Bake at 350°F for 8 to 12 minutes.
  7. Cook beef. Heat skillet to 300°F. Cook patties 3 to 4 minutes per side, raising to 325°F only if needed. Rest 3 to 5 minutes.
  8. Build bowls. Divide rice between 2 bowls. Add 4 patties per bowl, sauce, salad, pickled topping, scratch pita chips, parsley, and lemon.

Final Taste Target

The bowl should be juicy, buttery, garlicky, lemon-bright, creamy, herbaceous, and deeply savory, with fresh salad, pickled snap, and crisp scratch pita chips.
