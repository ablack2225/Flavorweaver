# Fluffy White Frosting

> A preserved handwritten frosting recipe from Sue Wallesch, made over a double boiler with egg whites, sugar, light corn syrup, salt, and vanilla.

## Measured Ingredients

### Fluffy White Frosting

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 3 | Egg whites |  |
| 3/4 cup | Sugar |  |
| 6 Tbsp | Light corn syrup |  |
| 1/2 tsp | Salt |  |
| 1 tsp | Vanilla | Added after cooling |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Place egg whites, sugar, light corn syrup, and salt in the top of a double boiler. |
| 2 | Set over rapidly boiling water. |
| 3 | Beat for 4-5 minutes. |
| 4 | Remove from heat and cool. |
| 5 | Add vanilla. |
| 6 | Beat until frosting stands in peaks. |
