# Grape Juice Crush

> A preserved family punch made with grape juice, orange juice, lemon juice, sugar, cold water, and ginger ale added just before serving.

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Mix all ingredients except the ginger ale. |
| 2 | Chill the punch mixture in the refrigerator ahead of time, if desired. |
| 3 | Chill the ginger ale separately. |
| 4 | Add the ginger ale just before serving the punch. |
