## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | In a microwave-safe dish, add cherry pie filling. |
| 2 | Mix yellow cake mix, margarine, brown sugar, and cinnamon together. Do not add the water marked omit on the card. |
| 3 | Spread or crumble topping mixture over cherry pie filling. |
| 4 | Cook in microwave for 12-14 minutes, or until heated through and topping is set. |
