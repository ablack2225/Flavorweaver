# German Chocolate Pound Cake

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Melt the chocolate in the microwave and allow it to cool. |
| 2 | Preheat oven to 300°F. |
| 3 | Cream together sugar, shortening, eggs, flavorings, and buttermilk. |
| 4 | Blend in the sifted dry ingredients until well blended. |
| 5 | Stir in the cooled chocolate. |
| 6 | Pour batter into a greased and lightly floured tube or Bundt pan. |
| 7 | Bake for about 1 1/2 hours, or until the cake tests done. |
| 8 | Cool in pan for 10 minutes. |
| 9 | Turn out onto a serving platter. |
