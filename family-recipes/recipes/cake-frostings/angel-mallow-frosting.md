# Angel Mallow Frosting

> A preserved family marshmallow-style frosting made with sugar, egg whites, vanilla, and marshmallow cream.

## Measured Ingredients

### Angel Mallow Frosting

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1/2 cup | Sugar |  |
| 2 Tbsp | Water |  |
| 2 | Egg whites |  |
| 1/2 tsp | Vanilla |  |
| 1 (7 oz) jar | Marshmallow cream |  |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Combine sugar, egg whites, and water in a double boiler. |
| 2 | Beat with a mixer over boiling water until soft peaks form. |
| 3 | Add the marshmallow cream and beat until stiff peaks form. |
| 4 | Remove from heat and beat in the vanilla. |
| 5 | Use frosting on whatever kind of cake you desire. |
