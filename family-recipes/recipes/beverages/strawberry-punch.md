# Strawberry Punch

> A preserved family strawberry punch made with ginger ale, frozen sliced strawberries, frozen limeade, and frozen pink lemonade.

## Measured Ingredients

### Strawberry Punch

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 (2 liter) bottle | Ginger ale | Chill before serving |
| 1 small box | Frozen sliced strawberries | Slightly thaw before mixing |
| 1 (12 oz) can | Frozen limeade | Thaw; do not dilute |
| 1 (12 oz) can | Frozen pink lemonade | Thaw; do not dilute |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Slightly thaw the sliced strawberries so they can be broken up easily. |
| 2 | Thaw the frozen limeade and frozen pink lemonade. |
| 3 | Just before serving, mix all ingredients together. |
| 4 | Stir to blend well. |
| 5 | Do not dilute the frozen limeade or frozen pink lemonade. |
