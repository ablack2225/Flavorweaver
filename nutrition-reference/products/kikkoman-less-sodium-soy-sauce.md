## Verification Notes

This product is still sodium-dense even though it is lower sodium than regular soy sauce, so measure carefully in recipe calculations.

Because this product contains wheat and soy, note it in recipes when gluten-limiting or soy awareness matters.

If using regular soy sauce, tamari, coconut aminos, dark soy sauce, low-sodium tamari, or another brand, create a separate product reference or mark the calculation as estimated pending label.
