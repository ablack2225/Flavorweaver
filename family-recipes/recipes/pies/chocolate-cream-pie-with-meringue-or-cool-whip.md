# Chocolate Cream Pie with Meringue or Cool-Whip

> A preserved family chocolate cream pie made from Sue's cream pie base, adjusted with extra sugar and cornstarch, melted unsweetened chocolate, and finished either with baked meringue or a chilled Cool-Whip topping.

## Measured Ingredients

### Chocolate Cream Filling

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|

| 1 1/2 cups | Sugar | Increased from base cream pie amount for chocolate version |
| 1/3 cup | Cornstarch | Increased from base cream pie amount for chocolate version |
| 1/2 tsp | Salt |  |
| 1 large can | Evaporated milk | Blend with whole milk to make 3 cups total |
| Enough to make 3 cups total | Whole milk | Combined with evaporated milk |
| 4 | Egg yolks | Slightly beaten |
| 1 Tbsp plus 1 tsp | Vanilla |  |

### Meringue Topping Option

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 4 | Egg whites | From separated eggs |
| 1/2 cup | Sugar |  |

### Cool-Whip Topping Option

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| As desired | Cool-Whip | Add after pie has cooled or chilled |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Prepare and fully bake a 9-inch pie shell. |
| 2 | Stir sugar, cornstarch, and salt together in a saucepan. |
| 3 | Blend evaporated milk, whole milk, and egg yolks together. |
| 4 | Gradually stir milk mixture into sugar mixture. |
| 5 | Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture thickens and boils. |
| 6 | Boil and stir for 1 minute. |
| 7 | Remove from heat. |
| 8 | Stir in melted unsweetened chocolate and vanilla. Do not add margarine to the chocolate version. |
| 9 | Immediately pour filling into the baked pie shell. |
| 10 | For meringue topping, beat egg whites and cream of tartar until soft peaks form. |
| 11 | Gradually beat in sugar and continue beating until stiff peaks form. |
| 12 | Spread meringue over the hot pie filling. |
| 13 | Bake at 400°F for about 10 minutes, until lightly brown. |
| 14 | Cool before serving. |
| 15 | For Cool-Whip topping instead, skip the meringue, refrigerate the filled pie until cooled and set, then top with Cool-Whip before serving. |

## Notes

This recipe was split from the original Vanilla Cream Pie & Variations page so the chocolate cream pie version can stand on its own.

This version may be finished with baked meringue or chilled and topped with Cool-Whip.

The chocolate variation increases the sugar to 1 1/2 cups, increases the cornstarch to 1/3 cup, omits the margarine, and adds 2 oz melted unsweetened chocolate with the vanilla.

Servings, prep time, cook time, and total time are estimated. Meringue bake time is from the recipe text.
