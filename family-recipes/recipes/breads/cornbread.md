## Measured Ingredients

### Cornbread

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 cup | Flour |  |
| 1 cup | Cornmeal |  |
| 4 tsp | Baking powder |  |
| 1 tsp | Salt |  |
| 2 heaping Tbsp | Sugar |  |
| 3/4-1 cup | Milk | Use enough to make a relatively thin mixture |
| 1-2 Tbsp | Cooking oil |  |
| 1 | Egg |  |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 425°F. |
| 2 | Mix all ingredients together until well combined. |
| 3 | Add enough milk to make a relatively thin mixture. |
| 4 | Pour into a greased pan or muffin tin. |
| 5 | Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown. |
