# Molasses Sugar Cookies

> A preserved family molasses sugar cookie recipe with cinnamon, ginger, cloves, and cracked tops from chilling and rolling in granulated sugar.

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Cream shortening. |
| 2 | Gradually add sugar, beating at medium speed until mixture is light and fluffy. |
| 3 | Add egg and molasses; mix well. |
| 4 | Combine all dry ingredients and mix well. |
| 5 | Add about one fourth of the dry ingredients at a time to the creamed mixture, mixing until smooth after each addition. |
| 6 | Chill dough at least 1 hour. |
| 7 | Preheat oven to 375°F. |
| 8 | Shape dough into 1-inch balls and roll in granulated sugar. |
| 9 | Place 2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheets. |
| 10 | Bake for 10 minutes. Tops will crack. |
| 11 | Remove from cookie sheets to a sheet of aluminum foil to cool. |
