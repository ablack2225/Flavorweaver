| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Mix lemon and lime Jello with boiling water until dissolved. |
| 2 | Stir in evaporated milk, crushed pineapple, and chopped pecans. |
| 3 | Chill until set. |
| 4 | Serve cold. |
