# Spiced Oatmeal & Raisins

> A preserved family breakfast oatmeal made with quick-cooking oats, raisins, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt.

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Bring water to a boil in a medium saucepan. |
| 2 | Stir in oatmeal, raisins, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt. |
| 3 | Cook for 1 minute, stirring occasionally. |
| 4 | Cover, remove from heat, and let stand for a few minutes before serving. |
| 5 | Serve with sugar and milk. |
