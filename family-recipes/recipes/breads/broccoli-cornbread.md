# Broccoli Cornbread

> A preserved family savory cornbread made with cornbread mix, chopped broccoli, onion, cottage cheese, eggs, milk, and melted margarine.

## Measured Ingredients

### Broccoli Cornbread

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 package | Cornbread mix |  |
| 1 | Onion | Chopped |
| 1 (10 oz) package | Chopped broccoli | Thawed |
| 3/4 of a 12 oz carton | Cottage cheese |  |
| 4 | Eggs |  |
| 1/3 cup | Milk |  |
| 1/2 cup | Margarine | Melted |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 400°F. |
| 2 | Mix all ingredients together. |
| 3 | Pour into a lightly greased casserole dish. |
| 4 | Bake for about 20-25 minutes, or until the cornbread is lightly browned. |
