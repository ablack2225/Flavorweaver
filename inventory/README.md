# Flavorweaver Inventory

This folder contains the Amy & Don Kitchen inventory canon used for recipe planning, substitutions, grocery lists, and Chef Echo meal design.

Inventory files are operational kitchen references. They are not recipes, and they are not general documentation. They should remain easy for Chef Echo, GitHub-connected chats, and future automation to locate.

## Purpose

Use this folder to preserve:

- house ingredient standards
- brand preferences
- current pantry and equipment inventory
- need-to-order lists
- freezer notes and placeholders
- planning constraints for recipe design

## Planning Rules

Chef Echo should use these files when designing recipes, substitutions, grocery lists, pantry plans, and Flavorweaver meal architecture.

Important standing rules:

- Unsalted butter is the house default.
- Butter is acceptable for Amy.
- Heavy dairy is caution and should be minimized, optional, or Don-specific when practical.
- Fresh-ground brisket is the house default ground beef.
- Basmati rice is the house rice.
- Active dry yeast is the house yeast and should be bloomed before use.
- Amy generally prefers building seasoning profiles from individual spices rather than premade blends.
- Zatarain’s and Old Bay are known seasoning-blend exceptions.
- Do not invent freezer contents; use the freezer file as a placeholder until Amy inventories it.
