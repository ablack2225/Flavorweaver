# Coconut Sour Cream Cake

> A preserved handwritten cake recipe from Sandy Hatton, known in the family as Aunt Sandy. Sue's family note on the card says this cake is good and rich.

### Coconut Cream Cheese Icing

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 tsp | Vanilla |  |

| 1 box | Powdered sugar | Box size not specified on handwritten card |

## Instructions

### Make the Cake

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 350°F. |
| 2 | Grease and flour a 13x9 pan. |
| 3 | Mix cake mix with cooking oil, eggs, sour cream, and 8 ounces cream of coconut. |
| 4 | Pour batter into prepared 13x9 pan. |
| 5 | Bake at 350°F for 30-45 minutes, or until cake tests done. |
| 6 | Cool before topping with icing mixture. |

### Make the Icing

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Combine vanilla, softened cream cheese, powdered sugar, cream of coconut, and frozen coconut. |
| 2 | Mix until smooth and spreadable. |
| 3 | Spread icing over cooled cake. |

## Notes

This handwritten recipe belongs to Sandy Hatton, known in the family as Aunt Sandy.

The handwritten card says the cake is good and rich.

The icing is preserved with the cake because it was written on the same card as Coconut Cream Cheese Icing.

Prep time, total time, servings, and cooling time are estimated. Bake time, pan size, and oven temperature are from the handwritten recipe.
