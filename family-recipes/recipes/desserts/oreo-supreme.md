# Oreo Supreme

> A preserved family frozen Oreo ice cream dessert with an Oreo crumb crust, softened vanilla ice cream, fudge topping, optional caramel topping, Cool Whip, and reserved Oreo crumbs.

## Measured Ingredients

### Oreo Supreme

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 (1 lb) package | Oreo cookies | Crush; reserve 1 cup crumbs for topping |
| 1/2 gallon | Vanilla ice cream | Softened |
| 1 (8 oz) carton | Cool Whip |  |
| 1 stick | Margarine | Melted |
| 16 oz | Fudge ice cream topping |  |
| 16 oz | Caramel ice cream topping | Optional |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Crush Oreos in a blender or food processor. |
| 2 | Reserve 1 cup of crumbs for the topping. |
| 3 | Melt margarine and mix with the remaining Oreo crumbs. |
| 4 | Press crumb mixture into the bottom of a 13x9x2-inch pan. |
| 5 | Spread softened vanilla ice cream on top. |
| 6 | Freeze until firm. |
| 7 | Remove from freezer and add the ice cream toppings desired. |
| 8 | Spread Cool Whip over the top. |
| 9 | Sprinkle reserved Oreo crumbs over the Cool Whip. |
| 10 | Freeze until ready to serve. |
| 11 | Slice with a knife and serve immediately, as it begins to melt if left out too long. |
