# Orange Salad Delight

> A preserved family orange gelatin salad made with Cool-Whip, cottage cheese, orange gelatin, mandarin oranges, and crushed pineapple.

## Ingredient List

| Component | Ingredients |
|---|---|
| Creamy Gelatin Base | Cool-Whip, cottage cheese, orange gelatin |
| Fruit | Mandarin orange slices, crushed pineapple |

## Measured Ingredients

### Orange Salad Delight

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 (8 oz) container | Cool-Whip |  |
| 1 (12 oz) carton | Cottage cheese |  |
| 1 small box | Orange gelatin |  |
| 1 small can | Mandarin orange slices |  |
| 1 small can | Crushed pineapple | Drained |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Mix all ingredients together until well blended. |
| 2 | Refrigerate for several hours before serving. |

Original recipe note: different flavors of gelatin and different types of fruit may be used in this recipe.
