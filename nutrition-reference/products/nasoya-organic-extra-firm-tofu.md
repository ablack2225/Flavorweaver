## Storage Notes

Keep refrigerated.

If the whole 14 oz block is not used, store the remaining tofu covered in water in the refrigerator, change the water daily, and use within 5 days.

## Verification Notes

Use weight-based calculations when possible because tofu block size, draining, pressing, and cutting can change practical serving portions.

If using another tofu brand, silken tofu, firm tofu, super-firm tofu, pre-marinated tofu, or baked tofu, create a separate product reference or mark the calculation as estimated pending label.

Micronutrients such as vitamin D, calcium, iron, and potassium may vary and are not included here unless a future label provides clear values.
