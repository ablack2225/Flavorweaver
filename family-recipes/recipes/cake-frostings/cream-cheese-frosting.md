# Cream Cheese Frosting

> A preserved family cream cheese frosting made with softened cream cheese, milk, vanilla, salt, and confectioners' sugar.

## Measured Ingredients

### Cream Cheese Frosting

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 (3 oz) package | Cream cheese | Softened |
| 1 Tbsp | Milk | Add more if needed, 1 tsp at a time |
| 1 tsp | Vanilla |  |
| Dash | Salt |  |
| 2 1/2 cups | Confectioners' sugar | Sifted |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Blend cream cheese, milk, vanilla, and salt together. |
| 2 | Gradually add the sifted confectioners' sugar. |
| 3 | Beat until the frosting is smooth and of spreading consistency. |
| 4 | If necessary, stir in additional milk, 1 teaspoon at a time. |
| 5 | Use to fill and frost cake. |
