# Pecan Sandies

> A preserved cookie recipe from Sue Wallesch, shared with her by Jean, one of Sue's house-cleaning customers. This clarified family-practice version uses butter pecan cake mix, eggs, oil, and chopped pecans.

## Measured Ingredients

### Pecan Sandies

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 box | Butter pecan cake mix | Preferably with pudding included |
| 1/2 cup | Vegetable oil |  |
| 2 large | Eggs |  |
| 1 cup | Chopped pecans |  |
| 1 small box / 3.4 oz | Instant vanilla or butterscotch pudding mix | Optional; add only for an extra rich, fudgy texture |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 350°F. |
| 2 | Line baking sheets with parchment paper. |
| 3 | In a large bowl, beat vegetable oil and eggs together until well combined. |
| 4 | Gradually add dry cake mix, and optional pudding mix if using, to the wet ingredients. Mix until fully incorporated. Dough will be very thick. |
| 5 | Fold in chopped pecans. |
| 6 | Scoop rounded tablespoons of dough and place 2 inches apart on prepared baking sheets. |
| 7 | Bake for 10-12 minutes, until edges are golden brown. |
| 8 | Cool cookies on the baking sheet for 1 minute. |
| 9 | Transfer to a wire rack to cool completely. |
