## Measured Ingredients

### Pumpkin Pie

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 | 10-inch unbaked pie crust | Confirmed by family practice |
| 2 | Eggs | Slightly beaten |
| 1 (16 oz) can | Pumpkin |  |
| 3/4 cup | Sugar |  |
| 1/2 tsp | Salt |  |
| 1 tsp | Ground cinnamon |  |
| 1/2 tsp | Ground ginger |  |
| 1/4 tsp | Ground cloves |  |
| 1 1/2 cups | Evaporated milk |  |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 425°F. |
| 2 | In a large bowl, combine all ingredients in the order listed. |
| 3 | Beat until thoroughly blended. |
| 4 | Pour filling into a 10-inch unbaked pie crust. |
| 5 | Bake at 425°F for 15 minutes. |
| 6 | Reduce oven temperature to 350°F. |
| 7 | Bake an additional 40-50 minutes, or until pie is set. |
| 8 | Check doneness by inserting a knife near the center of the pie. If it comes out clean, the pie is done. |

## Notes

Thanksgiving required family and pumpkin pie. This recipe came from Mama Hatton, was passed down to Sue, and Amy now carries the torch for keeping that tradition alive.

Jimmie and Sue were both known in the family for their pies. This pumpkin pie is preserved as part of that family pie tradition.

Family-practice clarification: use a 10-inch unbaked pie crust for this recipe.

The printed recipe lists 1 hour 15 minutes prep time. Bake times are from the recipe text. Servings and active prep time are estimated.
