## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Mix and bake the German chocolate cake mix according to the directions on the box. |
| 2 | Make either a layered cake or bake in a 13x9x2-inch baking pan, whichever is preferred. |
| 3 | Allow the cake to cool completely before frosting. |
| 4 | To make the frosting, combine evaporated milk, sugar, egg yolks, and margarine in a saucepan. |
| 5 | Cook over medium heat until thickened, about 12 minutes, stirring constantly to prevent scorching. |
| 6 | Mix in vanilla, coconut, and pecans. |
| 7 | Allow frosting to cool slightly until thickened. |
| 8 | Spread frosting on the cake. |
