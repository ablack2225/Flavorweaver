# Sweet Potato Casserole

> A preserved family side dish made with mashed sweet potatoes, sugar, warm spices, eggs, and a cornflake-brown sugar-nut topping.

## Ingredient List

| Component | Ingredients |
|---|---|

| Cornflake Topping | Crushed corn flakes, brown sugar, chopped nuts, melted margarine |

## Measured Ingredients

### Sweet Potato Base

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 2 cups | Sweet potatoes | Cooked and mashed |
| 3/4 cup | Sugar |  |
| 1/2 tsp | Vanilla |  |
| 2 | Eggs | Beaten |
| 1/4 tsp | Cinnamon |  |
| 1/2 tsp | Nutmeg |  |
| 6 Tbsp | Margarine | Melted |

### Cornflake Topping

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 cup | Corn flakes | Crushed |
| 1/2 cup | Brown sugar |  |
| 1/2 cup | Chopped nuts |  |
| 3/4 cup | Margarine | Melted |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 400°F. |
| 2 | Mix cooked mashed sweet potatoes, sugar, vanilla, beaten eggs, cinnamon, nutmeg, and 6 Tbsp melted margarine together until well combined. |
| 3 | Pour mixture into a greased baking dish. |
| 4 | Bake for 20 minutes. |
| 5 | While sweet potatoes are baking, make the topping by combining crushed corn flakes, brown sugar, chopped nuts, and 3/4 cup melted margarine. |
| 6 | Pour topping mixture over the sweet potatoes. |
| 7 | Return to oven and bake for 10 minutes. |
