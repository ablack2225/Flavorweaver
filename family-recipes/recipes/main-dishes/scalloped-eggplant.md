# Scalloped Eggplant

> A preserved family eggplant casserole made with diced eggplant, onion, packaged cornbread dressing mix, cream of mushroom soup, milk, egg, and American cheese slices.

|---:|---|---|

| 1/2 cup | Onion | Chopped |
| 3/4 cup | Packaged cornbread dressing mix |  |

| 1/2 cup | Milk |  |
| 1 | Egg |  |
| As needed | American cheese slices | Amount not specified |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 350°F. |
| 2 | Cook diced eggplant in a large pot of boiling water for 6-7 minutes; drain. |
| 3 | Place cooked eggplant in a greased casserole dish, approximately 8x8x2 inches. |
| 4 | Add chopped onion and packaged cornbread dressing mix. |
| 5 | Mix cream of mushroom soup, milk, and egg together. |
| 6 | Pour soup mixture over the eggplant mixture. |
| 7 | Stir together to mix all ingredients well. |
| 8 | Bake at 350°F for about 20 minutes. |
| 9 | Remove from oven and place American cheese slices on top. |
| 10 | Return to oven and bake an additional 10-15 minutes. |

## Notes

Although this recipe could lean side dish, it is filed under Main Dishes because the printed category says Main Dish and the casserole is structured as a stand-alone dish.

Original family note: this casserole tastes remarkably similar to cornbread dressing and was served with some of the traditional things that go with dressing. If you like dressing, this unusual recipe is worth trying.

The printed recipe lists 4 servings and 40 minutes prep time. Eggplant cook time and bake time are from the recipe text. The amount of American cheese slices is not specified in the original recipe.
