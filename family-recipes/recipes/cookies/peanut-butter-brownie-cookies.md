# Peanut Butter Brownie Cookies

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Heat oven to 350°F. |
| 2 | In a medium bowl, beat brownie mix, melted butter, softened cream cheese, and egg with a spoon for 50 strokes, until well blended. Dough will be sticky. |
| 3 | Drop dough by rounded tablespoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets, placing them 2 inches apart, to make 24 cookies. |
| 4 | Smooth the edge of each cookie to form a round cookie. |
| 5 | In a small bowl, mix powdered sugar and peanut butter with a spoon until the mixture forms a ball. |
| 6 | With hands, roll rounded teaspoonfuls of peanut butter mixture into 24 balls. |
| 7 | Lightly press 1 peanut butter ball into the center of each ball of dough. |
| 8 | Bake for 10-14 minutes, or until edges are set. |
| 9 | Cool cookies on cookie sheets at least 30 minutes. |
| 10 | Remove cooled cookies from cookie sheets. |
| 11 | Spread a thin layer of chocolate fudge frosting over the peanut butter portion of each cooled cookie. |
