# Chicken Tortilla Casserole

> A preserved family chicken tortilla casserole layered with torn corn tortillas, cooked chicken, cream soups, diced tomatoes and green chilies, onion, green bell pepper, and cheddar cheese.

## Measured Ingredients

### Chicken Tortilla Casserole

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1/4 cup | Margarine |  |
| 1 medium | Green bell pepper | Chopped |
| 1 medium | Onion | Chopped |
| 1 can | Cream of mushroom soup |  |
| 1 can | Cream of chicken soup |  |
| 1 can | Diced tomatoes and green chilies |  |
| 2 cups | Cooked chicken | Chopped |
| 12 | Corn tortillas | Torn into small pieces |
| 2 cups | Cheddar cheese | Grated |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 325°F. |
| 2 | Sauté onion and green pepper in margarine until tender. |
| 3 | Mix soups, tomatoes, chicken, and the onion-pepper mixture together. |
| 4 | Alternately layer tortilla pieces, soup mixture, and cheese. |
| 5 | Repeat for three layers, ending with cheese on top. |
| 6 | Bake about 40 minutes, or until hot and bubbling. |
