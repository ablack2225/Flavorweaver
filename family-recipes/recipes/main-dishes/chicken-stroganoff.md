# Chicken Stroganoff

> A preserved family chicken stroganoff recipe with browned chicken, Kitchen Bouquet, bouillon, thyme, cream of mushroom soup, sour cream, and a rich gravy served over creamed potatoes.

## Measured Ingredients

### Chicken Stroganoff

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 large | Chicken | Cut into serving pieces |
| 2 tsp | Kitchen Bouquet seasoning | For brushing chicken |
| 3/4 cup | Cooking oil |  |
| 3/4 cup | Margarine |  |
| 2 | Chicken bouillon cubes |  |
| 3/4 tsp | Thyme |  |
| 1 cup | Water | Handwritten ingredient on original page |
| 1 can | Cream of mushroom soup |  |
| 1 cup | Sour cream |  |
| To taste | Salt and pepper |  |
| As needed, optional | Milk | To thin gravy if needed |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Remove skin from chicken pieces and wash thoroughly. |
| 2 | Lightly sprinkle chicken with salt. |
| 3 | Brush chicken with Kitchen Bouquet seasoning. |
| 4 | Heat cooking oil and margarine in a large skillet. |
| 5 | Add chicken pieces and cook over moderate heat until each side is lightly brown. |
| 6 | Combine water, bouillon cubes, and thyme. |
| 7 | Pour broth mixture over the chicken. |
| 8 | Cover and cook about 15 minutes. |
| 9 | Remove chicken from the skillet. |
| 10 | Combine cream of mushroom soup and sour cream; season with black pepper, if desired. |
| 11 | Pour soup mixture into the juices left from cooking the chicken and mix well. |
| 12 | If gravy is too thick, add a little milk to thin it. |
| 13 | Place chicken back in the gravy, turning to coat all sides. |
| 14 | Serve over creamed potatoes. |

## Notes

Original family note: this makes the most wonderful, rich gravy. Sue's kids always loved when she prepared this dish, and it was truly one of the family favorites over the years.

The handwritten 1 cup water ingredient is included because the instructions combine water with the bouillon and thyme.

The printed recipe lists 4 servings and 45 minutes prep time. Cook time and total time are estimated from the browning and simmer method.
