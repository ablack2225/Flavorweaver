Butter Chicken over Basmati Rice

Butter chicken served over basmati rice with a creamy tomato-cashew sauce, marinated chicken, and a butter-based finish.

Meal TypeMain Dish
TotalAbout 1 hr, plus marination
Serves4 servings
ServiceBasmati Rice
butter chickenchicken makhanibasmati ricetomato cashew saucecream saucemarinated chicken

Kitchen Standards

Butter Only

Use butter only; no ghee.

Tomatoes

Use about 1.3 lb fresh tomatoes.

Cashew Base

Use cashews for the sauce base.

Basmati Service

Serve over basmati rice.

Cooking Structure Summary

First Marinade

Seasons chicken with chili, salt, and lemon before the yogurt marinade.

Second Marinade

Adds yogurt, ginger-garlic, spices, kasuri methi, and oil for deeper flavor.

Tomato-Cashew Sauce

Builds a creamy tomato sauce with cashews, butter, spices, and cream.

Finish & Serve

Chicken finishes in the sauce, cream and kasuri methi round it out, and service is hot basmati rice.

Flavorweaver Cook Notes

Use butter only; do not use ghee.

Use about 1.3 lb fresh tomatoes rather than gram-based measurement.

Use cashews for the sauce base.

Serve over basmati rice.

Do Not Forget

Do not use ghee.

Do not swap cashews for almonds unless intentionally testing a new version.

Keep the basmati rice service note.
