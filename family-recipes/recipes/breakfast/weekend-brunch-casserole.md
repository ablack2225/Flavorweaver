# Weekend Brunch Casserole

> A preserved family breakfast casserole made with pork sausage, crescent rolls, cheddar cheese, jalapenos, eggs, and milk.

## Measured Ingredients

### Weekend Brunch Casserole

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 lb | Ground pork sausage | Hot sausage may be used |
| 1/2 cup | Chopped onion | Optional |
| 2-3 | Jalapeno peppers | Seeded and chopped; optional |
| 1 can | Crescent rolls | Press perforations together to form crust |
| 2 cups | Grated cheddar cheese |  |
| 1/2 tsp | Salt |  |
| 1/8 tsp | Black pepper |  |
| 4 | Eggs | Beaten |
| 3/4 cup | Milk |  |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 425°F. |
| 2 | Crumble sausage into a medium skillet and add the onion and jalapeno peppers. |
| 3 | Brown sausage mixture and drain well. |
| 4 | Line the bottom of a greased 13x9x2-inch baking dish with crescent rolls, pressing the perforations together to seal and form a crust. |
| 5 | Sprinkle the sausage mixture and cheddar cheese over the crust. |
| 6 | Mix the remaining ingredients together. |
| 7 | Pour egg mixture over the meat and cheese. |
| 8 | Bake for 15 minutes, or until eggs are set. |
| 9 | Let stand for 5 minutes. |
| 10 | Cut into squares and serve hot. |
