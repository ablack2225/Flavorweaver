# Chocolate Dream Cake

> A preserved family chocolate cake made with cocoa, buttermilk, and a warm chocolate-pecan frosting spread over the cake while still warm.

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 400°F. |
| 2 | Combine flour and sugar in a mixing bowl. |
| 3 | In a small saucepan, combine margarine, shortening, water, and cocoa. Bring to a boil. |
| 4 | Pour the hot cocoa mixture over the flour-sugar mixture and mix well. |
| 5 | Combine the remaining cake ingredients and add all at once to the batter, mixing well. |
| 6 | Pour into a greased, lightly floured 13x9x2-inch baking pan. |
| 7 | Bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the cake tests done. |
| 8 | Let cake cool 10-15 minutes while preparing frosting. |
| 9 | For frosting, combine margarine, cocoa, and milk in a saucepan. Heat slowly and bring to a boil. |
| 10 | Add powdered sugar, vanilla, and chopped pecans. Beat well. |
| 11 | Spread frosting over the cake while it is still warm. |
| 12 | Leave cake in pan until ready to serve. Cut into squares. |
