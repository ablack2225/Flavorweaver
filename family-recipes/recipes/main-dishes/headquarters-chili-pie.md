# Headquarters Chili Pie

> A preserved family chili pie recipe made with seasoned ground beef, tomatoes, Ranch Style Beans, and a baked cornbread-cheddar topping.

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | In a large stock pot, lightly brown ground beef, onion, and garlic in cooking oil, stirring as needed. |
| 2 | Add canned tomatoes, diced tomatoes and chilies, chili powder, red pepper flakes, cumin, and beef bouillon. |
| 3 | Bring to a boil. Cover and simmer 45 minutes. |
| 4 | Add Ranch Style Beans and cook another 20 minutes. |
| 5 | Spoon chili into a 13x9-inch baking dish. |
| 6 | Heat chili in a 375°F oven for 15 minutes. |
| 7 | While chili is heating, make the cornbread crust. Combine cornmeal, flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt; mix well. |
| 8 | Beat eggs and milk together. |
| 9 | Add egg mixture to the cornmeal mixture along with melted margarine and grated cheddar cheese. Stir until well mixed. |
| 10 | Remove chili from oven and spoon the cornbread mixture evenly over the top. |
| 11 | Return to oven and bake 30-35 minutes, or until cornbread is brown. |
