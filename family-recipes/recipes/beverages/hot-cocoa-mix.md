## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Combine sugar, dry milk powder, and cocoa in a large bowl. Mix well. |
| 2 | Store the mix in an airtight container. |
| 3 | To serve, place 1/4 cup of mix in a cup. |
| 4 | Pour in 1 cup boiling water and stir well. |
