# Sour Cream Chicken Enchiladas

> A preserved family chicken enchilada recipe made with corn tortillas, cooked chicken, cream of chicken soup, chopped green chilies, sour cream, onion, and Monterey Jack cheese.

## Measured Ingredients

### Sour Cream Chicken Enchiladas

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 12 | Corn tortillas | Softened briefly in oil |
| 1 | Onion | Chopped |
| 2 cans | Cream of chicken soup |  |
| 1 can | Chopped green chilies |  |
| 1 (8 oz) carton | Sour cream |  |
| 2 cups | Monterey Jack cheese | Grated; divided |
| 2-3 cups | Cooked chicken | Deboned |
| Small amount | Cooking oil | For softening tortillas |
| Small amount, optional | Milk | Use only if sauce is too thick |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 350°F. |
| 2 | Soften tortillas in a skillet in a small amount of cooking oil for just a second or two on each side; drain. |
| 3 | Mix cream of chicken soup, sour cream, and chopped green chilies. |
| 4 | If the sauce is too thick, add a small amount of milk to thin. |
| 5 | Place chicken, chopped onion, and cheese in the middle of each tortilla and roll up. |
| 6 | Spread a small amount of the soup mixture in the bottom of a large greased casserole dish to keep the enchiladas from sticking. |
| 7 | Place enchiladas seam-side down in the prepared dish. |
| 8 | Pour remaining soup mixture over the top. |
| 9 | Sprinkle with grated Monterey Jack cheese. |
| 10 | Bake at 350°F for about 30 minutes. |
| 11 | Enjoy. |
