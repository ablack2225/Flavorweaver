# Neverland Cookies

## Measured Ingredients

### Neverland Cookies

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 2 1/4 cups | All-purpose flour |  |
| 1 tsp | Baking powder |  |
| 1 tsp | Baking soda |  |
| 1 tsp | Salt |  |
| 1 1/2 cups / about 14 oz | Creamy peanut butter | Peter Pan style, as listed on clipping |
| 1 cup / 8 oz | Stick margarine | Softened; Blue Bonnet style, as listed on clipping |
| 1 cup | Granulated sugar |  |
| 1 cup | Brown sugar | Firmly packed |
| 1 tsp | Vanilla |  |
| 2 large | Eggs | Slightly beaten |
| 1 (12 oz) bag | White morsels | About 2 cups |
| 1 (6.5 oz) jar | Macadamia nuts | Coarsely chopped; about 1 1/3 cups |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 350°F. |
| 2 | Blend flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a small bowl; set aside. |
| 3 | Place peanut butter, softened margarine, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla in a large mixing bowl. |
| 4 | Beat peanut butter-sugar mixture with an electric mixer until creamy. |
| 5 | Add eggs and mix until well blended. |
| 6 | Gradually add flour mixture. |
| 7 | Stir in white morsels and chopped macadamia nuts. |
| 8 | Drop batter by rounded tablespoons onto an ungreased cookie sheet, 1 1/2 inches apart. |
| 9 | Bake for 15-16 minutes. |
| 10 | Cool 5 minutes on cookie sheet. |
| 11 | Remove to wire rack and cool completely. |
