# Cream of Broccoli Soup

> A preserved family cream of broccoli soup made with onion, frozen broccoli, chicken bouillon, milk or half-and-half, and optional Velveeta or Cheddar cheese.

## Measured Ingredients

### Cream of Broccoli Soup

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1/2 cup | Onion | Chopped |
| 2 Tbsp | Margarine |  |
| 1/4 cup | Flour |  |
| 2 cups | Frozen broccoli | Chopped |
| 3 | Chicken bouillon cubes | Dissolved in hot water |
| 1/4 tsp | Black pepper |  |
| 1 cup | Milk | Sue notes she likes to use half-and-half |
| 2 cups | Water | Hot, for dissolving bouillon |
| Optional | Velveeta or Cheddar cheese | Adds a special touch |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Sauté onion in margarine in the microwave for about 5 minutes, until onion is tender. |
| 2 | Stir in flour and black pepper. |
| 3 | Dissolve chicken bouillon cubes in hot water. |
| 4 | Gradually mix bouillon into the onion mixture, stirring to make smooth. |
| 5 | Add chopped frozen broccoli. |
| 6 | Cover and microwave on high power for about 10-12 minutes, stirring about halfway through. |
| 7 | Stir in milk or half-and-half. |
| 8 | Add Cheddar or Velveeta cheese, if desired. |

## Notes

Sue noted that she liked to use half-and-half in place of milk for this soup.

Optional Velveeta or Cheddar cheese adds a special touch to the soup.
