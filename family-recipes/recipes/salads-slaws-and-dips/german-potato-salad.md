# German Potato Salad

> A preserved family German potato salad made with baked potatoes, onion, celery, radishes, mustard, vinegar, celery seed, parsley, mayonnaise, salt, and black pepper.

## Measured Ingredients

### German Potato Salad

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| About 10 large | Baking potatoes | Washed, wrapped in foil, baked until tender |
| 1 medium | Onion | Chopped |
| 2-3 stalks | Celery | Chopped |
| 9-10 | Fresh radishes | Sliced thin |
| 1 tsp | Prepared mustard |  |
| 1 Tbsp | White vinegar |  |
| 1 tsp | Celery seeds |  |
| 2 Tbsp | Parsley flakes |  |
| 1-2 tsp | Salt | To taste |
| To taste | Black pepper |  |
| About 1 cup | Mayonnaise | Add more if mixture is too dry |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Wash potatoes and wrap in foil. |
| 2 | Bake at 375°F until potatoes are tender. |
| 3 | In a large mixing bowl, combine chopped onion, celery, sliced radishes, prepared mustard, mayonnaise, celery seeds, parsley, vinegar, salt, and pepper. |
| 4 | Blend dressing mixture until well combined. |
| 5 | Peel potatoes while still warm and cut into cubes. |
| 6 | Blend mayonnaise mixture into the potatoes a little at a time until all potatoes have been mixed in. |
| 7 | Stir gently to blend all ingredients. |
| 8 | Add additional mayonnaise if the mixture is too dry. |
| 9 | For best flavor, prepare in advance and allow time to marinate before eating. |
| 10 | Store in the refrigerator. This salad keeps for several days. |

## Notes

This recipe came from Rudy Sr., Sue's father-in-law, and carries more than just a potato salad method. He was remembered as a truly good cook who passed his love and secrets of great barbecue down to Rudy. This recipe preserves a piece of that family table.

Original family note: this recipe was given to Sue by her father-in-law, Rudy Sr., many years ago. He was truly a good cook, and he taught Rudy all his secrets about cooking great barbecue.

Original hint: this potato salad is better if prepared in advance and given time to marinate before eating. It will keep several days in the refrigerator.

Servings, prep time, bake time, and total time are estimated. Several vegetable amounts are preserved in the flexible style of the original recipe.
