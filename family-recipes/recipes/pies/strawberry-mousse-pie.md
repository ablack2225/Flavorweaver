## Measured Ingredients

### Strawberry Mousse Pie

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 | Graham cracker pie crust | GV style, as listed on clipping |
| 1 packet | Unflavored gelatin |  |
| 1/4 cup | Cold water | For softening gelatin |
| 2 cups | Strawberries | Hulled and sliced |
| 1/4 cup | Extra fine granulated sugar | GV style, as listed on clipping |
| 1 cup | Heavy cream | Chilled |
| 3 Tbsp | Powdered sugar | GV style, as listed on clipping |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Sprinkle gelatin over cold water in a saucepan. Let stand for 1 minute to soften. |
| 2 | Stir over low heat until gelatin is dissolved, about 1 minute. |
| 3 | Remove from heat. |
| 4 | Place strawberries, extra fine granulated sugar, and gelatin mixture in a food processor or blender. |
| 5 | Whirl to puree. |
| 6 | Pour strawberry puree into a bowl. |
| 7 | Chill until mixture mounds when dropped from a spoon, about 1 hour. |
| 8 | Beat chilled heavy cream and powdered sugar in a chilled bowl until soft peaks form. |
| 9 | Stir one-fourth of the whipped cream into the strawberry puree to lighten the mixture. |
| 10 | Fold in remaining whipped cream. |
| 11 | Pour mousse mixture into graham cracker crust. |
| 12 | Refrigerate for 2 hours before serving. |
