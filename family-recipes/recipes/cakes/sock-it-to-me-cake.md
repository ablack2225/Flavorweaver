# Sock-it-to-me Cake

> A preserved family Bundt-style cake made with butter recipe cake mix, sour cream, cinnamon-sugar filling, optional nuts, and a simple confectioners' sugar glaze.

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Preheat oven to 375°F. |
| 2 | In a small bowl, combine reserved cake mix, cinnamon, brown sugar, and nuts if desired. Set aside. |
| 3 | In a large bowl, blend cake mix, sour cream, oil, sugar, water, and eggs. |
| 4 | Beat at high speed for 2 minutes. |
| 5 | Pour 2/3 of the batter into a greased and lightly floured tube or Bundt pan. |
| 6 | Sprinkle the cinnamon-sugar mixture over the batter in the pan. |
| 7 | Spread the remaining batter evenly over the filling mixture. |
| 8 | Bake for 45-55 minutes, until the cake springs back when touched lightly. |
| 9 | Cool right side up for about 25 minutes. |
| 10 | Remove from pan onto a serving platter and cool completely. |
| 11 | Make a glaze with confectioners' sugar and milk. |
| 12 | Drizzle glaze over the cake. |
