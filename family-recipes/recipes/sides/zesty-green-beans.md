## Measured Ingredients

### Zesty Green Beans

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 Tbsp | Vegetable oil |  |
| 1 lb | Green beans | Trimmed |
| 1 large | Onion | Chopped |
| 2 cloves | Garlic | Minced |
| 1 (10 oz) can | Diced tomatoes and green chilies | RO*TEL style |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | In a large skillet, heat vegetable oil. |
| 2 | Add green beans, chopped onion, and minced garlic. |
| 3 | Cook and stir until vegetables are tender-crisp. |
| 4 | Stir in diced tomatoes and green chilies. |
| 5 | Simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. |
