## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Brown hamburger meat and drain. |
| 2 | Put cabbage, onion, and bell pepper on top of the browned meat. |
| 3 | Cover and cook on low until vegetables are tender. |
| 4 | Add stewed tomatoes. |
| 5 | Add salt, pepper, and sugar to taste. |
