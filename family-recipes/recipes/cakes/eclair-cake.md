# Eclair Cake

> A preserved family dessert from Rudy Wallesch, made with vanilla pudding, whipped topping, graham cracker layers, and chocolate frosting.

## Measured Ingredients

### Eclair Cake

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 2 (3.4 oz) packages | Instant vanilla pudding mix |  |
| 3 1/2 cups | Milk |  |
| 1 (8 oz) container | Whipped topping | Thawed |
| 1 box | Graham crackers | Enough for three layers in a 9x13 pan |
| 1 can | Dark chocolate frosting | Prepared frosting |

## Equipment

| Item | Notes |
|---|---|
| 9x13 baking pan | For layering the cake |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | In a medium bowl, whisk together instant vanilla pudding mix and milk until smooth. |
| 2 | Fold in thawed whipped topping. |
| 3 | Line the bottom of a 9x13 pan with a single layer of graham crackers, breaking crackers as needed to fill the layer. |
| 4 | Pour half of the pudding mixture over the crackers and spread evenly. |
| 5 | Add a second layer of graham crackers. |
| 6 | Pour remaining pudding mixture over the second layer of crackers and spread evenly. |
| 7 | Top with a third layer of graham crackers. |
| 8 | Carefully spread chocolate frosting over the top layer of graham crackers. |
| 9 | Cover and refrigerate at least 8 hours or overnight. |
| 10 | Serve chilled. |
| 11 | Store leftovers covered in the refrigerator. |

## Notes

This recipe was preserved from Rudy Wallesch's handwritten recipe note and Amy's clarified family-practice version.

Amy noted that the handwritten version should read as two boxes of vanilla pudding.

This cake is best after chilling long enough for the graham crackers to soften. It may be made up to 2 days ahead.

Store leftovers covered in the refrigerator for up to 4 days.

To freeze, wrap tightly in a double layer of foil and plastic. Label, date, and freeze for up to 2 months. Thaw overnight in the refrigerator.

Prep time and total time are estimated. Chill time and pan size are documented.
