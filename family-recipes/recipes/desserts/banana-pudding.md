# Banana Pudding

> A preserved family no-bake banana pudding made with bananas, vanilla wafers, sweetened condensed milk, vanilla instant pudding, and Cool Whip.

## Measured Ingredients

### Banana Pudding

| Amount | Ingredient | Notes |
|---:|---|---|
| 4 large | Bananas | Slice for layering |
| 1 box | Vanilla wafers |  |
| 1 can | Sweetened condensed milk |  |
| 1 1/2 cups | Cold water |  |
| 1 small box | Vanilla instant pudding |  |
| 1 (8 oz) carton | Cool Whip | Thawed |
| Extra, optional | Vanilla wafers | For decorating the top, if desired |

## Instructions

| Step | Action |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Combine cold water and sweetened condensed milk. |
| 2 | Gradually whisk in vanilla pudding, being careful not to mix too much or the pudding will become too thick. The mixture should be very thin. |
| 3 | Fold in Cool Whip. |
| 4 | Layer bananas, vanilla wafers, and pudding mixture in a large dish, ending with the last of the pudding mixture on top. |
| 5 | Add extra cookies on top for decoration, if desired. |
| 6 | Chill before serving. |
