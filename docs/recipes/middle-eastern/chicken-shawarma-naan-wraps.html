Chicken Shawarma Naan Wraps with Garlic Sauce and Lemon Tahini

Deeply spiced chicken cooked hot, sliced thin, and re-crisped into shawarma-style edges, wrapped in warm homemade naan with garlic sauce, lemon tahini, crisp potatoes, pickles, tomato, cucumber, onion, and herbs.

Kitchen Execution Summary

Complete Wrap

This card covers the full wrap: shawarma chicken, required warm naan, garlic sauce, lemon tahini, crispy potatoes, pickled acid, fresh vegetables, and final griddle set.

Shawarma Chicken

Whole chicken pieces are marinated, cooked hot, rested, sliced thin, and re-crisped so the wrap has shawarma-style edges.

Required Naan

Fluffy Skillet Naan is the preferred house wrap base and should be made or warmed as part of the meal flow.

Required Sauce Balance

Garlic sauce is the primary chicken-shawarma sauce. Lemon tahini adds a nutty finishing stripe, not a heavy blanket.

Before Cooking / Advance Prep

Marinade timing — Marinate the chicken 12 to 18 hours before cooking. Do not extend the lemon-garlic marinade beyond 24 hours.

Dry spice blend — Mix the shawarma spice blend 1 to 2 days ahead and keep it covered.

Sauces — Make the garlic sauce and lemon tahini up to 1 day ahead; refrigerate and stir before using.

Required naan component — If making Fluffy Skillet Naan, refrigerate the dough after the first rise, then bring it to room temperature before shaping and cooking.

Service Countdown

1 to 2 days ahead: mix the dry shawarma spice blend with 1 tsp kosher salt, 2 tsp cumin, 1 tsp coriander, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1/2 tsp allspice, 1/4 tsp cardamom, 1/4 tsp cinnamon, 1/4 tsp ginger, tiny pinch nutmeg, 1/2 tsp black pepper, and 1/8 to 1/4 tsp cayenne.

Up to 1 day ahead: make garlic sauce and lemon tahini; chill both sauces.

12 to 18 hours before cooking: marinate 1 lb chicken with the full measured marinade.

60 minutes before serving: cook crispy potatoes or fries; stir sauces and prep fresh vegetables.

15 minutes before serving: re-crisp sliced chicken and warm 3 to 4 naan.

At service: build wraps with garlic sauce, chicken, potatoes, pickles, fresh vegetables, lemon tahini, and herbs.

Measured Ingredients by Component

These component sections are reference only. The cooking flow is handled by the stage-based prep bowls and full timeline below.

Shawarma Chicken Marinade

1 lb chicken; 2 Tbsp olive oil or neutral oil; 1 Tbsp lemon juice; 1 tsp lemon zest; 4 grated garlic cloves; 1 tsp kosher salt; 2 tsp ground cumin; 1 tsp ground coriander; 1 tsp smoked paprika; 1/2 tsp turmeric; 1/2 tsp allspice; 1/4 tsp ground cardamom; 1/4 tsp cinnamon; 1/4 tsp ground ginger; tiny pinch nutmeg; 1/2 tsp black pepper; 1/8 to 1/4 tsp cayenne; 1 tsp honey or brown sugar; optional 1 Tbsp Greek yogurt.

Toum-Style Garlic Sauce

1/4 cup mayonnaise; 2 Tbsp Greek yogurt or sour cream; 1 to 2 grated garlic cloves; 1 to 2 tsp lemon juice; 1 tsp olive oil; pinch kosher salt; tiny pinch sugar or honey; 1 to 2 tsp cold water only if needed.

Lemon Tahini Sauce

1/4 cup tahini; 1 1/2 Tbsp lemon juice; 1 small grated garlic clove; 1/4 tsp kosher salt; 1/8 tsp ground cumin; 3 to 5 Tbsp ice-cold water; tiny drizzle honey only if bitter.

Crispy Potato Tuck-In

2 medium potatoes, cut into 1/4-inch fries or small wedges, or frozen fries; 1 to 2 Tbsp neutral oil; 1/2 tsp kosher salt; optional 1/2 tsp paprika; optional 1/4 tsp garlic powder.

Fresh Wrap Fill and Assembly

3 to 4 warm naan, preferably Fluffy Skillet Naan; 1 medium tomato; 1/2 cucumber; 1/4 to 1/2 small onion; 1/2 cup pickles or pickled onions; small handful parsley or cilantro; optional lemon wedges.

Cross-Component Prep Map

Garlic: measure 4 cloves for the chicken marinade, 1 to 2 cloves for the garlic sauce, and 1 small clove for the lemon tahini.

Lemon: use 1 Tbsp juice and 1 tsp zest in the marinade, 1 to 2 tsp juice in the garlic sauce, and 1 1/2 Tbsp juice in the tahini.

Naan: warm 3 to 4 naan at service. Do not fill cold naan.

Pickles or pickled onions: drain 1/2 cup very well so the wrap stays crisp instead of soggy.

Do Not Forget

Marinate the chicken 12 to 18 hours before cooking.

Keep the lemon juice controlled at 1 Tbsp for the overnight chicken marinade.

Fluffy Skillet Naan is the required house bread component unless using prepared naan.

Cook chicken whole, rest, slice thin, then re-crisp for shawarma-style edges.

Garlic sauce is the primary sauce; lemon tahini is a thin stripe.

Drain pickles and wet toppings before assembly.

Full Cooking Timeline and Instructions

  1. Mix the dry shawarma spice blend. In a small bowl, combine 1 tsp kosher salt, 2 tsp ground cumin, 1 tsp ground coriander, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1/2 tsp allspice, 1/4 tsp ground cardamom, 1/4 tsp cinnamon, 1/4 tsp ground ginger, a tiny pinch nutmeg, 1/2 tsp black pepper, and 1/8 to 1/4 tsp cayenne.
  2. Make the chicken marinade. Whisk 2 Tbsp oil, 1 Tbsp lemon juice, 1 tsp lemon zest, 4 grated garlic cloves, 1 tsp honey or brown sugar, optional 1 Tbsp Greek yogurt, and the full spice blend.
  3. Marinate the chicken. Add 1 lb chicken, turn until coated, keep pieces whole, cover, and refrigerate 12 to 18 hours.
  4. Make the garlic sauce. Stir 1/4 cup mayonnaise, 2 Tbsp Greek yogurt or sour cream, 1 to 2 grated garlic cloves, 1 to 2 tsp lemon juice, 1 tsp olive oil, a pinch of salt, and a tiny pinch of sugar or honey. Thin with 1 to 2 tsp cold water only if needed.
  5. Make the lemon tahini. Whisk 1/4 cup tahini, 1 1/2 Tbsp lemon juice, 1 small grated garlic clove, 1/4 tsp salt, and 1/8 tsp cumin. Add 3 to 5 Tbsp ice-cold water, 1 Tbsp at a time, until pourable.
  6. Prepare the required naan. Make or warm 3 to 4 naan. For the house version, use Fluffy Skillet Naan. Keep naan covered and warm.
  7. Prepare crispy potatoes. Cut 2 medium potatoes into 1/4-inch fries or small wedges, or use frozen fries. Toss fresh potatoes with 1 to 2 Tbsp oil, 1/2 tsp salt, optional 1/2 tsp paprika, and optional 1/4 tsp garlic powder. Cook until crisp.
  8. Prep the fresh and pickled fill. Slice 1 tomato, 1/2 cucumber, and 1/4 to 1/2 onion. Chop herbs if using. Drain 1/2 cup pickles or pickled onions very well.
  9. Cook the chicken hot. Remove chicken from the refrigerator 20 to 30 minutes before cooking. Cook in a hot skillet, griddle, broiler, grill pan, or electric skillet until browned and cooked through, about 4 to 6 minutes per side.
  10. Rest and slice the chicken. Rest cooked chicken 5 to 10 minutes. Slice very thin across the grain into shawarma-style strips. Save the juices.
  11. Re-crisp the sliced chicken. Return sliced chicken and saved juices to a hot skillet for 1 to 2 minutes, just until edges pick up color.
  12. Warm the naan for assembly. Warm 3 to 4 naan until soft and flexible. Do not fill cold naan.
  13. Build each wrap with measured components. For each warm naan, spread about 1 Tbsp garlic sauce, add 1/3 to 1/4 of the hot sliced chicken, about 1/4 cup crispy potatoes, 1 to 2 Tbsp drained pickles or pickled onions, tomato, cucumber, onion, herbs, another 1 to 2 tsp garlic sauce, and a thin 1 to 2 tsp stripe lemon tahini.
  14. Set the wrap and serve. Fold or roll the naan. Optional: griddle seam-side down for 30 to 60 seconds. Serve immediately.

Building the Final Wrap

1. Required Warm Naan

Use 1 warm naan per wrap, preferably Fluffy Skillet Naan.

2. Garlic Sauce Foundation

Spread about 1 Tbsp garlic sauce over each naan.

3. Hot Shawarma Chicken

Add 1/3 to 1/4 of the thin-sliced re-crisped chicken.

4. Crispy Potato Tuck-In

Add about 1/4 cup crispy potatoes per wrap.

5. Pickled Acid

Add 1 to 2 Tbsp drained pickles or pickled onions.

6. Fresh Crunch

Add tomato, cucumber, onion, and herbs in controlled amounts.

7. Sauce Finish

Add 1 to 2 tsp garlic sauce and a thin 1 to 2 tsp lemon tahini stripe.

8. Wrap and Griddle

Fold tightly and griddle seam-side down if desired. Serve immediately.

Final Taste Target

The finished wrap should taste juicy, deeply spiced, garlicky, lemon-bright, lightly smoky, tangy from pickles, creamy from sauce, nutty from a thin tahini stripe, and fresh from vegetables and herbs. The chicken should have browned edges and a sliced shawarma texture; the naan should stay warm and flexible.

Chef's Notes

Tahini is useful here, but it should not replace the garlic sauce. For chicken shawarma, the garlic sauce is the main sauce; lemon tahini is a nutty finishing stripe.

This recipe succeeds because it is not just spiced chicken in bread. It is built from required components: hot sliced shawarma chicken, warm naan, garlic sauce, pickled acid, crisp potato texture, fresh vegetables, and controlled tahini.
