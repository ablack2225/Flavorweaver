Chicken Shawarma Naan Wraps with Garlic Sauce and Lemon Tahini

Spiced chicken thighs, required warm naan, garlic sauce, lemon tahini, crisp potatoes, pickles, tomato, cucumber, onion, and herbs.

Meal TypeComplete Wrap
Total1h 45m active + marinade
Yield3 to 4 wraps
StatusFlavorweaver Test Recipe
chicken shawarmanaan wrapgarlic saucelemon tahinicrispy potatoes

Kitchen Execution Summary

Shawarma Chicken

Marinate whole thighs, cook, rest, slice thin, then re-crisp.

Required Naan

Fluffy Skillet Naan is the preferred house wrap base.

Sauce Bar

Garlic sauce is primary; lemon tahini is a thin stripe.

Acid + Crunch

Pickles or pickled onions plus crisp potatoes are required for balance.

Required Components

Required bread: Fluffy Skillet Naan, 3 to 4 pieces. Use prepared naan only for first-test practicality.

Embedded required components: chicken shawarma thighs, toum-style garlic sauce, lemon tahini sauce, crispy potato tuck-in, and pickled finish.

Before Cooking Day / Advance Prep

For Sunday service: marinate Saturday evening, 12 to 18 hours before cooking. Friday prep should be limited to the dry spice blend and sauces.

Naan: if making house naan, make dough Saturday and refrigerate after first rise; bring out Sunday before shaping and cooking.

Measured Ingredients by Component

Shawarma Chicken Marinade

1 lb chicken thighs; 2 Tbsp oil; 1 Tbsp lemon juice; 1 tsp lemon zest; 4 grated garlic cloves; 1 tsp kosher salt; 2 tsp cumin; 1 tsp coriander; 1 tsp smoked paprika; 1/2 tsp turmeric; 1/2 tsp allspice; 1/4 tsp cardamom; 1/4 tsp cinnamon; 1/4 tsp ginger; tiny pinch nutmeg; 1/2 tsp black pepper; 1/8 to 1/4 tsp cayenne; 1 tsp honey or brown sugar; optional 1 Tbsp Greek yogurt.

Toum-Style Garlic Sauce

1/4 cup mayonnaise; 2 Tbsp Greek yogurt or sour cream; 1 to 2 grated garlic cloves; 1 to 2 tsp lemon juice; 1 tsp olive oil; pinch kosher salt; tiny pinch sweetener; 1 to 2 tsp cold water only if needed.

Lemon Tahini

1/4 cup tahini; 1 1/2 Tbsp lemon juice; 1 small grated garlic clove; 1/4 tsp kosher salt; 1/8 tsp cumin; 3 to 5 Tbsp ice-cold water; tiny drizzle honey only if bitter.

Wrap Fill

3 to 4 warm naan; 2 medium potatoes or frozen fries; 1 tomato; 1/2 cucumber; 1/4 to 1/2 small onion; 1/2 cup pickles or pickled onions; small handful herbs; optional lemon wedges.

Stage-Based Prep Bowls

Bowl 1 — Chicken

1 lb chicken thighs, left whole.

Bowl 2 — Wet Base

2 Tbsp oil; 1 Tbsp lemon juice; 1 tsp lemon zest; 4 grated garlic cloves; 1 tsp honey or brown sugar; optional 1 Tbsp Greek yogurt.

Bowl 3 — Spice Blend

1 tsp salt; 2 tsp cumin; 1 tsp coriander; 1 tsp smoked paprika; 1/2 tsp turmeric; 1/2 tsp allspice; 1/4 tsp cardamom; 1/4 tsp cinnamon; 1/4 tsp ginger; tiny pinch nutmeg; 1/2 tsp black pepper; 1/8 to 1/4 tsp cayenne.

Bowl 4 — Garlic Sauce

1/4 cup mayo; 2 Tbsp yogurt or sour cream; 1 to 2 garlic cloves; 1 to 2 tsp lemon juice; 1 tsp olive oil; salt; tiny sweetener.

Bowl 5 — Lemon Tahini

1/4 cup tahini; 1 1/2 Tbsp lemon juice; 1 small garlic clove; 1/4 tsp salt; 1/8 tsp cumin; 3 to 5 Tbsp ice water.

Bowl 6 — Required Naan

3 to 4 warm naan, preferably Fluffy Skillet Naan.

Bowl 7 — Crispy Potatoes

2 medium potatoes; 1 to 2 Tbsp oil; 1/2 tsp salt; optional 1/2 tsp paprika; optional 1/4 tsp garlic powder.

Bowl 8 — Fresh + Pickled Finish

1 tomato; 1/2 cucumber; 1/4 to 1/2 onion; herbs; 1/2 cup drained pickles or pickled onions.

Full Cooking Timeline and Instructions

  1. Mix spice blend: combine the full measured Bowl 3 spice blend.
  2. Saturday evening: whisk Bowl 2 wet base with Bowl 3 spice blend, coat 1 lb chicken thighs, and refrigerate 12 to 18 hours.
  3. Make garlic sauce: mix Bowl 4 and chill.
  4. Make tahini: mix Bowl 5, adding ice water 1 Tbsp at a time until pourable.
  5. Prepare naan: make or warm 3 to 4 naan; use Fluffy Skillet Naan for the house version.
  6. Cook potatoes: cook Bowl 7 until crisp and hold warm.
  7. Prep fill: slice Bowl 8 fresh items and drain 1/2 cup pickles or pickled onions.
  8. Cook chicken: cook whole marinated thighs until browned and cooked through, about 4 to 6 minutes per side. Rest 5 to 10 minutes, then slice thin.
  9. Re-crisp: return sliced chicken and juices to a hot pan for 1 to 2 minutes.
  10. Build each wrap: use 1 warm naan, about 1 Tbsp garlic sauce, 1/3 to 1/4 of the chicken, about 1/4 cup potatoes, 1 to 2 Tbsp pickles, fresh fill, 1 to 2 tsp garlic sauce, and 1 to 2 tsp lemon tahini.
  11. Set and serve: fold or roll; griddle seam-side down for 30 to 60 seconds if desired.

Do Not Forget

Marinate Saturday evening for Sunday. Keep lemon juice to 1 Tbsp. Fluffy Skillet Naan is the required house bread component unless using prepared naan for the first test. Garlic sauce is primary; tahini is a thin stripe. Drain pickles and wet toppings.

Final Taste Target

Juicy, deeply spiced, garlicky, lemon-bright, tangy from pickles, creamy from sauce, nutty from a thin tahini stripe, and fresh from vegetables and herbs. Not soggy, tahini-heavy, or yogurt-dominant.
